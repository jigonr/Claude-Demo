## About Costco

Costco Wholesale is a membership-based warehouse club operating hundreds of locations worldwide, serving millions of members annually.

## About the Role

We're seeking a Payroll Coordinator to manage payroll processing for our Portland distribution center and regional headquarters. You will process pay cycles, manage employee data, and ensure regulatory compliance. This role requires meticulous attention to accuracy and deadlines.

## Responsibilities

- Process bi-weekly payroll for hourly and salaried employees
- Verify timekeeping data and resolve discrepancies
- Manage employee master data and maintain data accuracy
- Prepare and distribute pay statements and tax documents
- Ensure compliance with federal, state, and local tax regulations
- Support managers with payroll questions and documentation

## Requirements

**Education**
Bachelor's degree in Accounting, Finance, or Business Administration preferred; high school diploma minimum

**Experience**
1-2 years of payroll processing, HR operations, or accounting experience

**Skills**
- Payroll processing systems and HRIS platforms
- Attention to detail and accuracy
- Knowledge of payroll taxes and regulations
- Data entry and spreadsheet skills
- Communication and employee service orientation

**Certifications**
- None required; CPP certification preferred
