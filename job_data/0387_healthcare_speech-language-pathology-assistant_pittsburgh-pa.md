# Speech-Language Pathology Assistant

**Company:** Roche | **Category:** Healthcare | **Location:** Pittsburgh, PA (Remote) | **Salary:** $45,000-$58,000/year | **Type:** Full-time | **Posted:** Mar 02, 2026

## About Roche

Roche is a global pharmaceutical company dedicated to advancing personalized healthcare and treating serious diseases. We're committed to innovation and patient-centric solutions.

## About the Role

You'll help patients improve communication and swallowing abilities through therapeutic support. As a Speech-Language Pathology Assistant, you'll assist licensed SLPs with therapy delivery and patient care. This remote Pittsburgh role offers meaningful healthcare work with flexibility.

## Responsibilities

- Assist speech-language pathologists with patient assessment and treatment
- Implement therapeutic exercises under SLP supervision
- Maintain accurate treatment records and patient documentation
- Prepare therapy materials and therapeutic equipment
- Support patient motivation and participation in therapy

## Requirements

**Education:** High school diploma or equivalent; SLP assistant certification preferred

**Experience:** 0-2 years

**Certifications:** SLPA certification (or eligible)

**Skills:**
- Patient care and communication
- Speech pathology terminology
- Ability to follow clinical protocols
- Organizational and documentation skills
- Compassion and patience
