## About the Role

As a Training Coordinator working fully remote from Detroit, you'll design and deliver learning programs that help Travelers employees grow and succeed. This role offers flexibility and meaningful work in supporting workforce development at a Fortune 500 company.

- Manage learning management systems and training records
- Support compliance and regulatory training requirements
- Communicate training needs and deadlines to managers
