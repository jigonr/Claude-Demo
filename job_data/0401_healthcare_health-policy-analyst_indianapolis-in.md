## About Sanofi

Sanofi is a global healthcare company researching, developing, and distributing pharmaceutical solutions in multiple therapeutic areas including vaccines, oncology, and specialty care.

## About the Role

We're seeking a Health Policy Analyst to monitor healthcare policy developments, analyze regulatory impacts on our pharmaceuticals business, and provide strategic recommendations. You will track legislation, engage with policymakers, and ensure compliance with regulations.

## Responsibilities

- Monitor federal and state healthcare legislation and regulatory developments
- Analyze impact of policy changes on pharmaceutical pricing, coverage, and market access
- Prepare policy briefs and impact assessments for senior leadership
- Engage with policy organizations and advocacy groups
- Track regulatory requirements and ensure business compliance
- Support development of company policy positions and testimony

## Requirements

**Education**
Bachelor's degree in Health Policy, Public Health, Political Science, or related field

**Experience**
1-3 years of health policy, regulatory affairs, or government relations experience

**Skills**
- Healthcare policy knowledge and legislative analysis
- Regulatory affairs and compliance understanding
- Research and writing skills
- Stakeholder engagement and communication
- Data analysis and policy evaluation

**Certifications**
- None required; MPH or policy certification preferred
