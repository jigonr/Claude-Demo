**HashiCorp** | Tech | Cincinnati, OH (Remote) | $52,000–$68,000 USD/year | Full-time | Posted Feb 18, 2026

## About HashiCorp

HashiCorp is the leader in Infrastructure Automation. We build open-source and commercial products that allow organizations to provision, secure, and run any infrastructure for any application. Our mission is to democratize infrastructure automation for everyone.

## About the Role

We're looking for a Cloud Operations Analyst to help manage and optimize infrastructure at scale. You'll work with the tools HashiCorp is famous for—Terraform, Vault, and more—while supporting teams that rely on our platforms. This fully remote role gives you the freedom to work from anywhere while making a real impact on how enterprises manage cloud infrastructure.

## Responsibilities

- Monitor cloud infrastructure health, performance metrics, and resource utilization across AWS, Azure, and GCP
- Troubleshoot and resolve operational incidents affecting cloud deployments and services
- Optimize cloud resource configurations for cost efficiency and performance
- Collaborate with infrastructure and platform engineering teams on automation improvements
- Document operational procedures and maintain runbooks for common issues
- Participate in on-call rotation and support escalations during production incidents
