## About the Role

This is a direct-hire Commercial Appraiser position with a reputable property valuation firm in the Orlando area. You'll work remotely while building a career in commercial real estate appraisal. You'll be responsible for assessing properties, conducting market analysis, and preparing professional valuations that support real estate transactions and investment decisions.

## Responsibilities

- Conduct commercial property appraisals and prepare comprehensive valuation reports
- Perform market analysis and research comparable properties
- Maintain detailed property records and documentation
- Support transaction timelines and client communication
- Ensure compliance with appraisal standards and regulations

**Experience:** 2-4 years of commercial property appraisal experience

**Skills:**

- Knowledge of USPAP and valuation methodologies
- Proficiency with appraisal software and valuation databases
- Strong research and analytical abilities
- Professional communication and client management

**Certifications:** State-certified commercial appraiser license required
