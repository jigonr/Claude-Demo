# Phlebotomy Technician

**Stryker** | Healthcare | Atlanta, GA | $30,000–$39,000/year | Full-Time | Posted Jan 25, 2026

## About Stryker

Stryker is a global leader in medical devices serving orthopedic, surgical, neurotechnology, and spine markets. We improve patient outcomes through innovative medical technology and are committed to supporting healthcare professionals.

## About the Role

As a Phlebotomy Technician at Stryker's Atlanta operations, you'll collect blood samples and provide essential support to clinical teams. Working onsite, you'll ensure safe, accurate specimen collection and maintain high standards of patient care. This entry-level healthcare role offers clear pathways for career advancement in clinical settings.

## Responsibilities

- Draw blood samples from patients using proper phlebotomy techniques
- Label and process blood samples according to protocols
- Maintain venipuncture equipment and supplies
- Ensure patient comfort and safety during procedures
- Maintain confidentiality and handle sensitive patient information
- Document procedures and maintain accurate records

## Requirements

**Experience**

**Skills**
- Phlebotomy techniques and safe blood handling
- Patient communication and bedside manner
- Attention to detail and accuracy
- Physical stamina and manual dexterity
- Understanding of HIPAA and patient confidentiality
- Ability to work efficiently under pressure

**Certifications**
- Phlebotomy certification (may be obtained after hire)
