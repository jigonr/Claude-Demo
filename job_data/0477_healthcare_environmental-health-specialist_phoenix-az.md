## About Abbott Laboratories

Abbott is a global healthcare company providing diverse products and services, from diagnostics to medical devices. We're committed to advancing patient care and public health.

## About the Role

You'll ensure healthcare environments meet safety and health standards. As an Environmental Health Specialist, you'll assess risks, develop safety programs, and ensure compliance. This hybrid Phoenix role contributes to the safe environments where patient care happens.

## Responsibilities

- Assess environmental health risks in healthcare facilities
- Develop and implement environmental safety programs
- Monitor compliance with environmental regulations
- Conduct training on environmental safety practices
- Prepare documentation and reports on findings

## Requirements

**Education:** Bachelor's degree in Environmental Health, Public Health, or related field

**Experience:** 0-2 years

**Certifications:** REHS or DAPH certification (preferred)

**Skills:**
- Environmental health assessment techniques
- Regulatory compliance and standards
- Risk identification and mitigation
- Communication and training abilities
- Attention to detail and safety focus
