## About State Farm

State Farm is the nation's leading auto insurer, also providing home, health, and financial services to millions of customers. With a proud history dating back to 1922, we're committed to being a trusted partner in our communities while maintaining a stable, customer-focused culture.

## About the Role

The Communications Coordinator role offers the chance to grow your career in corporate communications within a Fortune 500 company. You'll manage diverse projects—from employee newsletters to regional community engagement—while working in a supportive team environment. This hybrid position provides flexibility while maintaining strong in-person collaboration with colleagues in St. Louis.

## Responsibilities

- Develop and coordinate internal communications campaigns, including newsletters, announcements, and employee engagement content
- Manage editorial calendars and ensure brand consistency across multiple platforms and channels
- Collaborate with regional teams to gather content, ensure timely distribution, and measure engagement metrics
- Support external communications for regional initiatives, including community involvement and stakeholder relations
- Track and report on communications effectiveness, making recommendations for optimization
