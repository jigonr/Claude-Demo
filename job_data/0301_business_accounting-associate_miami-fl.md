## About Coca-Cola

The Coca-Cola Company is the world's largest beverage company, with operations in 200+ countries. Our Miami hub serves as a strategic regional center for distribution, logistics, and financial operations across the Southeast.

## About the Role

We're seeking an Accounting Associate for our Miami distribution and logistics hub. You will support accounts payable processing, manage supplier invoices, reconcile expense reports, and assist with month-end close activities. This role is essential to maintaining the financial accuracy and operational rhythm of one of our largest regional operations.

## Responsibilities

- Process supplier invoices and match to purchase orders and receiving documents
- Reconcile accounts payable subledger and investigate aging exceptions
- Review and approve employee expense reports for policy compliance
- Prepare monthly journal entries for distribution center operations and logistics costs
- Assist in bank reconciliation and identify discrepancies with finance team oversight
- Support external audit preparation by organizing supporting documentation
