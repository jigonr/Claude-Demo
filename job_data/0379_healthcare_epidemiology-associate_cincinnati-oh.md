# Epidemiology Associate

**Company:** Mass General Brigham | **Category:** Healthcare | **Location:** Cincinnati, OH | **Salary:** $44,000–$57,000 | **Type:** Full-Time | **Posted:** 2026-03-06

## About Mass General Brigham

Mass General Brigham is a premier healthcare organization comprising Massachusetts General Hospital, Brigham and Women's Hospital, and numerous affiliated institutions. We lead in clinical care, research, and innovation in healthcare.

## About the Role

As an Epidemiology Associate at Mass General Brigham, you'll contribute to our population health research and clinical epidemiology efforts. Working remotely, you'll analyze health data, support research studies, and help generate evidence that informs patient care and public health decision-making. This position offers meaningful work in disease prevention and health outcomes research.

## Responsibilities

- Perform data collection, entry, and cleaning for epidemiological studies
- Conduct literature reviews and assist with research protocol development
- Analyze health data using statistical software; support publication preparation
- Maintain confidentiality of patient data; ensure compliance with HIPAA
- Support grant applications and research documentation

## Requirements

**Education:** Bachelor's degree in Epidemiology, Public Health, or related life sciences field

**Experience:** 1-2 years of research experience in epidemiology or public health

**Skills:**
- Familiarity with epidemiological research methods
- Proficiency with statistical software (R, Python, SAS, or STATA)
- Strong data management and organizational skills
- Scientific writing and communication abilities
- Understanding of data privacy and healthcare regulations

**Certifications:** None required
