## About Broad Institute
The Broad Institute advances biomedical research through genomic science. Our research has pioneered breakthroughs in cancer, infectious disease, and stem cell biology.

## About the Role
We're seeking a EKG Technician to join our Research team in Palo Alto. This role offers the opportunity to contribute meaningfully to our mission while developing your professional skills in a collaborative environment. You'll work with talented colleagues dedicated to excellence and innovation in our field.

## Responsibilities
- Perform electrocardiogram tests and operate EKG equipment
- Prepare patients for EKG testing and explain procedures
- Ensure accurate electrode placement and signal quality
- Maintain equipment and perform routine calibration
- Document test results and communicate findings to physicians
- Maintain patient privacy and comply with infection control standards

## Requirements
- **Education:** High school diploma or GED; EKG technician certification preferred
- **Experience:** 0-2 years in clinical testing or EKG operations
- **Skills:** EKG equipment operation, Patient communication, Attention to detail, Infection control, Medical terminology
- **Certifications:** Certified EKG Technician (CET) or equivalent
