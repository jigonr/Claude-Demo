## About Rippling

Rippling is the global workforce platform that empowers companies to manage every aspect of the employee experience. From HR to IT to finance, Rippling automates the workflows that connect people, technology, and money.

## About the Role

As an Infrastructure Engineer I at our Research Triangle Park facility, you'll design and implement the cloud infrastructure powering Rippling's platform. Work on-site with a team focused on reliability, scalability, and operational excellence at the scale our customers depend on.

- Implement monitoring, logging, and alerting systems
- Optimize cloud costs and resource utilization
- Collaborate with engineering teams on system reliability improvements
- Participate in oncall rotation for production infrastructure support
