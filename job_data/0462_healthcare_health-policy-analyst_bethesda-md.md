## About Dana-Farber Cancer Institute
Dana-Farber is a world-leading cancer research and treatment center affiliated with Harvard Medical School.

## About the Role
You'll analyze health policy issues impacting cancer care and research. You will develop expertise in critical areas while working alongside experienced professionals who are committed to your growth.

## Responsibilities
- Analyze healthcare policy impact on organizational operations and research
- Monitor legislative changes in oncology and cancer care regulation
- Develop policy position statements and advocacy materials
- Present policy analyses to leadership and board committees
- Build relationships with policy stakeholders and advocacy organizations
- Support grant applications and policy-focused research initiatives

## Requirements

- **Education:** Master's degree in Public Health, Policy, Health Administration, or related field
- **Experience:** 3-5 years of health policy research or analysis experience
- **Skills:** Policy analysis, Health systems knowledge, Quantitative analysis, Writing, Stakeholder engagement
- **Certifications:** Health policy expertise, Grant writing
