# Clinical Laboratory Technician

**CDC** | Healthcare | Minneapolis, MN | $45,000 - $58,000 yearly | Full-Time | Posted 2026-01-20

## About CDC

U.S. federal agency protecting public health through disease prevention and science. We're building a culture that is public service, scientific rigor, emergency-ready, where talented professionals can do their best work and grow their careers.

## About the Role

We're seeking a Clinical Laboratory Technician to join our team. This role offers the opportunity to make a meaningful impact while working with a talented cross-functional team. You'll be instrumental in driving government/public health initiatives forward and contributing to our strategic goals.

## Responsibilities

- Perform clinical laboratory tests on patient specimens following established protocols
- Operate and maintain laboratory instrumentation and quality control systems
- Collect and process patient specimens ensuring proper handling and documentation
- Review results and escalate critical values to clinical staff immediately
- Maintain accurate and timely laboratory information system records
- Participate in quality assurance programs and continuing education

## Requirements

**Education:** Associate's degree in Clinical Laboratory Science

**Experience:** 0-2 years of relevant experience

**Skills:**
- Laboratory instrumentation
- Phlebotomy
- Microbiology
- Chemistry
- Quality control

**Certifications:**
- Medical Lab Technician (MLT) or Medical Technologist (MT) ASCP
