## About Intuitive Surgical

Intuitive Surgical pioneered robotic-assisted minimally invasive surgical technology. Our innovation extends beyond the operating room to research, development, and clinical evaluation of next-generation surgical solutions.

## About the Role

We're seeking a Radiology Technician to support our medical device research and development operations in Des Moines. You'll operate diagnostic imaging equipment, assist with device testing and validation, and ensure compliance with radiation safety standards. This role combines clinical expertise with technical innovation.

## Responsibilities

- Operate X-ray, CT, and ultrasound equipment for device testing and clinical evaluation
- Prepare imaging protocols and assist with imaging procedure optimization
- Maintain and calibrate diagnostic imaging equipment according to manufacturer specifications
- Document imaging results and maintain equipment logs for regulatory compliance
- Support clinical studies by positioning devices and capturing high-quality diagnostic images
- Ensure proper radiation safety protocols and maintain ALARA standards

## Requirements

**Education**
Associate's degree in Radiologic Technology from an accredited program

**Experience**
1-3 years of radiology technician or medical imaging experience

**Skills**
- Diagnostic imaging equipment operation and troubleshooting
- Patient safety and positioning techniques
- Radiation safety and ALARA principles
- Medical device knowledge and technical documentation
- Data management and imaging system software

**Certifications**
- ARRT (American Registry of Radiologic Technologists) certification required
- State radiology license or ability to obtain
