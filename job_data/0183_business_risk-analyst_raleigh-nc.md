# Risk Analyst

**Company:** EY | **Category:** Business | **Location:** Raleigh, NC | **Salary:** $58,000–$76,000 | **Type:** Full-Time | **Posted:** 2026-03-24

## About EY

EY is a leading global professional services organization providing audit, consulting, tax, and transaction advisory services. Our Risk & Compliance Solutions practice helps organizations manage risk and drive sustainable growth.

## About the Role

As a Risk Analyst at EY Raleigh, you'll contribute to engagements that strengthen client governance, manage enterprise risk, and ensure regulatory compliance. Working remotely, you'll support projects across multiple industries, gaining exposure to diverse risk management frameworks and best practices. This is an excellent foundation for a career in risk management or internal audit.

## Responsibilities

- Conduct risk assessments and identify control gaps across client processes and systems
- Document business processes and develop risk matrices aligned with industry standards
- Perform compliance reviews against regulatory requirements and internal policies
- Develop risk mitigation recommendations and implementation roadmaps
- Prepare client reports, presentations, and governance documentation

## Requirements

**Education:** Bachelor's degree in Business, Finance, Accounting, or related discipline

**Experience:** 1-3 years in risk management, compliance, or internal audit roles

**Skills:**
- Knowledge of enterprise risk management frameworks (COSO, ISO 31000)
- Proficiency with Excel and data analysis
- Strong writing and presentation abilities
- Ability to synthesize complex information for diverse audiences
- Understanding of internal audit and compliance processes

**Certifications:** CIA, CRMA, or similar risk/audit certification preferred
