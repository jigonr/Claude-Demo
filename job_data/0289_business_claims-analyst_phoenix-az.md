## About the Role
We're seeking a Claims Analyst to join our Industrial team in Phoenix. ## Responsibilities
- Investigate and process insurance claims for accuracy and policy compliance
- Review claim documentation and verify coverage eligibility
- Communicate with claimants, healthcare providers, and third parties to resolve discrepancies
- Document findings and maintain detailed claim records in management systems
- Identify patterns in claims data and report fraud indicators
- Meet processing timelines while maintaining high accuracy standards

- **Experience:** 2-4 years in claims processing, insurance, or risk management
- **Skills:** Claims management systems, Attention to detail, Communication, Problem-solving, Data entry proficiency
- **Certifications:** ARM (Associate in Risk Management) preferred
