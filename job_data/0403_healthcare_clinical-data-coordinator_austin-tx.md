# Clinical Data Coordinator

**Company:** GSK
**Category:** Healthcare
**Location:** Austin, TX, US (Remote)
**Salary:** $50,000 - $65,000/year
**Employment Type:** Full-time
**Posted:** 2026-03-21

## About GSK
GSK is a science-led global healthcare company with a mission to discover, develop, and deliver health solutions.

## About the Role
You'll manage clinical trial data ensuring regulatory compliance and data integrity. You will develop expertise in critical areas while working alongside experienced professionals who are committed to your growth.

## Responsibilities
- Manage and validate data from clinical trials
- Prepare case report forms and source documentation
- Resolve data discrepancies and ensure regulatory compliance
- Support database development and quality assurance
- Prepare regulatory submissions and audit responses
- Track protocol deviations and manage data changes

## Requirements

- **Education:** Bachelor's degree in healthcare-related field or equivalent experience
- **Experience:** 2-4 years of clinical data management or clinical research experience
- **Skills:** Data management, Case report forms, Clinical trial knowledge, Regulatory compliance, Database systems
- **Certifications:** GCP (Good Clinical Practice) certification, ACRP certification
