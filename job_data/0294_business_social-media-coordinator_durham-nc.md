# Social Media Coordinator

**Company:** Lockheed Martin | **Category:** Business | **Location:** Durham, NC | **Salary:** $39,000–$52,000 | **Type:** Full-Time | **Posted:** 2026-01-20

## About Lockheed Martin

Lockheed Martin is a global security and aerospace company that employs thousands of people and operates in more than 100 countries.

## About the Role

As a Social Media Coordinator at Lockheed Martin, you'll be responsible for developing and executing our social media strategy across multiple platforms. Working remotely, you'll create compelling content, engage with our professional audiences, and help build our brand presence in the aerospace and defense industry.

## Responsibilities

- Create and schedule social media content across LinkedIn, Twitter, Facebook, and Instagram
- Monitor social media channels; respond to inquiries and engage with audiences
- Analyze social media metrics and performance; prepare reports for management
- Collaborate with marketing and communications teams on campaign strategy
- Support brand reputation management and crisis communication protocols

## Requirements

**Education:** Bachelor's degree in Marketing, Communications, or related field

**Experience:** 1-2 years of social media management or marketing experience

**Skills:**
- Proficiency with social media platforms and management tools
- Strong written communication and storytelling abilities
- Content creation and basic graphic design skills
- Analytical abilities and data interpretation
- Understanding of social media trends and best practices

**Certifications:** None required
