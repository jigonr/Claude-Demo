## About Mount Sinai Health

Mount Sinai Health System is one of New York's oldest and most respected academic medical centers, combining research innovation with patient-centered care. We're committed to advancing medicine while serving our diverse New York communities.

## About the Role

As a Physical Therapy Aide at Mount Sinai Health, you'll support patients in their physical recovery and rehabilitation journey. Working in our New York facility with hybrid flexibility, you'll assist therapists in delivering compassionate care while gaining valuable healthcare experience. This role offers clear pathways to advancement in the healthcare field.

## Responsibilities

- Assist physical therapists with patient care, helping patients perform exercises and mobility activities safely
- Prepare therapy rooms and equipment; maintain cleanliness and organization of treatment spaces
- Support patient comfort and safety; communicate patient concerns to supervising therapists
- Maintain accurate records of patient sessions and treatment progress
- Assist with equipment maintenance and supply inventory management
