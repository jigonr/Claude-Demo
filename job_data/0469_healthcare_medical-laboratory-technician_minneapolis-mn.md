# Medical Laboratory Technician

**Company:** Bristol-Myers Squibb | **Category:** Healthcare | **Location:** Minneapolis, MN | **Salary:** $42,000–$55,000 | **Type:** Full-Time | **Posted:** 2026-03-21

## About Bristol-Myers Squibb

Bristol-Myers Squibb is a global biopharmaceutical company focused on discovering, developing, and delivering innovative medicines for patients. We have a commitment to addressing serious diseases and improving patient outcomes.

## About the Role

As a Medical Laboratory Technician at Bristol-Myers Squibb Minneapolis, you'll perform critical laboratory analyses that support our pharmaceutical research and development. Working in a hybrid arrangement, you'll be part of the team that develops the medicines of tomorrow.

## Responsibilities

- Perform laboratory tests and analyses using standard procedures and protocols
- Collect and prepare patient or research samples for testing
- Operate laboratory equipment and instruments; perform routine maintenance
- Record and verify test results; maintain accurate laboratory records
- Quality assurance testing and adherence to laboratory standards

## Requirements

**Education:** Associate's degree in Clinical Laboratory Science or High School diploma with certification

**Experience:** 1-3 years of medical laboratory or clinical laboratory experience

**Skills:**
- Proficiency with laboratory equipment and instrumentation
- Knowledge of laboratory safety and quality standards
- Strong attention to detail and accuracy
- Basic understanding of medical terminology
- Ability to follow laboratory protocols and procedures

**Certifications:** Clinical Laboratory Technician (CLT) or Medical Laboratory Technician (MLT) certification

- Paid time off and paid holidays
- Professional development and certification support
