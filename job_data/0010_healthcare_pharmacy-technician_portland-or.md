## About Kaiser Permanente

Kaiser Permanente is one of America's leading integrated healthcare organizations, serving over 12 million members through a model that connects health plans, hospitals, and physician groups under one coordinated system. Guided by the ethos of "thrive together," Kaiser Permanente is committed to preventive care, health equity, and delivering affordable, high-quality services that help members and communities live their healthiest lives.

## About the Role

As a Pharmacy Technician at Kaiser Permanente in Portland, you will be a vital part of our member-centered pharmacy team, supporting the safe and efficient delivery of medications within an integrated care environment. Working closely with pharmacists, prescribers, and fellow healthcare professionals, you will handle prescription processing, inventory management, and direct member interactions. This onsite role is an excellent opportunity to build a pharmacy career within a team-based system that values preventive health and compassionate service.

## Responsibilities

- Receive, process, and fill prescription orders accurately using Kaiser Permanente's pharmacy dispensing systems, verifying patient information, dosage, and drug interactions under pharmacist supervision
- Manage medication inventory including ordering, receiving, stocking, and rotating pharmaceutical supplies while maintaining proper storage conditions and minimizing waste
- Verify insurance eligibility and process prescription claims, resolving rejected claims and communicating coverage options to members with clarity and compassion
- Assist members at the pharmacy counter and via phone, answering questions about prescription status, refill procedures, and general pharmacy services in accordance with HIPAA compliance requirements
- Support pharmacists in maintaining accurate records, performing quality assurance checks, and ensuring compliance with all federal, state, and Kaiser Permanente pharmacy regulations

## Requirements

- **Education:** High school diploma or GED required; completion of a pharmacy technician training program preferred
- **Experience:** 0-2 years of pharmacy or healthcare experience; retail or hospital pharmacy experience is a plus
- **Skills:** Prescription processing, pharmacy dispensing systems, medication inventory management, insurance verification, patient communication, and HIPAA compliance
- **Certifications:** Oregon Pharmacy Technician license required; PTCB (Pharmacy Technician Certification Board) certification preferred
- **Additional:** Strong interpersonal skills, ability to work efficiently in a high-volume pharmacy setting, and a commitment to Kaiser Permanente's values of member-centered service and team collaboration
