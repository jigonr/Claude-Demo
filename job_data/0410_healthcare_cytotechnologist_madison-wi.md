# Cytotechnologist

**Company:** Broad Institute | **Category:** Healthcare | **Location:** Madison, WI | **Salary:** $49,000–$63,000 | **Type:** Full-Time | **Posted:** 2026-02-20

## About Broad Institute

The Broad Institute is a world-renowned biomedical research organization dedicated to expanding our understanding of biology and disease. Through collaborative research, we work to develop novel approaches for disease prevention and treatment.

## About the Role

As a Cytotechnologist at the Broad Institute, you'll provide essential analytical support to genomic and cellular research programs. You'll work with advanced microscopy equipment and collaborate with PhD-level scientists to advance cutting-edge biomedical research. This remote position offers meaningful work in disease research and discovery science.

## Responsibilities

- Prepare, stain, and examine cell and tissue specimens using microscopic techniques
- Perform quality assurance on laboratory processes and cell preparations
- Maintain detailed records of specimen data and analysis results
- Collaborate with research scientists to understand analytical requirements
- Prepare reports and documentation supporting research findings

## Requirements

**Education:** Associate's degree in Cytotechnology or Bachelor's degree in life sciences field

**Experience:** 2-4 years of cytotechnology or related laboratory experience

**Skills:**
- Proficiency with microscopy and cell preparation techniques
- Laboratory information systems experience
- Strong attention to detail and accuracy
- Understanding of cell biology and staining procedures
- Ability to follow strict laboratory protocols

**Certifications:** ASCP Certification in Cytotechnology (CT) preferred
