## About Broad Institute
The Broad Institute advances biomedical research through genomic science. Our research has pioneered breakthroughs in cancer, infectious disease, and stem cell biology.

## About the Role
We're seeking a Medical Assistant to join our Research team in Boulder. This role offers the opportunity to contribute meaningfully to our mission while developing your professional skills in a collaborative environment. You'll work with talented colleagues dedicated to excellence and innovation in our field.

## Responsibilities
- Assist physicians and clinical staff with patient examinations and medical procedures
- Maintain accurate and confidential patient medical records and documentation
- Perform routine vital signs monitoring and basic patient care tasks
- Prepare examination rooms and sterilize medical instruments per protocol
- Support clinical research activities and coordinate patient communications
- Ensure compliance with HIPAA regulations and infection control standards

## Requirements
- **Education:** High school diploma or GED; medical assistant certification preferred
- **Experience:** 0-2 years of clinical or healthcare support experience
- **Skills:** Patient communication, Medical terminology, Clinical procedures, EHR systems
- **Certifications:** BLS/CPR, Medical Assistant Certification (AAMA or CAAHEP)
