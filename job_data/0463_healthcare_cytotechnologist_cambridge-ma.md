# Cytotechnologist

**Genentech** | Biotechnology | Cambridge, MA | $71,000–$91,000/year | Full-time | Posted Mar 16, 2026

## About Genentech

Genentech is a member of the Roche Group, pioneering biotechnology for oncology, immunology, and other diseases. Based in the heart of biotech country, we combine rigorous science with cutting-edge research to develop transformative medicines.

## About the Role

As a Cytotechnologist at Genentech, you'll work at the intersection of laboratory science and drug discovery. Your meticulous examination of cellular specimens supports our research teams in understanding disease mechanisms and evaluating therapeutic compounds. This hybrid role offers technical expertise combined with exposure to groundbreaking biotech research.

## Responsibilities

- Prepare tissue and cellular specimens according to standardized protocols, using staining, fixation, and processing techniques
- Examine slides under microscopes, identifying abnormal cells and morphological features with precision
- Document findings and prepare detailed reports for review by pathologists and researchers
- Maintain laboratory equipment and quality control standards; troubleshoot technical issues
- Collaborate with research teams and participate in continuous improvement of cytology methods

**Skills**

**Certifications**
Cytotechnologist certification (ASCP, preferred)
