## About Amplitude
Amplitude is the Product Intelligence platform trusted by 5000+ customers including Spotify, Instacart, and DraftKings. We help product teams understand user behavior and ship winning features.

## About the Role
You'll drive security and stability while our platform processes trillions of analytics events. You will develop expertise in critical areas while working alongside experienced professionals who are committed to your growth.

## Responsibilities
- Monitor security alerts and investigate potential security incidents
- Analyze logs and network traffic for anomalous behavior
- Document incident details and escalate critical findings
- Support incident response and recovery procedures
- Maintain security baselines and detection rule libraries
- Contribute to security training and awareness programs

## Requirements

- **Education:** Bachelor's degree in Cybersecurity, Computer Science, or related field
- **Experience:** 1-2 years of security monitoring or incident response experience
- **Skills:** SIEM tools, Log analysis, Incident response, Network protocols, Security frameworks
- **Certifications:** Security+, CompTIA A+
