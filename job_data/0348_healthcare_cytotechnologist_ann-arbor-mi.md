**Providence Health** | Healthcare | Ann Arbor, MI | $52,000–$66,000/year | Full-Time | Posted Jan 9, 2026

## About Providence Health

Providence Health is a not-for-profit healthcare system serving communities across Michigan and beyond. We're committed to providing compassionate, high-quality care grounded in Catholic values and clinical excellence.

## About the Role

As a Cytotechnologist at our Ann Arbor laboratory, you'll play a vital role in patient diagnosis and care. You'll examine cellular specimens with precision, identify abnormalities, and contribute to accurate diagnostic reporting. This onsite role offers the satisfaction of knowing your work directly impacts patient outcomes.

## Responsibilities

- Prepare and stain tissue and body fluid specimens for microscopic examination
- Screen and examine specimens to identify abnormalities and cellular changes
- Prepare quality written reports documenting findings and impressions
- Collaborate with pathologists on specimen interpretation and complex cases
- Maintain laboratory quality assurance and proficiency standards
- Assist with special procedures and immunohistochemical staining

## Requirements

**Education**
Associate degree in Cytotechnology from an accredited program

**Experience**
1–3 years in clinical cytology, histology, or pathology laboratory settings

**Skills**
- Microscopy and specimen preparation
- Cytology and cell identification
- Attention to detail and diagnostic accuracy
- Quality assurance and compliance
- Electronic medical records (EMR)
- Communication and report writing

**Certifications**
- ASCP certification required or eligible within 1 year
