## About Regeneron

Regeneron is a leading biotechnology company pioneering innovative medicines for serious diseases. Our Bethesda headquarters serves as a hub for clinical research and development operations.

## About the Role

We're seeking a Pharmacy Assistant to support our clinical trial pharmacy operations. You will manage inventory of investigational drugs, prepare and dispense study medications to trial participants, and maintain precise documentation required for FDA compliance. Working remotely, you'll coordinate with our clinical team to ensure participants receive medications on schedule while adhering to strict research protocols.

## Responsibilities

- Receive, verify, and log investigational drug shipments according to chain-of-custody protocols
- Prepare study medications in compliance with protocol specifications and dispense to trial participants
- Maintain detailed medication logs and manage pharmacy inventory database systems
- Respond to participant medication-related questions and troubleshoot adherence issues
- Monitor expiration dates and coordinate proper disposal of expired study drugs
- Generate pharmacy reports for quarterly regulatory inspections and audit documentation

## Requirements
