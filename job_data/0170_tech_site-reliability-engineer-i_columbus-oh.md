## About Twilio

Twilio enables developers to add communications (voice, SMS, video, and email) to their applications. Our platform processes billions of interactions annually for companies ranging from startups to Fortune 500 enterprises.

## About the Role

We're seeking a Site Reliability Engineer I to help us build and maintain the infrastructure that powers customer communication across voice, SMS, and video. You'll work on observability, incident response, and deployment automation for services serving billions of requests daily. This role bridges software engineering and operations—you'll write code to make systems more reliable while being on-call to support production.

## Responsibilities

- Develop monitoring and alerting strategies to detect issues before they impact customer experience
- Write infrastructure-as-code using Terraform or CloudFormation to manage AWS resources
- Participate in on-call rotation (1-week per month) and respond to production incidents
- Automate repetitive operational tasks using Python or Go scripting
- Conduct post-incident reviews to identify root causes and implement preventive measures
- Mentor junior engineers and document runbooks for common operational scenarios
