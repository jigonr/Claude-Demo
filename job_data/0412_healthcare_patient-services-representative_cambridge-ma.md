# Patient Services Representative

**Pfizer** | Healthcare | Cambridge, MA | $41,000–$54,000 USD/year | Full-time | Posted Mar 25, 2026

## About Pfizer

Pfizer is one of the world's leading biopharmaceutical companies, advancing healthcare through innovative medicines that save and improve lives. Our Cambridge facility is home to groundbreaking research and development, where we create treatments for some of the world's most challenging health conditions.

## About the Role

As a Patient Services Representative, you'll be the compassionate voice connecting patients to the medications and support they need. You'll handle inquiries, coordinate program enrollment, and ensure patients have access to Pfizer's patient assistance programs. This on-site role in our Cambridge hub puts you at the heart of our mission to serve patients.

## Responsibilities

- Answer patient and healthcare provider inquiries about Pfizer medications, side effects, and usage instructions
- Process enrollment applications for patient assistance programs and support services
- Manage patient documentation and maintain accurate records in our support systems
- Coordinate with pharmacies and clinics to facilitate medication access and delivery
- Respond to urgent patient concerns and escalate complex cases to clinical specialists
- Document all interactions and generate reports on program utilization and patient outcomes
