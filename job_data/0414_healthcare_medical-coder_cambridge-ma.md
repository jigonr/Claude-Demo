## About Moderna

Moderna is a biotechnology company pioneering mRNA therapeutics to address infectious diseases, cancer, and other serious conditions. Based in Cambridge and founded by Nobel laureates, we're advancing a new class of medicines through cutting-edge science and clinical excellence.

## About the Role

As a Medical Coder supporting Moderna's clinical trials, you'll ensure accurate documentation of critical safety and efficacy data for groundbreaking mRNA therapies. Working hybrid in Cambridge, you'll code adverse events, maintain regulatory databases, and contribute to FDA submissions that advance mRNA medicine globally.

- Collaborate with clinical operations and regulatory teams
- Support regulatory submissions and inspections
