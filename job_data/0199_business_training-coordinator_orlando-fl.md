## About Honeywell
Honeywell is a Fortune 100 industrial technology leader serving aerospace, building automation, and specialty materials globally.

## About the Role
We invest heavily in developing talent through structured training and mentorship. You will develop expertise in critical areas while working alongside experienced professionals who are committed to your growth.

## Responsibilities
- Design and deliver technical training programs for operations staff
- Manage training schedules, materials, and participant registrations
- Evaluate training effectiveness through assessments and feedback
- Maintain training records and certifications for compliance
- Identify skills gaps and develop targeted training curriculum
- Partner with department heads to align training with business needs

## Requirements

- **Education:** Bachelor's degree in Human Resources, Education, Business, or related field
- **Experience:** 2-4 years of training coordination or instructional design experience
- **Skills:** Adult learning principles, Training evaluation, Program management, Presentation skills, Learning management systems
- **Certifications:** ATD certification (preferred)
