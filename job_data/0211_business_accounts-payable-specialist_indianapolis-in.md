# Accounts Payable Specialist

**UPS** | Business | Indianapolis, IN | $35,000–$46,000 USD/year | Full-time | Posted Mar 11, 2026

## About UPS

United Parcel Service is the world's largest package delivery company and a leader in supply chain management. Our Indianapolis operations center is a vital hub for processing, sorting, and delivering packages across the Midwest.

## About the Role

As an Accounts Payable Specialist at UPS, you'll ensure vendors and suppliers are paid accurately and on time. Work on-site in our Indianapolis operations center, processing thousands of transactions and maintaining the financial accuracy that supports our logistics network.

## Responsibilities

- Process accounts payable invoices and payments for vendors and suppliers
- Verify invoice accuracy and reconcile vendor statements
- Enter payment transactions in our accounting system and maintain detailed records
- Resolve discrepancies and coordinate with vendors regarding payment issues
- Assist with month-end close activities and reconciliations
- Support audit activities and maintain compliance with financial procedures

## Requirements

**Education**
High School diploma or GED; Associate's degree in Accounting or Finance preferred

**Experience**
1–2 years in accounts payable, finance operations, or accounting roles

**Skills**
- Accounts payable processing and invoice reconciliation
- Proficiency with accounting software and SAP systems
- Strong attention to detail and organizational skills
- Excel and spreadsheet skills
- Basic understanding of accounting principles
- Ability to work independently and meet deadlines

**Certifications**
None required
