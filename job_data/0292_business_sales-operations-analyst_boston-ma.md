# Sales Operations Analyst

**Company:** Goldman Sachs | **Category:** Business | **Location:** Boston, MA | **Salary:** $65,000–$85,000 | **Type:** Full-Time | **Posted:** 2026-01-17

## About Goldman Sachs

Goldman Sachs is a leading global investment banking, securities, and investment management firm serving corporations, financial institutions, governments, and individuals worldwide.

## About the Role

As a Sales Operations Analyst at Goldman Sachs Boston, you'll be responsible for optimizing the systems and processes that drive institutional sales success. You'll work closely with sales leadership to implement technology, design analytical frameworks, and ensure data quality. This is an excellent opportunity to build operational expertise in a world-class investment banking environment.

## Responsibilities

- Design and maintain Salesforce configurations that align with sales team workflows and reporting requirements
- Build and manage sales dashboards, pipeline reports, and forecast tracking systems
- Analyze sales data to identify trends, opportunities, and operational inefficiencies
- Coordinate system implementations and conduct user training sessions
- Conduct quarterly reviews with sales leadership to assess tool adoption and process effectiveness

## Requirements

**Education:** Bachelor's degree in Business, Finance, Computer Science, or related field

**Experience:** 2-4 years of sales operations or business systems experience

**Skills:**
- Salesforce administration and advanced configuration
- SQL and Excel for data analysis and reporting
- Understanding of sales processes and metrics
- Problem-solving and process design abilities
- Strong stakeholder communication skills

**Certifications:** Salesforce Administrator or Advanced Administrator certification (required)
