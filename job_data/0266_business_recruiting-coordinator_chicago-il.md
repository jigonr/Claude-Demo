# Recruiting Coordinator

**Company:** Vanguard | **Category:** Business | **Location:** Chicago, IL | **Salary:** $47,000–$63,000 | **Type:** Full-Time | **Posted:** 2026-02-03

## About Vanguard

Vanguard is one of the world's largest investment management companies, managing over $8 trillion in global assets. We're committed to putting investors first and creating a workplace where talented people thrive.

## About the Role

As a Recruiting Coordinator at Vanguard Chicago, you'll be the operational backbone of our talent acquisition process. You'll manage logistics, ensure exceptional candidate experience, and help build high-performing teams across the organization. This remote position offers valuable human resources exposure and the chance to develop your career in talent management.

## Responsibilities

- Schedule interviews, coordinate candidate communication, and prepare interview materials
- Maintain recruiting database and pipeline; track candidate status throughout hiring process
- Prepare offer letters, onboarding documentation, and candidate correspondence
- Assist with job posting distribution and applicant tracking system management
- Support recruiting events and campus recruiting initiatives

## Requirements

**Education:** Bachelor's degree preferred, or equivalent recruiting/administrative experience

**Experience:** 1-2 years of recruiting coordination or similar administrative/HR role

**Skills:**
- Proficiency with applicant tracking systems and recruiting databases
- Strong organizational and multitasking abilities
- Excellent written and verbal communication
- Attention to detail and ability to follow processes
- Ability to interact professionally with diverse candidates

**Certifications:** None required
