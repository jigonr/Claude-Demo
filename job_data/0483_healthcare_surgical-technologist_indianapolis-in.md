# Surgical Technologist

**Roche** | Pharmaceuticals | Remote | $35,000–$46,000/year | Full-time | Posted Feb 18, 2026

## About Roche

Roche is a global pioneer in healthcare, diagnostics, and pharmaceuticals. Our research and innovation address major global health challenges, from cancer to rare diseases.

## About the Role

As a Surgical Technologist at Roche, you'll support clinical and surgical operations across our pharmaceutical development and clinical research programs. This remote role provides procedural expertise combined with exposure to cutting-edge medical research. You'll ensure operational excellence while contributing to healthcare innovation.

## Responsibilities

- Prepare surgical and procedural environments, ensuring proper sterilization and organization of instruments and equipment
- Monitor sterile fields and maintain compliance with infection control standards throughout procedures
- Assist with procedure setup and management; coordinate communication between surgical and clinical teams
- Maintain detailed records of procedures, inventory, and equipment maintenance
- Support continuous quality improvement and compliance with accreditation standards

## Requirements

**Education**
High school diploma or GED; completion of accredited surgical technology program

**Experience**
1-3 years of surgical technology experience

**Skills**
- Surgical asepsis and sterilization
- Equipment operation and maintenance
- Procedure coordination
- Documentation and compliance
- Problem-solving under pressure

**Certifications**
Certified Surgical Technologist (CST)
