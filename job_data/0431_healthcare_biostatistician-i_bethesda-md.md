## About ICON
ICON is a global clinical research organization running Phase I-IV trials across every therapeutic area.

## About the Role
As a Biostatistician, you'll analyze complex datasets from global clinical trials. You will develop expertise in critical areas while working alongside experienced professionals who are committed to your growth.

## Responsibilities
- Design statistical analysis plans for clinical trials
- Analyze clinical trial data using SAS or R
- Prepare statistical tables and figures for regulatory submissions
- Assess data quality and completeness
- Support interim analyses and safety reviews
- Contribute to statistical methods development

## Requirements

- **Education:** Master's degree in Biostatistics, Statistics, or related field
- **Experience:** 2-4 years of biostatistics or clinical statistics experience
- **Skills:** SAS/R, Clinical trial design, Statistical analysis, Regulatory knowledge, Data management
- **Certifications:** Statistical certification
