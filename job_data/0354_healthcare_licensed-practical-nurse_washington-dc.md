# Licensed Practical Nurse

**Company:** AbbVie | **Category:** Healthcare | **Location:** Washington, DC (Remote) | **Salary:** $50,000-$66,000/year | **Type:** Full-time | **Posted:** Feb 21, 2026

## About AbbVie

AbbVie is a global biopharmaceutical company committed to developing innovative medicines to address serious health conditions. We're focused on advancing patient care and improving treatment options.

## About the Role

You'll support patients in clinical settings, providing compassionate care while coordinating with broader healthcare teams. As a Licensed Practical Nurse, you'll monitor patients, maintain records, and ensure quality care delivery. This remote Washington DC role offers flexibility while making a real difference in patient outcomes.

## Responsibilities

- Monitor and record patient vital signs and health status
- Provide direct patient care and assist with daily needs
- Coordinate with nurses and physicians on patient care
- Maintain accurate patient records and documentation
- Support patient comfort and communication

## Requirements

**Education:** LPN diploma or Associate's degree in Nursing

**Experience:** 0-2 years

**Certifications:** LPN license

**Skills:**
- Patient care and communication
- Vital signs monitoring
- HIPAA compliance and confidentiality
- Basic nursing procedures
- Ability to work in collaborative environment
