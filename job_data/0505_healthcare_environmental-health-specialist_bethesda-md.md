# Environmental Health Specialist

**Company:** Fortrea

## About Fortrea
Fortrea is a global provider of integrated clinical research services with operations in 100+ countries.

## About the Role
You'll ensure environmental and occupational health compliance across our research sites. You will develop expertise in critical areas while working alongside experienced professionals who are committed to your growth.

## Responsibilities
- Conduct environmental audits and health assessments of clinical research sites
- Ensure compliance with OSHA and EPA regulations at all facilities
- Develop and implement occupational health and safety protocols
- Investigate incidents and recommend corrective actions
- Train staff on environmental health best practices
- Maintain documentation and regulatory correspondence

## Requirements

- **Education:** Bachelor's degree in Environmental Health, Public Health, or related field
- **Experience:** 2-4 years of occupational or environmental health experience
- **Skills:** OSHA regulations, Environmental auditing, Health and safety, Compliance, Documentation
- **Certifications:** CIH (Certified Industrial Hygienist) or working toward, CESY (Certified Environmental Safety & Health)
