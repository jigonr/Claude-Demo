# Speech-Language Pathology Assistant

**Company:** Abbott Laboratories | **Category:** Healthcare | **Location:** Columbus, OH | **Salary:** $42,000 - $55,000 | **Type:** Full-Time | **Posted:** 2026-02-02

## About Abbott Laboratories

Abbott Laboratories is a diversified healthcare company discovering and delivering products that save lives.

## About the Role

Support our speech-language pathologists at Abbott Laboratories in delivering therapy and rehabilitation to patients with communication and swallowing disorders.

## Responsibilities

- Assist with patient assessments and therapy sessions under SLP supervision
- Implement therapeutic activities and exercises with patients
- Maintain therapy materials and maintain organized treatment spaces
- Document patient progress and communicate updates to therapists
- Provide emotional support and encouragement to patients
- Help with scheduling and administrative support

## Requirements

**Education:** High school diploma or equivalent; relevant degree or certification strongly preferred

**Experience:** 0-3 years

**Skills:**
- Patient care
- Communication disorders knowledge
- Empathy
- Organization
- HIPAA compliance
