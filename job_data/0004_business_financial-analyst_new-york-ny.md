# Financial Analyst

## About JPMorgan Chase

JPMorgan Chase is a global leader in financial services, offering solutions in investment banking, asset management, and treasury services to institutions, governments, and individuals worldwide. The firm's commitment to excellence and innovation has defined the industry for over two centuries.

## About the Role

As a Financial Analyst, you will join one of the firm's strategic business units to support critical decision-making through rigorous financial analysis, modeling, and reporting. Working alongside top-tier professionals, you will contribute to high-impact strategic initiatives that shape the direction of the firm's operations and client engagements. This role is a cornerstone of the firm's renowned analyst program, designed to accelerate your career trajectory in finance.

## Responsibilities

- Develop and maintain complex financial models to support valuation, forecasting, and scenario analysis for strategic initiatives across the firm
- Prepare detailed presentations and memoranda for senior leadership, synthesizing quantitative findings into clear, actionable recommendations
- Conduct in-depth industry research and competitive analysis using Bloomberg Terminal and proprietary databases to inform investment decisions
- Collaborate with cross-functional teams including risk management, compliance, and front-office professionals to ensure alignment on financial deliverables
- Support quarterly and annual reporting cycles by aggregating data, performing variance analysis, and identifying key performance drivers

## Requirements

- **Education:** Bachelor's degree in Finance, Economics, Accounting, or a related quantitative discipline from an accredited institution
- **Experience:** 0-2 years of relevant experience in financial analysis, investment banking, or corporate finance
- **Skills:** Excel (advanced), PowerPoint, Bloomberg Terminal, financial modeling, SQL, VBA
- **Certifications:** None required; CFA progress or Series 7/63 licensure is viewed favorably
