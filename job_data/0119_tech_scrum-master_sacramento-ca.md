**Akamai** | Tech | Sacramento, CA | $71,000–$89,000/year | Full-time | Posted Mar 6, 2026

## About Akamai

Akamai is the global leader in content delivery and edge computing. We power the delivery of the world's largest websites and digital experiences, handling billions of requests per day.

## About the Role

As a Scrum Master at Akamai Sacramento, you'll be the agile champion for engineering teams delivering critical infrastructure. You'll facilitate team processes, remove blockers, and coach teams toward excellence. This hybrid role offers both structure and autonomy—work from our Sacramento office or home as needed while supporting teams across our organization.

## Responsibilities

- Facilitate sprint planning, daily standups, retrospectives, and other agile ceremonies across assigned teams
- Identify and help resolve impediments that slow team productivity; escalate blocked work quickly
- Coach teams on agile practices and help them refine processes to improve efficiency
- Work with product owners and stakeholders to understand priorities and manage dependencies
- Track sprint metrics and health indicators; recommend process improvements based on data
