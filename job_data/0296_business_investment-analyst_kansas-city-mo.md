# Investment Analyst

**Company:** Unilever | **Category:** Business | **Location:** Kansas City, MO (Remote) | **Salary:** $59,000-$76,000/year | **Type:** Full-time | **Posted:** Feb 14, 2026

## About Unilever

Unilever is a global fast-moving consumer goods leader with over 400 brands touching billions of lives every day—from Dove and Lipton to Ben & Jerry's and TRESemmé. We're committed to sustainable living and creating shared value through responsible business practices.

## About the Role

Join our corporate development team in evaluating strategic investments and acquisition opportunities. You'll analyze potential targets, build financial models, and contribute to decisions that shape our portfolio. This remote role offers exposure to high-stakes capital allocation decisions while working with experienced M&A professionals.

## Responsibilities

- Analyze potential acquisition targets and market entry opportunities
- Develop financial models and valuation analyses for investment decisions
- Conduct due diligence on target companies including financial, operational, and strategic fit assessments
- Prepare investment memos and recommendations for senior management
- Monitor portfolio companies post-acquisition and track investment performance

## Requirements

**Education:** Bachelor's degree in Finance, Business, or related field

**Experience:** 1-2 years

**Skills:**
- Financial modeling (DCF, comparable companies, precedent transactions)
- Advanced Excel and financial analysis
- Valuation methodologies
- Business research and market analysis
- Professional presentation skills
