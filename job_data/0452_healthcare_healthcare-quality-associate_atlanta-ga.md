## About Eli Lilly

Eli Lilly is a global pharmaceutical company discovering and developing medicines that address serious illnesses. We're committed to improving lives through innovative treatments.

## About the Role

As a Healthcare Quality Associate at Eli Lilly Atlanta, you'll be the guardian of quality in our pharmaceutical operations. Working hybrid, you'll monitor quality metrics, conduct audits, and drive continuous improvement. This role requires attention to detail and a commitment to excellence that impacts patient safety.

## Responsibilities

- Monitor healthcare quality metrics and performance indicators; track compliance with regulatory standards
- Conduct internal audits and inspections to identify areas for improvement
- Document findings and recommend corrective actions; track implementation and effectiveness
- Support regulatory compliance and documentation requirements; prepare audit responses
- Collaborate with operations and quality teams to identify and prevent quality issues
