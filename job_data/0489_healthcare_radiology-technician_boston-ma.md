# Radiology Technician

**Company:** BioNTech | **Category:** Healthcare | **Location:** Boston, MA (Remote) | **Salary:** $60,000-$77,000/year | **Type:** Full-time | **Posted:** Jan 15, 2026

## About BioNTech

BioNTech is a global biotechnology company pioneering mRNA technology to create innovative medicines for cancer, infectious diseases, and rare diseases. Our headquarters in Boston leads R&D initiatives advancing personalized medicine and breakthrough immunotherapies that transform patient outcomes.

## About the Role

You'll join our Clinical & Research Imaging team, providing critical technical expertise for our diagnostic and research imaging operations. As a Radiology Technician, you'll master advanced imaging modalities while contributing to cutting-edge biotechnology research. Your technical excellence ensures the imaging quality that enables our scientific discoveries.

## Responsibilities

- Operate diagnostic imaging equipment (X-ray, CT, MRI, ultrasound) with precision and safety
- Prepare patients for imaging procedures and ensure their comfort throughout
- Maintain detailed records of imaging studies and ensure regulatory compliance
- Perform equipment maintenance and quality assurance checks
- Collaborate with radiologists and clinical staff on imaging protocols

## Requirements

**Education:** Associate's degree in Radiologic Technology from AERS-accredited program

**Experience:** 0-2 years

**Certifications:** ARRT Certification (or eligible)

**Skills:**
- ARRT certification or eligible for certification
- PACS system proficiency
- Infection control and patient safety protocols
- Ability to follow strict technical imaging standards
- Strong attention to detail and documentation
