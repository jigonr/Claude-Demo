# Data Analyst

**Monday.com** | Tech | Remote | $78,000–$101,000/year | Full-time | Posted Jan 7, 2026

## About Monday.com

Monday.com is the leading work operating system, helping teams worldwide plan, track, and execute their projects seamlessly. With millions of active users across 180+ countries, we're scaling rapidly and building the future of how work gets organized and delivered.

## About the Role

As a Data Analyst at Monday.com, you'll be the data storyteller who connects the dots between what our users do and the decisions we make. Your insights will directly influence product strategy, help us understand user behavior at scale, and drive business metrics that matter. Working in our remote-first environment, you'll collaborate with product, engineering, and business teams to answer the questions that move us forward.

## Responsibilities

- Design and maintain dashboards that track key product metrics, user behavior, and platform performance across millions of active users
- Conduct exploratory analysis on customer engagement patterns to identify growth opportunities and feature adoption trends
- Collaborate with product and engineering teams to define success metrics and validate hypotheses through A/B testing
- Build data pipelines and automate reporting processes to enable self-service analytics across teams
- Present findings and recommendations to leadership, translating complex data into actionable insights
