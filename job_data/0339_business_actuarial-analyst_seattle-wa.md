# Actuarial Analyst

**Company:** PwC | **Category:** Business | **Location:** Seattle, WA | **Salary:** $85,000–$106,000 | **Type:** Full-Time | **Posted:** 2026-03-04

## About PwC

PwC is one of the world's leading professional services firms, with significant expertise in actuarial services. We serve major corporations, government agencies, and institutional clients across insurance, pensions, healthcare, and other industries requiring complex actuarial analysis.

## About the Role

As an Actuarial Analyst at PwC Seattle, you'll work on meaningful projects that shape client business decisions. This role bridges your academic foundation and the real-world complexity of actuarial practice. You'll have structured support to advance your actuarial career through exam preparation, mentorship, and exposure to diverse practice areas.

## Responsibilities

- Perform actuarial valuations and analyses for pension plans, insurance products, and healthcare programs
- Develop financial projections and scenario analyses using actuarial software platforms
- Prepare technical documentation, assumptions memos, and client-facing reports
- Review and validate data; perform quality assurance on models and calculations
- Support senior actuaries in client meetings and proposal development

## Requirements

**Education:** Bachelor's degree in Actuarial Science, Mathematics, Economics, or related quantitative field

**Experience:** 0-2 years of actuarial or related analytical experience

**Skills:**
- Strong mathematical and statistical abilities
- Proficiency with Excel and VBA
- Familiarity with actuarial software (Prophet, Moses, or similar)
- Excellent analytical and problem-solving skills
- Clear communication of complex technical concepts

**Certifications:** Progress toward actuarial exams (SOA or CAS) expected
