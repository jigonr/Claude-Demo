# Community Health Worker

**Kaiser Permanente** | Integrated Healthcare | Baltimore, MD | $35,000–$45,000/year | Full-time | Posted Mar 15, 2026

## About Kaiser Permanente

Kaiser Permanente is one of the largest integrated healthcare organizations in the United States, delivering comprehensive health services to millions. We're committed to improving the health of our members and communities through prevention, care, and healing.

## About the Role

As a Community Health Worker at Kaiser Permanente Baltimore, you'll be the bridge between patients and care. Your work—educating communities, connecting people to resources, and advocating for health equity—directly improves outcomes in neighborhoods that need it most. This hybrid role offers flexibility and the satisfaction of meaningful, tangible impact.

## Responsibilities

- Develop and deliver culturally tailored health education programs on chronic disease management, prevention, and wellness
- Engage with community members to assess health needs and barriers; build trust and relationships across diverse populations
- Connect individuals to Kaiser Permanente services, insurance programs, and community resources
- Collect and report data on community health outcomes and program participation to drive continuous improvement
- Support health equity initiatives and work collaboratively with clinical and community teams

## Requirements

**Education**
High school diploma or GED; Community Health Worker certification preferred

**Experience**
0-3 years of community health, health education, or social services experience

**Skills**

- Community engagement

- Data collection

**Certifications**
Community Health Worker Certification (preferred)
