# Patient Services Representative

**Company:** Mayo Clinic | **Category:** Healthcare | **Location:** Houston, TX | **Salary:** $30,000 - $39,000 | **Type:** Full-Time | **Posted:** 2026-03-12

## About Mayo Clinic

Mayo Clinic is an integrated healthcare delivery system dedicated to patient care and research.

## About the Role

As a Patient Services Representative at Mayo Clinic, you'll be the welcoming face for our patients, helping them navigate their healthcare journey with compassion and efficiency.

## Responsibilities

- Welcome and register patients while maintaining a warm, professional environment
- Verify insurance information and explain coverage details to patients
- Schedule appointments and coordinate with clinical teams
- Handle patient inquiries and concerns with empathy and expertise
- Maintain accurate patient records and follow HIPAA protocols
- Support clinical staff with administrative tasks and patient flow

## Requirements

**Education:** High school diploma or equivalent; relevant degree or certification strongly preferred

**Experience:** 1-3 years

**Skills:**
- Patient communication
- Medical terminology
- Electronic health records
- Customer service
- HIPAA compliance

## Benefits

- Comprehensive compensation package
- Health, dental, and vision coverage
- 401(k) or retirement benefits
- Flexible time off and work arrangements
- Professional development opportunities
