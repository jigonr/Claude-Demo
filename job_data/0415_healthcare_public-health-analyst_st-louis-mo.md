## About the Role
You'll analyze public health data informing global health initiatives. You will develop expertise in critical areas while working alongside experienced professionals who are committed to your growth.

## Responsibilities
- Analyze epidemiological data on disease prevalence and trends
- Support public health surveillance and reporting
- Evaluate intervention programs and health outcomes
- Develop data visualizations for public health stakeholders
- Research best practices and evidence-based approaches
- Contribute to public health publications and presentations

## Requirements

- **Education:** Bachelor's degree in Public Health, Epidemiology, or related field
- **Experience:** 1-3 years of public health research or analysis experience
- **Skills:** Epidemiological methods, Data analysis, Health informatics, Communication, Writing
- **Certifications:** Epidemiology certification (preferred)
