# Training Coordinator

**T. Rowe Price** | Business | Atlanta, GA (Remote) | $45,000–$58,000/year | Full-Time | Posted Mar 2, 2026

## About T. Rowe Price

T. Rowe Price is a global investment manager with headquarters in Baltimore and offices worldwide. We manage assets for individuals, institutions, and financial advisors, backed by a culture of research and disciplined investing. Our 8,000+ employees share a commitment to continuous learning and professional development.

## About the Role

As a Training Coordinator, you'll be the engine behind T. Rowe Price's learning initiatives. Working fully remote, you'll coordinate training programs spanning compliance, product knowledge, and professional development. You'll partner with HR and business teams to ensure employees have the skills they need to succeed in a fast-moving investment management environment.

## Responsibilities

- Coordinate scheduling and logistics for training sessions and workshops
- Assist with developing training materials and course content
- Track training attendance, completion rates, and learner feedback
- Communicate training requirements and deadlines to department managers
- Support onboarding and regulatory compliance training programs
- Manage learning management system (LMS) and training records

## Requirements

**Skills**

- Attention to detail and problem-solving

- Basic knowledge of training and development practices
