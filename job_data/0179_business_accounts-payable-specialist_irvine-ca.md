# Accounts Payable Specialist

**Company:** Johnson & Johnson | **Category:** Business | **Location:** Irvine, CA | **Salary:** $48,000–$63,000 | **Type:** Full-Time | **Posted:** 2026-02-23

## About Johnson & Johnson

Johnson & Johnson is one of the world's most comprehensive healthcare companies, with a consumer health division that produces well-known personal care and wellness products.

## About the Role

As an Accounts Payable Specialist at our Irvine location, you'll be responsible for the efficient processing and payment of vendor invoices for our Consumer Health division. You'll work in a hybrid environment, managing vendor relationships, ensuring accuracy, and supporting our finance operations.

## Responsibilities

- Process and verify vendor invoices; reconcile to purchase orders and receiving reports
- Prepare and process payment runs using accounts payable software
- Manage vendor relationships, resolve payment discrepancies, and maintain vendor files
- Perform account reconciliations and investigate variances
- Prepare accounts payable reports and analysis for management review

## Requirements

**Education:** Bachelor's degree in Accounting, Finance, or related field

**Experience:** 2-3 years of accounts payable or similar accounting experience

**Skills:**
- Proficiency with accounting software and ERP systems
- Strong Excel skills for financial analysis
- Solid understanding of accounting principles
- Excellent organizational and attention to detail
- Ability to work independently and meet deadlines

**Certifications:** None required
