## About Johns Hopkins Medicine

Johns Hopkins Medicine is a world-renowned academic medical center known for clinical excellence, groundbreaking research, and a commitment to improving health. Our San Jose location brings this legacy of caring to California's Bay Area.

## About the Role

As a Certified Nursing Assistant at Johns Hopkins Medicine, you'll be at the heart of patient care. Working onsite in San Jose, you'll provide direct patient support, maintain comfortable care environments, and contribute to the compassionate, high-quality care that Johns Hopkins is known for. This role offers meaningful patient interaction and a pathway into nursing.

## Responsibilities

- Provide direct patient care under nursing supervision
- Assist patients with personal hygiene, grooming, and activities of daily living
- Monitor and report patient vital signs and comfort concerns
- Maintain clean and safe patient environments
- Support nursing staff with patient care procedures
- Contribute to patient communication and emotional support
