**Kaiser Permanente** | Integrated Healthcare | San Jose, CA | $60,000–$78,000/year | Full-time | Posted Mar 16, 2026

## About Kaiser Permanente

Kaiser Permanente is one of the largest integrated healthcare organizations in the United States, serving millions with comprehensive health services and commitment to quality care.

## About the Role

As a Histotechnician at Kaiser Permanente San Jose, you'll process tissue specimens that enable accurate pathological diagnoses. Working hybrid, you'll maintain quality standards and collaborate with pathologists. This role offers competitive pay and the satisfaction of supporting diagnostic excellence.

## Responsibilities

- Process tissue specimens using manual and automated techniques; maintain quality standards throughout
- Prepare slides through staining, mounting, and cover-slipping; ensure consistent preparation
- Operate and maintain histology equipment; perform routine maintenance and troubleshoot issues
- Maintain accurate specimen tracking and documentation throughout the workflow
- Collaborate with pathologists and laboratory supervisors on quality improvement initiatives

## Requirements

**Education**
High school diploma or GED; completion of accredited histotechnology program

**Experience**
2-3 years of histotechnology experience

**Skills**
- Tissue processing and preparation
- Staining and mounting techniques
- Laboratory equipment operation
- Quality control procedures
- Data documentation

**Certifications**
Histotechnician certification (HT/HTL, ASCP)
