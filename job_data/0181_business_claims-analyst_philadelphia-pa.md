# Claims Analyst

**Costco** | Business | Philadelphia, PA | $45,000–$58,000/year | Full-time | Posted Mar 4, 2026

## About Costco

Costco Wholesale is the largest membership warehouse club in the United States, serving millions of members with value and quality goods. We're also a major employer offering excellent benefits and career growth.

## About the Role

As a Claims Analyst at Costco Philadelphia, you'll manage the benefits process that supports our members and employees. Working hybrid, you'll ensure accurate claims processing, resolve issues efficiently, and maintain the high service standards Costco is known for. This role offers stability and clear advancement pathways.

## Responsibilities

- Review and process insurance claims; verify accuracy of claims data and member information
- Identify and investigate claim discrepancies; communicate with providers and members to resolve issues
- Manage claims database; track claims status and maintain detailed records
- Respond to member inquiries about benefits, coverage, and claim status
- Prepare reports on claims trends, denials, and performance metrics for management review

## Requirements

**Skills**

- Insurance knowledge
- Data entry and database management
- Attention to detail
- Customer service
