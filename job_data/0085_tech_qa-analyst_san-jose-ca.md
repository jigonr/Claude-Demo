# QA Analyst

**Company:** Asana | **Category:** Tech | **Location:** San Jose, CA | **Salary:** $81,000–$105,000 | **Type:** Full-Time | **Posted:** 2026-03-19

## About Asana

Asana is a leading work management platform that helps teams organize, track, and manage their work. We serve hundreds of thousands of organizations seeking to increase productivity and collaboration.

## About the Role

As a QA Analyst at Asana San Jose, you'll be responsible for quality assurance across our web and mobile platforms. You'll work in a hybrid environment with engineers and product managers, testing features that impact millions of users. This role offers the opportunity to develop advanced QA skills and contribute to a product trusted by leading organizations worldwide.

## Responsibilities

- Design and execute comprehensive test plans covering functionality, performance, and usability
- Write test automation scripts using Python or JavaScript; maintain test automation frameworks
- Conduct regression testing and performance testing; identify and document defects
- Collaborate with product managers and engineers on testing requirements
- Monitor quality metrics and test coverage; provide recommendations for improvement

## Requirements

**Education:** Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or related field

**Experience:** 3-5 years of QA, testing, or quality assurance experience

**Skills:**
- Test automation using Python, JavaScript, or similar languages
- Experience with testing frameworks and CI/CD pipelines
- Proficiency with API testing and SQL
- Strong analytical and problem-solving abilities
- Excellent communication and documentation skills

**Certifications:** ISTQB certification or similar (preferred)
