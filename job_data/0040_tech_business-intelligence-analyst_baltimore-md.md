## About the Role

You'll join our Analytics team, turning raw data into insights that inform strategic decisions. As a Business Intelligence Analyst, you'll build dashboards, develop analytics solutions, and become a trusted advisor to business leaders. This remote position offers exposure to enterprise-scale data and the opportunity to grow your BI expertise.

## Responsibilities

- Design and develop interactive dashboards and reports using BI tools
- Extract, transform, and analyze data from multiple sources
- Support business stakeholders with ad hoc analysis and insights
- Monitor data quality and recommend data governance improvements
- Document technical specifications and maintain analytics libraries
