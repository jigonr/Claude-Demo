# Cytotechnologist

**AstraZeneca** | Healthcare | Bethesda, MD (Remote) | $66,000–$84,000/year | Full-Time | Posted Feb 23, 2026

## About AstraZeneca

AstraZeneca is a global, science-led pharmaceutical company committed to discovering, developing, and commercializing innovative medicines that address serious diseases. Our Oncology Division leads breakthrough research in cancer therapeutics, supported by world-class laboratory and diagnostic services.

## About the Role

We're looking for a detail-oriented Cytotechnologist to support our drug development and clinical pathology operations. You'll examine cellular specimens using advanced microscopy techniques, identify abnormalities, and provide critical data that informs our therapeutic research. Working remotely, you'll be part of a collaborative team committed to advancing precision medicine.

## Responsibilities

- Prepare, stain, and examine tissue and body fluid specimens using microscopy techniques
- Identify and document cellular abnormalities and morphological changes under supervision
- Maintain detailed laboratory records and quality assurance documentation
- Collaborate with pathologists and research scientists on specimen analysis and interpretation
- Perform special staining procedures and immunohistochemical techniques as needed
- Adhere to strict protocol standards for research-grade specimen handling and analysis

## Requirements

**Skills**

- Quality assurance procedures

**Certifications**
