# Pharmacy Technician

**Company:** Mount Sinai Health
**Category:** Healthcare
**Location:** Raleigh, NC, US (Onsite)
**Salary:** $33,000 - $45,000/year
**Employment Type:** Full-time
**Posted:** 2026-01-02

## About Mount Sinai Health
Mount Sinai is a leading health system with world-class clinical care, research, and education.

## About the Role
You'll ensure accurate medication dispensing supporting patient-centered care. You will develop expertise in critical areas while working alongside experienced professionals who are committed to your growth.

## Responsibilities
- Prepare and dispense medications following prescriptions
- Verify medication inventory and manage pharmacy stock
- Label medications and provide patient safety instructions
- Maintain confidentiality of patient medication information
- Support inventory management and ordering processes
- Assist with continuing education and license maintenance

## Requirements

- **Education:** High school diploma or equivalent; pharmacy technician certification required
- **Experience:** 0-2 years of pharmacy or healthcare experience
- **Skills:** Medication dispensing, Pharmacy systems, Patient safety, HIPAA compliance, Inventory management
- **Certifications:** Pharmacy Technician Certification (CPhT), CPR/BLS

## Benefits
- Comprehensive health insurance (medical, dental, vision)
- 401(k) retirement plan with company match
- Paid time off (18-22 days annually)
- Professional development and continuing education support
- Employee assistance program (EAP) with mental health services
