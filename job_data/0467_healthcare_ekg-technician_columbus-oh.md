## About Johns Hopkins Medicine

Johns Hopkins Medicine is a world-leading institution advancing healthcare through research, education, and patient care.

## About the Role

Support our cardiology team at Johns Hopkins Medicine by performing electrocardiograms and cardiac monitoring procedures with precision and care.

## Responsibilities

- Perform 12-lead ECG procedures and cardiac stress tests
- Operate telemetry monitoring systems and interpret basic rhythms
- Prepare patients, explain procedures, and ensure comfort
- Maintain equipment calibration and troubleshoot technical issues
- Document findings and communicate abnormalities to clinical staff
- Maintain patient confidentiality and follow infection control protocols

**Experience:** 0-3 years
