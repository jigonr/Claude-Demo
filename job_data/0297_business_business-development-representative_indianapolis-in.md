# Business Development Representative

**Travelers** | Business | Indianapolis, IN | $38,000–$51,000/year | Full-time | Posted Mar 7, 2026

## About Travelers

Travelers is a leading provider of property and casualty insurance and other insurance products and services. With a legacy of trust and innovation, we serve millions of customers.

## About the Role

As a Business Development Representative at Travelers Indianapolis, you'll be the face of growth. Working hybrid, you'll prospect new business, build relationships, and drive our expansion in the Indiana market. This sales role offers competitive commission potential and clear pathways to management.

## Responsibilities

- Identify and prospect new business opportunities in assigned territory; conduct outreach via phone, email, and in-person meetings
- Qualify leads based on pain points and fit with Travelers' products and services
- Build relationships with prospects and referral sources; maintain database of opportunities
- Present Travelers' products and solutions; coordinate proposals and quotes with internal teams
- Track pipeline and report on business development activities; achieve quarterly growth targets
