# Respiratory Therapist

**AstraZeneca** | Healthcare | Austin, TX | $50,000–$65,000 USD/year | Full-time | Posted Mar 17, 2026

## About AstraZeneca

AstraZeneca is a global biopharmaceutical company focused on oncology, cardiovascular, renal and metabolism, respiratory, and immunology medicines. Our Austin facility supports respiratory medicine research and development.

## About the Role

As a Respiratory Therapist at AstraZeneca's Austin facility, you'll support respiratory research that advances treatment for serious respiratory diseases. Work hybrid in a pharmaceutical environment where your clinical expertise drives innovation.

## Responsibilities

- Provide respiratory care support for clinical research studies
- Assist with pulmonary function testing and patient assessments
- Manage respiratory equipment and ensure proper operation
- Monitor patient respiratory status during research protocols
- Document observations and maintain research records
- Support quality control and protocol compliance

## Requirements

**Education**
Associate's degree or Bachelor's degree in Respiratory Therapy

**Experience**
2–4 years in respiratory therapy, clinical research, or pharmaceutical support roles

**Skills**
- Respiratory assessment and therapy techniques
- Knowledge of pharmaceutical research and protocols
- Pulmonary function testing and equipment operation
- Patient care and communication skills
- Attention to detail and regulatory compliance
- Ability to work in research environment

**Certifications**
RRT (Registered Respiratory Therapist) certification required
