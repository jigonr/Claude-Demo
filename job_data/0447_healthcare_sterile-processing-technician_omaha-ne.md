# Sterile Processing Technician

**Company:** Biogen | **Category:** Healthcare | **Location:** Omaha, NE | **Salary:** $28,000 - $36,000 | **Type:** Full-Time | **Posted:** 2026-03-02

## About Biogen

Biogen is a biotechnology company discovering, developing, and delivering therapies.

## About the Role

Support Biogen's clinical operations as a Sterile Processing Technician, ensuring instruments are properly cleaned and sterilized.

## Responsibilities

- Clean and decontaminate surgical instruments following protocols
- Operate sterilization equipment and monitor sterilization cycles
- Prepare instrument trays and maintain inventory
- Maintain sterile field standards and infection control
- Document sterilization records and equipment maintenance
- Troubleshoot equipment issues
