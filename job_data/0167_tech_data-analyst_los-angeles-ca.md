## About Cisco
Cisco powers the Internet with secure, intelligent networking solutions serving billions of users worldwide. We process over 5 zettabytes of data annually across our platform, connecting organizations of all sizes.

## About the Role
We're seeking a Data Analyst to join our Networking team in Los Angeles. You'll work with talented colleagues dedicated to excellence and innovation in our field.

## Responsibilities
- Analyze complex datasets to identify trends and actionable business insights

- Create dashboards and visualizations in Tableau/Power BI for stakeholder reporting
- Collaborate with product and engineering teams to inform data-driven decisions
- Document data models and analytical methodologies for team knowledge sharing
- Troubleshoot data quality issues and implement preventive validation controls

## Requirements

- **Experience:** 1-3 years of data analysis or business intelligence experience
- **Skills:** SQL, Python or R, Tableau or Power BI, Data modeling, Statistical analysis
