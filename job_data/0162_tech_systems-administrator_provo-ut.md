# Systems Administrator

**Company:** Toast | **Category:** Tech | **Location:** Provo, UT | **Salary:** $49,000–$63,000 | **Type:** Full-Time | **Posted:** 2026-01-11

## About Toast

Toast is the cloud-based all-in-one platform for restaurants and quick-service businesses. We help restaurant owners streamline operations, engage customers, and grow their business.

## About the Role

As a Systems Administrator at Toast Provo, you'll be responsible for maintaining the IT infrastructure that supports our operations team. You'll work directly with colleagues across the company and support our mission to help restaurants thrive. This onsite role offers hands-on experience in systems administration and IT operations.

## Responsibilities

- Manage Windows and Linux servers; perform patches, updates, and maintenance
- Administer network infrastructure, firewalls, and VPN connections
- Support user accounts, permissions, and IT security policies
- Troubleshoot hardware and software issues; provide technical support
- Document systems, procedures, and maintain IT asset inventory

## Requirements

**Education:** Bachelor's degree in IT, Computer Science, or related field; or equivalent experience

**Experience:** 1-2 years of systems administration or IT support experience

**Skills:**
- Windows and Linux system administration
- Networking fundamentals and TCP/IP
- Active Directory and user management
- Troubleshooting and problem-solving abilities
- Strong communication and customer service skills

**Certifications:** CompTIA A+ or Network+ certification (preferred)
