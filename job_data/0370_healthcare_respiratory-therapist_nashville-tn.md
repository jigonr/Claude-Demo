## About IQVIA

IQVIA is a leading healthcare research and clinical development organization supporting pharmaceutical and biotech companies in bringing new treatments to patients.

## About the Role

We're seeking a Respiratory Therapist to support our clinical research operations, assisting with respiratory function testing and patient assessments for trials evaluating pulmonary medications and devices. Working remotely, you will conduct spirometry tests and monitor patient safety.

## Responsibilities

- Conduct spirometry and pulmonary function testing per study protocols
- Monitor patient vital signs and oxygen saturation during procedures
- Document test results and patient responses accurately
- Provide study participant education on respiratory procedures
- Maintain equipment and ensure compliance with calibration standards
- Collaborate with study coordinators and physicians on protocol adherence

## Requirements

**Experience**

**Skills**
- Pulmonary function testing and spirometry
- Patient safety and vital sign monitoring
- Electronic data capture and documentation
- Clinical trial protocol understanding
- Patient communication and comfort

**Certifications**
- ARDS certification required
- BLS/CPR certification current
