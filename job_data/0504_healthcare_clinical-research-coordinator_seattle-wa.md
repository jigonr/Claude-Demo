# Clinical Research Coordinator

**Intermountain Health** | Healthcare | Seattle, WA | $60,000–$77,000/year | Full-Time | Posted Jan 20, 2026

## About Intermountain Health

Intermountain Health is one of the nation's leading integrated healthcare systems, serving patients across the Pacific Northwest. We're committed to advancing evidence-based medicine through clinical research that improves patient outcomes and drives healthcare innovation.

## About the Role

As a Clinical Research Coordinator with Intermountain Health, you'll be the operational backbone of clinical studies that advance medical knowledge. You'll recruit participants, ensure protocol compliance, manage documentation, and work closely with physicians and research teams. This onsite role in Seattle combines administrative precision with meaningful healthcare mission.

## Responsibilities

- Coordinate day-to-day operations of clinical research studies
- Recruit and screen study participants meeting protocol criteria
- Obtain informed consent and manage participant documentation
- Monitor protocol compliance and ensure regulatory adherence
- Maintain study-specific databases and quality assurance records
- Communicate regularly with principal investigators and study teams

## Requirements

**Education**
Bachelor's degree in healthcare, life sciences, or related field

**Experience**
1–3 years in clinical research, healthcare coordination, or related roles

**Skills**
- Clinical research knowledge and GCP compliance
- Patient communication and interpersonal skills
- Attention to detail and data management
- Project coordination and organizational skills
- Ability to maintain participant confidentiality
- Electronic medical records (EMR) experience

**Certifications**
- CITI certification or GCP training (can be obtained on the job)
