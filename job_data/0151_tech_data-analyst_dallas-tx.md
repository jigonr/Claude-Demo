## About GitLab

GitLab is the all-in-one DevOps platform that powers collaboration for software development teams. We're transforming the way organizations deliver software, reducing cycle time and enabling engineering excellence. Our platform is used by over 30 million developers globally.

## About the Role

As a Data Analyst at GitLab's Dallas office, you'll unlock insights that drive product decisions. You'll work with product, engineering, and business teams to measure what matters—from user adoption to feature usage to performance metrics. Your analyses will directly influence how we prioritize features and improve the developer experience.

## Responsibilities

- Design and run analytics queries using SQL to answer business questions
- Create dashboards and visualizations to track key product metrics
- Analyze user behavior and product usage patterns
- Collaborate with product and engineering teams on data-driven decisions
- Document analytical methodologies and findings clearly
- Monitor data quality and troubleshoot analytics issues
