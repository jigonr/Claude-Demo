## About CDC
The CDC is the nation's premier public health institute, protecting Americans from infectious diseases, environmental hazards, and health threats.

## About the Role
You'll develop and deliver health education programs impacting millions. You will develop expertise in critical areas while working alongside experienced professionals who are committed to your growth.

## Responsibilities
- Develop and deliver health education programs on disease prevention
- Create educational materials for diverse audiences
- Track program participation and health outcomes
- Partner with community organizations to reach target populations
- Evaluate program effectiveness and recommend improvements
- Support surveillance and data collection efforts

## Requirements

- **Education:** Bachelor's degree in Public Health, Health Education, or related field
- **Experience:** 1-3 years of health education or community health experience
- **Skills:** Health communication, Program development, Community engagement, Data collection, Cultural competence
- **Certifications:** CHES (Certified Health Education Specialist) or pursuing
