## About Cleveland Clinic

Cleveland Clinic is consistently ranked among the nation's top hospitals. We're committed to the philosophy that the patient is the center of everything we do, guiding our integrated care delivery model and supporting exceptional clinical outcomes.

## About the Role

As a Respiratory Therapist at our Indianapolis location, you'll deliver critical respiratory care to diverse patient populations. You'll manage mechanical ventilation, administer medications, and work collaboratively with our multidisciplinary team to optimize patient outcomes. This is hands-on, meaningful work that directly impacts lives.

## Responsibilities

- Evaluate patients and initiate appropriate respiratory therapy protocols
- Manage and monitor mechanical ventilation systems and patient response
- Administer aerosol medications and perform chest physiotherapy
- Collect and analyze blood gas samples to assess respiratory status
- Educate patients and families on breathing exercises and respiratory hygiene
- Collaborate with physicians and nursing staff on treatment plans and adjustments
