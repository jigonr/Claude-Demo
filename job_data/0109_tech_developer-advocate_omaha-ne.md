# Developer Advocate

**SpaceX** | Tech | Omaha, NE | $60,000–$76,000/year | Full-Time | Posted Jan 22, 2026

## About SpaceX

SpaceX builds rockets and spacecraft for commercial and government customers, with a mission to make humanity multiplanetary. Our flight software enables the world's most reliable launch vehicles and spacecraft. We're looking for passionate technologists who want to shape how the world interacts with our systems.

## About the Role

As a Developer Advocate at SpaceX, you'll be the bridge between our Flight Software team and the developer community. You'll create resources that make our APIs more accessible, gather feedback from developers using our tools, and help eliminate friction in how people integrate with SpaceX systems. You'll own the developer experience from documentation to community engagement.

## Responsibilities

- Create and maintain technical documentation, code samples, and tutorials for SpaceX flight software APIs and developer tools
- Engage with external developers and internal teams to understand pain points and improve developer experience
- Build demo applications that showcase best practices for integrating with SpaceX systems
- Present at developer conferences and internal tech talks to share technical insights
- Maintain a feedback loop between developers and product teams to identify API improvements
- Monitor community forums and GitHub repositories to provide timely technical support
