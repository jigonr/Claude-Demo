# Patient Services Representative

**Stryker** | Medical Devices | Durham, NC | $30,000–$39,000/year | Full-time | Posted Jan 25, 2026

## About Stryker

Stryker is a leading medical technology company offering products and services that improve people's lives. We're committed to customer support and clinical excellence.

## About the Role

As a Patient Services Representative at Stryker Durham, you'll be the support patients rely on when using our medical devices. Working onsite, you'll provide education, answer questions, and ensure patient safety. This role offers meaningful healthcare impact and direct patient contact.

## Responsibilities

- Respond to patient inquiries about medical devices; provide education on usage and care
- Coordinate with healthcare providers to ensure patient access to needed devices
- Maintain patient database and communication records; ensure HIPAA compliance
- Support patient education and support programs; facilitate device training sessions
- Gather patient feedback and communicate insights to product and clinical teams

## Requirements

**Education**
High school diploma or GED

**Experience**

**Skills**

- Medical device knowledge
- HIPAA awareness
- Data management
- Customer service

None required
