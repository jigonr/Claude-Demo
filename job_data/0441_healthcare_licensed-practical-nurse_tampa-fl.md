# Licensed Practical Nurse

**Fortrea** | Healthcare | Tampa, FL | $37,000–$49,000 USD/year | Full-time | Posted Mar 21, 2026

## About Fortrea

Fortrea is a leading provider of clinical research services, supporting pharmaceutical companies and research institutions in their efforts to develop and advance new treatments. We partner with healthcare professionals who are passionate about advancing medicine.

## About the Role

As a Licensed Practical Nurse at Fortrea, you'll provide essential patient care in clinical research settings. You'll work directly with study participants, administer treatments, and contribute to the advancement of medical science. Our hybrid model allows you to work primarily on-site for direct patient care while maintaining schedule flexibility.

## Responsibilities

- Administer medications and treatments under physician supervision in accordance with protocol requirements
- Collect vital signs and patient specimens for clinical research studies
- Assess patient health status and document observations in electronic health records
- Monitor patients for adverse events and report safety concerns to study team
- Provide patient education regarding study procedures and medication adherence
- Maintain infection control and safety protocols in the clinical facility

## Requirements

**Education**
Licensed Practical Nurse (LPN) credential from accredited program

**Experience**
1–3 years of nursing practice in clinical, hospital, or healthcare settings

**Skills**
- Current LPN licensure in Florida
- Knowledge of clinical trial procedures and Good Clinical Practice (GCP)
- Strong patient assessment and communication skills
- Proficiency with electronic health records (EHR) systems
- Ability to work with diverse patient populations
- Attention to detail and regulatory compliance

**Certifications**
- Current Florida LPN license required
- CPR/BLS certification required
- GCP certification required within 60 days of hire
