# Respiratory Therapist

## About Mount Sinai Health
Mount Sinai is a leading health system with world-class clinical care, research, and education.

## About the Role
You'll ensure accurate medication dispensing supporting patient-centered care. You will develop expertise in critical areas while working alongside experienced professionals who are committed to your growth.

## Responsibilities
- Provide respiratory care and mechanical ventilation support
- Perform pulmonary function testing and monitoring
- Assist with airway management and intubation procedures
- Educate patients on respiratory conditions and treatment
- Maintain respiratory equipment and ensure safety
- Document interventions and patient response

## Requirements

- **Education:** Associate's degree in Respiratory Therapy; RRT licensure required
- **Experience:** 1-3 years of respiratory care experience
- **Skills:** Ventilator management, Airway management, Patient assessment, Equipment operation, Patient education
- **Certifications:** RRT certification, CPR/ACLS
