# Sales Operations Analyst

**Company:** Charles Schwab | **Category:** Business | **Location:** Boston, MA | **Salary:** $65,000–$85,000 | **Type:** Full-Time | **Posted:** 2026-02-12

## About Charles Schwab

Charles Schwab is a leading provider of financial services and banking products to investors and traders. With a 50-year legacy of democratizing investing, we're committed to empowering every client with the tools, guidance, and service they deserve.

## About the Role

As a Sales Operations Analyst, you'll be instrumental in driving revenue efficiency across our Boston sales organization. This role sits at the intersection of sales, strategy, and analytics—you'll transform data into actionable insights, design processes that scale, and enable our sales teams to focus on what they do best: building client relationships and driving growth.

## Responsibilities

- Maintain and analyze Salesforce data integrity, identifying trends and anomalies affecting sales team performance
- Create and maintain sales dashboards and reporting frameworks that track pipeline, conversion rates, and forecast accuracy
- Collaborate with sales leadership to design compensation models and territory structures that drive optimal results
- Support the implementation of new sales tools and processes, providing training and ongoing support to end users
- Generate weekly and monthly sales reports, conducting variance analysis and recommending process improvements

## Requirements

**Education:** Bachelor's degree in Business Administration, Finance, or related field preferred

**Experience:** 2-4 years in sales operations, business analysis, or similar analytical role

**Skills:**
- Salesforce administration and configuration
- Excel and SQL for data analysis
- Strong analytical and problem-solving abilities
- Excellent communication and stakeholder management
- Process documentation and optimization

**Certifications:** Salesforce Administrator certification (preferred)
