# Database Administrator I

**Company:** Shopify | **Category:** Tech | **Location:** Tampa, FL | **Salary:** $58,000–$73,000 | **Type:** Full-Time | **Posted:** 2026-03-24

## About Shopify

Shopify is a leading e-commerce platform that enables merchants of all sizes to build and grow their businesses. We power online stores, retail locations, and complex logistics operations for hundreds of thousands of merchants globally.

## About the Role

As a Database Administrator I at Shopify Tampa, you'll be responsible for the reliability and performance of databases that support our platform. You'll work with experienced infrastructure engineers, contribute to system improvements, and develop expertise in managing databases at scale.

## Responsibilities

- Administer PostgreSQL and MySQL databases, performing patches, upgrades, and maintenance
- Monitor database performance, capacity, and health; implement optimization strategies
- Design and execute backup and disaster recovery procedures with rigorous testing
- Manage database access controls and security policies
- Support application development teams in schema design and query optimization

## Requirements

**Education:** Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or related field

**Experience:** 1-3 years of database administration or systems administration

**Skills:**
- PostgreSQL or MySQL administration
- Linux system administration fundamentals
- Proficiency with monitoring and logging tools
- Shell scripting and basic automation
- Strong troubleshooting and analytical abilities

**Certifications:** Database certification (PostgreSQL, MySQL) preferred
