## About the Role

As a Speech-Language Pathology Assistant at Mount Sinai Health, you'll directly support patients in their communication and swallowing recovery. Working onsite in Pittsburgh, you'll assist licensed SLPs with therapy delivery, maintain clinical documentation, and provide compassionate patient care. This entry-level role offers valuable experience in a prestigious academic medical setting.

## Responsibilities

- Assist speech-language pathologists with patient evaluations and therapy sessions
- Support patient exercises and communication activities under SLP supervision
- Maintain accurate clinical documentation and progress notes
- Educate patients and families on therapeutic strategies
- Maintain equipment and prepare therapy materials
- Contribute to a patient-centered, collaborative care environment
