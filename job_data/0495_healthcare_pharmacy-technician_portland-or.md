# Pharmacy Technician

**Genentech** | Biotechnology | Portland, OR | $36,000–$50,000/year | Full-time | Posted Mar 25, 2026

## About Genentech

Genentech is a member of the Roche Group, pioneering biotechnology for life-altering medicines. We're advancing treatments for serious diseases through rigorous science and innovation.

## About the Role

As a Pharmacy Technician at Genentech Portland, you'll support our clinical and pharmaceutical operations. You'll manage medications, maintain regulatory compliance, and contribute to our mission of developing life-saving treatments. This onsite role offers technical expertise combined with meaningful healthcare impact.

## Responsibilities

- Receive, store, and manage pharmaceutical inventory; ensure proper handling and rotation of stock
- Prepare medication doses under pharmacist supervision; verify accuracy and completeness
- Maintain pharmacy records and documentation; ensure HIPAA and DEA compliance
- Support clinical trials and research programs; coordinate medication distribution for studies
- Assist patients and healthcare providers; communicate clearly about medications and procedures

## Requirements

**Education**
High school diploma or GED; completion of pharmacy technician program

**Experience**
1-3 years of pharmacy or pharmaceutical operations experience

**Skills**
- Pharmacy operations and procedures
- Medication management
- HIPAA and regulatory compliance
- Drug interactions knowledge
- Patient communication

**Certifications**
Certified Pharmacy Technician (CPhT, preferred)
