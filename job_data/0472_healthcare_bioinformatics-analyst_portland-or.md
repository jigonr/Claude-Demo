## About Boston Scientific
Boston Scientific develops innovative medical devices improving patient outcomes across cardiovascular, oncology, and neuromodulation.

## About the Role
You'll analyze biological data supporting device development and clinical trials. You will develop expertise in critical areas while working alongside experienced professionals who are committed to your growth.

## Responsibilities
- Analyze genomic and clinical data to support product development
- Develop and validate bioinformatics pipelines
- Manage scientific databases and data curation
- Collaborate with research scientists on computational analyses
- Document methods and create analysis reports
- Support regulatory submissions for medical devices

## Requirements

- **Education:** Bachelor's degree in Bioinformatics, Computational Biology, or related field
- **Experience:** 2-4 years of bioinformatics or computational biology experience
- **Skills:** Sequence analysis, Genomic databases, Python/R, Data management, Scientific communication
- **Certifications:** Bioinformatics certification (preferred)
