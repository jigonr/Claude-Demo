## About General Mills
General Mills is a global manufacturer and marketer of branded consumer foods with 100+ years of heritage.

## About the Role
You'll support our finance organization with accuracy and attention to detail. You will develop expertise in critical areas while working alongside experienced professionals who are committed to your growth.

## Responsibilities
- Process accounts payable and receivable transactions with accuracy
- Reconcile bank statements and general ledger accounts
- Support monthly close procedures and journal entry preparation
- Assist with internal and external audits
- Maintain accurate financial records in ERP systems
- Resolve discrepancies and support process improvements

## Requirements

- **Education:** Associate's degree in Accounting or Bachelor's degree in Accounting/Finance
- **Experience:** 1-3 years of accounting or bookkeeping experience
- **Skills:** Journal entry preparation, Account reconciliation, Financial reporting, ERP systems, Excel
- **Certifications:** Pursuing CPA (preferred)
