# Pharmacy Technician

**Company:** Baxter International
**Category:** Healthcare
**Location:** Pittsburgh, PA (Remote)
**Salary:** $31,000 - $43,000 USD/yearly
**Employment Type:** Full-time
**Posted:** 2026-02-17

## About Baxter International

Baxter International is a global healthcare company providing critical care and renal therapies, advanced surgical care, and pharmaceutical solutions to healthcare providers and patients worldwide.

## About the Role

We're seeking a Pharmacy Technician to support our remote pharmacy operations serving healthcare facilities nationwide. You will prepare and dispense medications, manage pharmacy inventory, and ensure regulatory compliance.

## Responsibilities

- Prepare medications according to pharmacist-verified prescriptions
- Manage pharmacy inventory and coordinate with suppliers
- Enter prescription information into pharmacy management systems
- Perform quality checks on compounded medications
- Communicate with patients and healthcare providers regarding prescriptions
- Maintain detailed records for DEA and state pharmacy compliance

## Requirements

**Education**
High school diploma or GED; pharmacy technician program completion required or in-progress

**Experience**
1-2 years of pharmacy technician or healthcare experience

**Skills**
- Medication preparation and compounding
- Pharmacy information systems
- Attention to detail and accuracy
- Customer service and communication
- Understanding of pharmaceutical terminology

**Certifications**
- Pennsylvania Pharmacy Technician License required
- CPhT certification preferred
