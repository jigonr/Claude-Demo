## About Zendesk

Zendesk is a leading customer service platform trusted by thousands of companies worldwide. Our Ann Arbor team supports product innovation and customer success across our suite of support, sales, and engagement tools.

## About the Role

We're growing our Ann Arbor analytics team and seeking a Business Intelligence Analyst to build dashboards and reports that drive customer success insights. You'll work with product, sales, and customer success teams to translate business questions into SQL queries, create visualizations, and uncover patterns in how customers use our platform. This role is perfect for someone who loves finding stories in data.

## Responsibilities

- Build SQL queries against our product database to answer business questions from stakeholders
- Design and maintain Tableau dashboards tracking customer health, engagement, and churn indicators
- Partner with product management to analyze feature adoption and identify user segmentation opportunities
- Conduct exploratory data analysis on customer feedback and support ticket data to identify trends
- Create weekly and monthly reports for executive leadership on key performance metrics
- Document data assets and maintain a self-service analytics wiki for internal teams
