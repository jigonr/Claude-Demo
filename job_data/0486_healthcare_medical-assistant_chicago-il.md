# Medical Assistant

**Company:** Syneos Health | **Category:** Healthcare | **Location:** Chicago, IL (Onsite) | **Salary:** $33,000-$44,000/year | **Type:** Full-time | **Posted:** Mar 18, 2026

## About Syneos Health

Syneos Health is a biopharmaceutical services company dedicated to advancing patient care through innovative clinical research and development solutions. We work at the intersection of patient, provider, and pharmaceutical innovation.

## About the Role

You'll work directly with patients and research teams in our Chicago clinical research facility. As a Medical Assistant, you'll perform essential clinical tasks—taking vitals, assisting with procedures, and collecting specimens—while supporting research that advances new treatments. This onsite role offers hands-on healthcare experience.

## Responsibilities

- Take and record vital signs (blood pressure, temperature, pulse, respiration)
- Prepare examination rooms and equipment for patient visits
- Assist with patient examinations and procedures under supervision
- Collect blood samples and other specimens according to protocol
- Maintain accurate patient records and study documentation

## Requirements

**Education:** High school diploma or GED; medical assistant certificate preferred

**Experience:** 0-2 years

**Certifications:** CMA or RMA certification (preferred)

**Skills:**
- Patient care and communication
- Medical terminology
- HIPAA compliance and confidentiality
- Ability to follow clinical protocols precisely
- Physical stamina for clinical environment
