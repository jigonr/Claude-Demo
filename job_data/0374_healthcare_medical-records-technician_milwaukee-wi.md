# Medical Records Technician

**Company:** AstraZeneca
**Category:** Healthcare
**Location:** Milwaukee, WI, US (Hybrid)
**Salary:** $29,000 - $38,000/year
**Employment Type:** Full-time
**Posted:** 2026-01-09

## About AstraZeneca
AstraZeneca is a global biopharmaceutical company focused on oncology, cardiovascular, and respiratory treatments.

## About the Role
You'll maintain critical medical records for our research and clinical operations. You will develop expertise in critical areas while working alongside experienced professionals who are committed to your growth.

## Responsibilities
- Manage patient medical records ensuring accuracy and compliance
- Code medical procedures and diagnoses for billing and research
- Maintain confidentiality and enforce HIPAA protocols
- Prepare records for internal audits and external reviews
- Respond to records requests from patients and providers
- Update systems with clinical information from various sources

## Requirements

- **Education:** Associate's degree in Health Information Technology or related field
- **Experience:** 1-3 years of medical records or health information management experience
- **Skills:** ICD/CPT coding, Medical terminology, HIPAA compliance, EHR systems, Quality assurance
- **Certifications:** RHIT or pursuing RHIT, HIPAA certification
