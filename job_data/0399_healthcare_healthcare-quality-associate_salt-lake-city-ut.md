## About Syneos Health

Syneos Health is a leading patient-centric, technology-enabled services company connecting pharmacovigilance, clinical research, and contract development and manufacturing services.

## About the Role

We're hiring a Healthcare Quality Associate to support quality assurance and compliance activities for our Salt Lake City clinical research operations. You will conduct quality reviews, document findings, develop corrective action plans, and work collaboratively with site teams to ensure adherence to GCP standards.

## Responsibilities

- Conduct quality audits and site inspections for clinical research facilities
- Document audit findings and develop corrective action plans
- Verify compliance with GCP, ICH, and regulatory guidelines
- Prepare quality reports for management and regulatory submissions
- Coordinate with site teams on quality improvements
- Maintain audit documentation and regulatory files

## Requirements

**Education**
Bachelor's degree in Healthcare, Life Sciences, Business, or related field

**Experience**
1-3 years of quality assurance, clinical research, or healthcare compliance experience

**Skills**
- Knowledge of Good Clinical Practice (GCP) standards
- Audit and compliance assessment
- Documentation and report writing
- Attention to regulatory detail
- Cross-functional communication skills

**Certifications**
- ACRP or SOCRA training preferred
