# Database Administrator I

**Company:** Cisco | **Category:** Tech | **Location:** Washington, DC | **Salary:** $78,000–$98,000 | **Type:** Full-Time | **Posted:** 2026-02-14

## About Cisco

Cisco is the worldwide technology leader in networking that transforms how people connect, communicate, and collaborate. Our infrastructure is built on reliability, scale, and innovation—and that starts with the databases that power it all.

## About the Role

As a Database Administrator I at Cisco, you'll own critical databases that support our enterprise systems and global operations. This is a hands-on role where you'll gain deep technical expertise while working on real-world challenges at scale. You'll collaborate with senior DBAs, application teams, and infrastructure engineers to ensure data integrity, performance, and availability.

## Responsibilities

- Monitor database performance and health, proactively identifying and resolving bottlenecks
- Execute backup and recovery procedures, testing disaster recovery plans quarterly
- Manage user access, privileges, and security policies across Oracle and PostgreSQL environments
- Optimize query performance and index structures based on execution plans and workload analysis
- Collaborate with application teams to understand schema requirements and design efficient databases

## Requirements

**Education:** Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Information Technology, or equivalent professional experience

**Experience:** 1-3 years of database administration experience

**Skills:**
- Oracle or PostgreSQL database administration
- Linux/Unix system administration
- SQL query writing and optimization
- Bash or Python scripting
- Understanding of replication, clustering, and backup technologies

**Certifications:** Oracle Associate DBA or PostgreSQL certification (preferred)
