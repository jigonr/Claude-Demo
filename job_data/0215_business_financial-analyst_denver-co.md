## About Lockheed Martin

Lockheed Martin is a global aerospace and defense company serving government and commercial customers. Our Denver Space Operations division develops advanced systems for national security and exploration missions.

## About the Role

We're seeking a Financial Analyst to support cost analysis, budget tracking, and financial forecasting for our Space Operations division. You will analyze contract spending patterns, monitor project budgets against actual expenditures, and develop financial models to support strategic decision-making in a complex defense procurement environment.

## Responsibilities

- Conduct cost analysis on space systems contracts using earned value management principles
- Develop monthly budget variance reports and present findings to program management
- Build financial models to forecast project costs and identify risk exposures
- Analyze historical spending data to improve future cost estimation accuracy
- Maintain compliance with Defense Contract Audit Agency (DCAA) financial standards
- Support strategic initiatives to optimize operational efficiency and reduce unit costs

## Requirements

**Skills**

**Certifications**
