# EKG Technician

**Company:** Baxter International | **Category:** Healthcare | **Location:** Austin, TX | **Salary:** $36,000–$47,000 | **Type:** Full-Time | **Posted:** 2026-01-17

## About Baxter International

Baxter International is a global healthcare company providing solutions for patients with critical illnesses and those recovering from surgery. We have a longstanding commitment to healthcare innovation and patient care.

## About the Role

As an EKG Technician at Baxter International Austin, you'll perform diagnostic testing that helps clinicians assess patient heart health. Working in a hybrid arrangement, you'll be part of our clinical operations team and contribute directly to patient care outcomes.

## Responsibilities

- Perform electrocardiographic (EKG) tests; operate and maintain EKG equipment
- Record patient information and test results accurately; maintain records
- Communicate with patients to ensure comfort and obtain necessary information
- Perform routine equipment maintenance and troubleshooting
- Support clinical staff with test scheduling and coordination

## Requirements

**Education:** High school diploma or GED; EKG certification preferred

**Experience:** 1-2 years of EKG or clinical healthcare experience

**Skills:**
- Proficiency with EKG equipment and operation
- Strong patient care and interpersonal abilities
- Accuracy and attention to detail
- Medical terminology knowledge
- Ability to work in a clinical healthcare setting

**Certifications:** EKG Technician Certification (preferred)
