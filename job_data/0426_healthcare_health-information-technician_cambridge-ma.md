# Health Information Technician

**Fortrea** | Healthcare | Cambridge, MA (Remote) | $49,000–$65,000 USD/year | Full-time | Posted Feb 16, 2026

## About Fortrea

Fortrea is a leading provider of clinical research services supporting the development of new medications and treatments. We work with pharmaceutical companies and research institutions to advance medical innovation.

## About the Role

As a Health Information Technician at Fortrea, you'll manage critical health data that supports clinical research. Work fully remote while maintaining the data integrity that allows research teams to focus on scientific discovery. Your attention to detail directly contributes to the success of studies that improve human health.

## Responsibilities

- Manage and organize electronic health records for clinical research studies
- Verify data accuracy and completeness according to study protocols and regulatory requirements
- Prepare reports on data quality metrics and audit findings
- Support data entry and ensure adherence to data governance standards
- Assist in developing and maintaining standard operating procedures for data management
- Coordinate with clinical and research teams regarding data-related questions

## Requirements

**Education**
Associate's degree in Health Information Technology, Health Information Management, or related field

**Experience**
1–3 years in health information management, medical records, or healthcare data administration

**Skills**
- Experience with electronic health record (EHR) systems
- Knowledge of HIPAA and data privacy regulations
- Proficiency with healthcare data management software
- Strong organizational and attention to detail skills
- Excel and database skills
- Excellent communication and problem-solving abilities

**Certifications**
RHIT (Registered Health Information Technician) or RHIA (Registered Health Information Administrator) preferred
