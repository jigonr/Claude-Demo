## About Liberty Mutual

Liberty Mutual is one of the largest, most customer-centric insurance companies in the United States. We insure millions of customers and manage billions in assets, supported by talented teams across the country.

## About the Role

As a Pricing Analyst working fully remote, you'll support Liberty Mutual's rate development and pricing strategy. You'll analyze data, develop pricing models, and contribute to insurance products that serve millions of customers. This role combines analytical rigor with business impact in a Fortune 500 insurance organization.

## Responsibilities

- Analyze rating data and develop loss trend projections
- Support actuarial teams with pricing model development
- Prepare pricing analyses and rate justification documentation
- Conduct competitor and market research for pricing strategy
- Maintain pricing databases and analytical tools
- Present findings and recommendations to management
