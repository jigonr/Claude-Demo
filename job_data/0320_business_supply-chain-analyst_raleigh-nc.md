## About Citigroup

Citigroup is a leading global financial services company serving millions of customers. We're committed to operational excellence and strategic efficiency.

## About the Role

As a Supply Chain Analyst at Citigroup Raleigh, you'll optimize our procurement and logistics operations. Working hybrid, you'll analyze data, identify efficiencies, and support vendor management. This role combines financial acumen with operational problem-solving.

## Responsibilities

- Analyze supply chain metrics and KPIs; identify opportunities for cost reduction and efficiency gains
- Support procurement processes; evaluate vendor performance and manage supplier relationships
- Prepare reports and dashboards on supply chain performance for management review
- Collaborate with procurement and operations teams to optimize sourcing strategies
- Track inventory levels and demand forecasting; make recommendations to reduce excess inventory
