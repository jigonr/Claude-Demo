## About FedEx
FedEx operates the world's largest express delivery network, shipping to 220+ countries with cutting-edge logistics technology.

## About the Role
We're seeking a Credit Analyst to join our Logistics team in Des Moines. This role offers the opportunity to contribute meaningfully to our mission while developing your professional skills in a collaborative environment. You'll work with talented colleagues dedicated to excellence and innovation in our field.

## Responsibilities
- Evaluate creditworthiness of corporate and commercial borrowers
- Analyze financial statements and assess credit risk
- Prepare credit reports and recommendations for loan decisions
- Monitor existing credit relationships and flag early warning indicators
- Support loan portfolio management and risk mitigation
- Maintain accurate credit documentation and compliance files

## Requirements
- **Education:** Bachelor's degree in Finance, Accounting, or Business
- **Experience:** 2-4 years in credit analysis, commercial lending, or financial analysis
- **Skills:** Financial analysis, Credit modeling, Regulatory knowledge, Excel proficiency, Risk assessment
- **Certifications:** CFA or accounting designation preferred
