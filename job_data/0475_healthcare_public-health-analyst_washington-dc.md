# Public Health Analyst

**Broad Institute** | Healthcare | Washington, DC | $57,000–$74,000 USD/year | Full-time | Posted Feb 5, 2026

## About Broad Institute

The Broad Institute of MIT and Harvard is a premier biomedical research institution dedicated to improving human health through science. We combine resources and expertise from both institutions to tackle the world's most pressing health challenges.

## About the Role

As a Public Health Analyst at Broad Institute, you'll support research that directly impacts disease prevention and population health. Work on-site in our Washington, DC office alongside world-leading researchers, contributing to studies that inform public health policy and improve health outcomes globally.

## Responsibilities

- Analyze epidemiological data and public health surveillance information
- Prepare research reports and presentations on disease trends and prevention strategies
- Collaborate with researchers on study design and data collection protocols
- Manage databases and ensure data quality and integrity in research projects
- Support grant writing and research proposal development
- Communicate findings to stakeholders including public health agencies and policymakers

## Requirements

**Education**
Master's degree in Public Health, Epidemiology, or related field preferred; Bachelor's required

**Experience**
2–4 years in epidemiological research, public health, or health data analysis

**Skills**
- Data analysis and statistical software (R, Python, or SAS)
- Epidemiological research methods and study design
- Knowledge of infectious and chronic disease epidemiology
- Excellent written and presentation skills
- Database management and data quality assurance
- Ability to work collaboratively in research environments

**Certifications**
CHES (Certified Health Education Specialist) preferred
