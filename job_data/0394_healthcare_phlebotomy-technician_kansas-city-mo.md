# Phlebotomy Technician

**UCSF Health** | Healthcare | Kansas City, MO | $27,000–$35,000 USD/year | Full-time | Posted Feb 23, 2026

## About UCSF Health

UCSF Health is one of the nation's leading academic medical centers, dedicated to providing exceptional patient care, advancing medical research, and training the next generation of healthcare professionals.

## About the Role

As a Phlebotomy Technician at UCSF Health's Kansas City location, you'll provide essential blood draw services that enable patient care and diagnosis. Work on-site in a leading medical center where your skills support life-saving medical care.

## Responsibilities

- Draw blood specimens from patients following proper collection procedures
- Ensure patient safety and comfort during blood draws
- Label and process blood samples for laboratory testing
- Maintain proper infection control and safety protocols
- Document collection information in patient records
- Handle and transport specimens according to protocols

## Requirements

**Education**
High School diploma or GED; Phlebotomy certification course completion

**Experience**
0–1 year in healthcare, patient services, or related roles

**Skills**
- Phlebotomy techniques and blood draw procedures
- Patient communication and empathy
- Attention to detail and accuracy
- Knowledge of medical terminology
- Infection control and safety practices
- Ability to work in fast-paced healthcare environment

**Certifications**
Phlebotomy Technician certification (CPT or equivalent) required or within 6 months
