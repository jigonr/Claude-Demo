# Physical Therapy Aide

**NIH** | Government/Research | Phoenix, AZ | $25,000–$34,000/year | Full-time | Posted Jan 4, 2026

## About NIH

The National Institutes of Health is the leading federal medical research agency. Our researchers and clinicians advance breakthrough treatments and understand disease mechanisms that improve health worldwide.

## About the Role

As a Physical Therapy Aide at NIH Phoenix, you'll support patients in their rehabilitation and recovery. You'll work directly under licensed physical therapists, assisting with treatment sessions and maintaining the therapeutic environment. This onsite position offers stable federal employment and exposure to cutting-edge healthcare research.

## Responsibilities

- Assist physical therapists in patient treatment sessions, supporting exercises and rehabilitation activities
- Prepare and maintain treatment rooms and equipment; ensure a safe, clean clinical environment
- Document patient progress and treatment sessions in electronic medical records
- Help patients with mobility and daily living activities as directed by supervising therapists
- Maintain inventory of supplies and report equipment needs to supervisory staff
