# Patient Services Representative

**Company:** Scripps Research | **Category:** Healthcare | **Location:** Milwaukee, WI | **Salary:** $27,000–$35,000 | **Type:** Full-Time | **Posted:** 2026-02-13

## About Scripps Research

Scripps Research is one of the world's leading independent, nonprofit biomedical research organizations. We advance science to solve pressing health problems including cancer, infectious diseases, neurological disorders, and metabolic conditions.

## About the Role

As a Patient Services Representative at Scripps Research, you'll be the first contact for research participants and play a vital role in the success of our clinical trials. You'll coordinate study visits, provide support to participants, and ensure they have excellent experiences while contributing to groundbreaking research.

## Responsibilities

- Schedule and coordinate patient appointments and clinical visits
- Provide patient education and answer questions about research protocols
- Collect consent forms and maintain patient documentation
- Assist with screening activities and data collection
- Support patient retention and follow-up communication

## Requirements

**Education:** High school diploma or GED; some college or healthcare experience preferred

**Experience:** 0-1 years of patient services or healthcare support experience

**Skills:**
- Strong interpersonal and communication abilities
- Excellent organizational and customer service skills
- Basic computer proficiency
- Ability to maintain patient confidentiality
- Flexibility and ability to work with diverse populations

**Certifications:** None required

- Paid time off and paid holidays
