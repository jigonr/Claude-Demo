# Compliance Analyst

**Bank of America** | Business | Kansas City, MO | $46,000–$61,000/year | Full-Time | Posted Mar 11, 2026

## About Bank of America

Bank of America is one of the world's leading financial institutions, serving millions of consumer, small business, and commercial banking customers. We operate under strict regulatory oversight and are committed to maintaining the highest standards of compliance and risk management.

## About the Role

As a Compliance Analyst in our Kansas City operations center, you'll be a frontline guardian of regulatory integrity. You'll monitor transactions, flag suspicious activity, and ensure Bank of America remains in compliance with complex federal and state regulations. This onsite role offers stability, clear career progression, and the knowledge that your work protects millions of customers.

## Responsibilities

- Monitor financial transactions and flag suspicious activity for investigation
- Review customer accounts and documentation for compliance with regulatory requirements
- Prepare compliance reports and audit materials for internal and external regulators
- Maintain transaction monitoring systems and update compliance procedures
- Coordinate with multiple departments to address compliance issues
- Participate in regulatory examinations and respond to audit findings

## Requirements

**Education**
Bachelor's degree in Finance, Accounting, Business, or related field

**Experience**
1–3 years in banking compliance, risk management, or audit roles

**Skills**
- Understanding of banking compliance and Anti-Money Laundering (AML) regulations
- Attention to detail and accuracy
- Data analysis and reporting
- Proficiency in SQL or financial systems
- Communication and documentation skills
- Ability to handle sensitive information confidentially
