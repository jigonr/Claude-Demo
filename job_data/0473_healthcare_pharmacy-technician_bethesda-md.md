## About Syneos Health
Syneos Health is a fully integrated biopharmaceutical services company supporting clinical research and patient care innovations.

## About the Role
We're seeking a Pharmacy Technician to join our Clinical Research team in Bethesda. This role offers the opportunity to contribute meaningfully to our mission while developing your professional skills in a collaborative environment. You'll work with talented colleagues dedicated to excellence and innovation in our field.

## Responsibilities
- Prepare and dispense medications under supervision of licensed pharmacist
- Maintain pharmacy inventory and track medication usage
- Process prescription orders and insurance claims
- Ensure accurate labeling and patient safety in medication dispensing
- Maintain clean, organized pharmacy workspace and equipment
- Support clinical trial medication management and documentation

## Requirements
- **Education:** High school diploma or GED; pharmacy technician certification required
- **Experience:** 1-2 years of pharmacy or medication handling experience
- **Skills:** Pharmacy operations, Attention to detail, Customer service, Medication knowledge, HIPAA compliance
- **Certifications:** Certified Pharmacy Technician (CPhT), BLS/CPR
