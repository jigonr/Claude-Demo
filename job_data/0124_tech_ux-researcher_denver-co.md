# UX Researcher

**Company:** Amplitude | **Category:** Tech | **Location:** Denver, CO | **Salary:** $68,000–$89,000 | **Type:** Full-Time | **Posted:** 2026-01-07

## About Amplitude

Amplitude is the leading product intelligence platform, helping digital businesses drive growth through data-driven product development. Our platform enables companies to understand user behavior at scale and build products people love.

## About the Role

As a UX Researcher at Amplitude, you'll be responsible for understanding our users and informing product strategy through research and data analysis. Working remotely, you'll collaborate with product, design, and engineering teams to generate insights that drive product improvements and innovation.

## Responsibilities

- Design and conduct user research studies including interviews, surveys, and usability testing
- Analyze user behavior data and behavioral metrics from analytics platforms
- Synthesize research findings and create actionable recommendations for product teams
- Present research insights to stakeholders and influence product decisions
- Maintain research documentation and build a repository of user insights

## Requirements

**Education:** Bachelor's degree in Psychology, HCI, Design, or related field

**Experience:** 2-3 years of user research, product research, or UX design experience

**Skills:**
- User research methodologies and design thinking
- Proficiency with research tools (UserTesting, Qualtrics, or similar)
- Data analysis and interpretation abilities
- Strong presentation and communication skills
- Familiarity with analytics platforms and product thinking

**Certifications:** None required
