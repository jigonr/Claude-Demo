## About FedEx

FedEx is the world's largest logistics company, connecting 220+ countries and territories. We operate complex supply chains for global enterprises, requiring sophisticated financial and tax expertise. Our tax team is essential to supporting compliance and strategic planning at scale.

## About the Role

As a Tax Analyst working remotely from Boulder, you'll navigate the complexities of multistate and federal tax for one of the world's largest companies. You'll research regulations, prepare filings, and analyze tax implications—all while supporting FedEx's logistics operations across the nation. This role builds deep tax expertise in a dynamic, mission-critical function.

## Responsibilities

- Research and analyze federal and state tax regulations relevant to logistics
- Prepare corporate income tax returns and supporting schedules
- Conduct tax provision analysis and estimate quarterly tax payments
- Support tax planning initiatives and identify optimization opportunities
- Maintain tax compliance calendars and coordinate filing deadlines
- Collaborate with external tax advisors and accounting firms
