# Health Information Technician

**Company:** Takeda | **Category:** Healthcare | **Location:** St. Louis, MO (Hybrid) | **Salary:** $32,000-$42,000/year | **Type:** Full-time | **Posted:** Feb 10, 2026

## About Takeda

Takeda is a global biopharmaceutical company driven by the mission to create better health by advancing life-changing medicines. We're committed to research, development, and delivery of innovative treatments across multiple therapeutic areas.

## About the Role

You'll join our healthcare operations team managing critical patient health records and information systems. As a Health Information Technician, you'll ensure data accuracy, security, and compliance—work that directly supports our ability to deliver quality healthcare services. This hybrid role offers valuable experience in healthcare data management.

## Responsibilities

- Manage patient health records and maintain data accuracy and completeness
- Organize and file health information using electronic and paper systems
- Verify insurance information and process authorization requests
- Ensure compliance with HIPAA regulations and privacy standards
- Respond to records requests from authorized healthcare providers

## Requirements

**Education:** High school diploma or equivalent; HIT certification preferred

**Experience:** 0-2 years

**Certifications:** RHIT or RHIA (preferred)

**Skills:**
- Medical terminology and health record systems
- Electronic health record (EHR) software
- HIPAA and privacy law knowledge
- Attention to detail and organizational skills
- Ability to maintain patient confidentiality
