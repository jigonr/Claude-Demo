# Medical Assistant

**Company:** IQVIA
**Category:** Healthcare
**Location:** Los Angeles, CA, US (Onsite)
**Salary:** $38,000 - $50,000/year
**Employment Type:** Full-time
**Posted:** 2026-01-16

## About IQVIA
IQVIA is the global leader in clinical research services, managing thousands of trials across therapeutic areas.

## About the Role
You'll provide direct patient care in our clinical research centers. You will develop expertise in critical areas while working alongside experienced professionals who are committed to your growth.

## Responsibilities
- Take vital signs and assist with patient examinations
- Prepare examination rooms and sterilize instruments
- Record patient medical histories and symptoms
- Support specimen collection for laboratory testing
- Schedule appointments and manage patient flow
- Maintain patient confidentiality and HIPAA compliance

## Requirements

- **Education:** High school diploma or equivalent; medical assistant certification preferred
- **Experience:** 0-2 years of medical assistant or healthcare experience
- **Skills:** Patient care, Vital signs, Medical terminology, EHR, Clinical procedures
- **Certifications:** CMA or RMA certification (preferred), CPR/BLS

## Benefits
- Comprehensive health insurance (medical, dental, vision)
- 401(k) retirement plan with company match
- Paid time off (18-22 days annually)
- Professional development and continuing education support
- Employee assistance program (EAP) with mental health services
