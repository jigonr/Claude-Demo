## About Abbott Laboratories
Abbott is a diversified global healthcare company with leading diagnostics, medical devices, and nutrition franchises serving patients worldwide.

## About the Role
We're seeking a Phlebotomy Technician to join our Medical Devices team in Irvine. This role offers the opportunity to contribute meaningfully to our mission while developing your professional skills in a collaborative environment. You'll work with talented colleagues dedicated to excellence and innovation in our field.

## Responsibilities
- Perform blood draws using proper phlebotomy techniques and infection control standards
- Ensure specimen collection, labeling, and handling comply with laboratory standards
- Interact professionally with patients to ease discomfort and address concerns
- Maintain accurate records of specimens and testing results
- Maintain equipment, supplies, and clean work environment
- Follow OSHA and CLIA regulations for bloodborne pathogen safety

## Requirements
- **Education:** High school diploma or GED; phlebotomy certification required
- **Experience:** 0-2 years of phlebotomy or laboratory specimen collection experience
- **Skills:** Venipuncture techniques, Specimen handling, Patient communication, Attention to detail, Manual dexterity
- **Certifications:** Phlebotomy Technician Certification (ASCP or AABB), BLS/CPR
