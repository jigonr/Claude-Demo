# Accounting Associate

**Company:** Robert Half | **Category:** Business | **Location:** Miami, FL | **Salary:** $50,000–$65,000 | **Type:** Full-Time | **Posted:** 2026-01-11

## About Robert Half

Robert Half is the world's first and largest staffing firm, specializing in professional placements across accounting, finance, law, and technology. We've spent decades matching exceptional talent with companies looking for quality professionals.

## About the Role

This is a direct-hire Accounting Associate position with a stable, growing firm in Miami. You'll gain comprehensive accounting experience across multiple functions, building a strong foundation in financial reporting and accounting operations. You'll be part of a collaborative team that values accuracy, professional standards, and continuous improvement.

## Responsibilities

- Post and reconcile journal entries in the general ledger; investigate and resolve discrepancies
- Support accounts payable and accounts receivable functions, including invoice processing and payment reconciliation
- Prepare and maintain account reconciliations for balance sheet accounts
- Assist with month-end and year-end close procedures, including accrual entries and adjustments
- Prepare financial statements and supporting schedules under supervision of the accounting manager

## Requirements

**Education:** Bachelor's degree in Accounting or Finance

**Experience:** 1-3 years of accounting experience

**Skills:**
- Proficiency in accounting software (QuickBooks, NetSuite preferred)
- Strong Excel skills for financial analysis and reporting
- Solid understanding of accounting principles and GAAP
- Excellent attention to detail and organizational abilities
- Clear communication and ability to work cross-functionally

**Certifications:** None required
