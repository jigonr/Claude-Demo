# Help Desk Technician

**Render** | Tech | Baltimore, MD (Remote) | $42,000–$55,000/year | Full-Time | Posted Mar 1, 2026

## About Render

Render is a modern cloud platform that makes it easy for developers to build, deploy, and scale web applications. We're simplifying cloud development for teams of all sizes, replacing legacy solutions with intuitive, powerful infrastructure.

## About the Role

As a Help Desk Technician at Render, you'll be the first point of contact for developers using our platform. Working fully remote, you'll resolve technical issues, provide guidance, and directly impact customer success. This entry-level tech role offers excellent learning opportunities and direct interaction with our developer community.

## Responsibilities

- Respond to and resolve customer support tickets and inquiries
- Troubleshoot platform issues and provide technical guidance
- Escalate complex issues to engineering teams appropriately
- Document resolutions and contribute to knowledge base
- Follow up with customers to ensure satisfaction
- Identify patterns and suggest feature improvements

## Requirements

**Education**
High school diploma or GED; some college preferred

**Experience**
0–1 years in customer service or technical support roles

**Skills**
- Customer service and communication skills
- Troubleshooting and problem-solving
- Basic technical knowledge (web applications, APIs, cloud concepts)
- Documentation and organization skills
- Patience and empathy with customers
- Willingness to learn and grow technically
