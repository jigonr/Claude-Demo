## About Roche
Roche is a global healthcare leader in diagnostics and pharmaceuticals. We're committed to advancing personalized healthcare across all therapeutic areas.

## About the Role
We're seeking a Health Information Technician to join our Pharmaceuticals team in Detroit. This role offers the opportunity to contribute meaningfully to our mission while developing your professional skills in a collaborative environment. You'll work with talented colleagues dedicated to excellence and innovation in our field.

## Responsibilities
- Manage and maintain accurate electronic health records
- Code diagnoses and procedures for billing and compliance purposes
- Ensure data quality and completeness in health information systems
- Release medical records in compliance with privacy regulations
- Assist with audit activities and regulatory compliance
- Support health information system implementation and training

## Requirements
- **Education:** High school diploma; associate's degree in health information technology preferred
- **Experience:** 1-2 years in health information management or medical coding
- **Skills:** Medical coding knowledge, EHR systems, Attention to detail, Regulatory compliance, Communication
- **Certifications:** Registered Health Information Technician (RHIT), HIPAA compliance knowledge
