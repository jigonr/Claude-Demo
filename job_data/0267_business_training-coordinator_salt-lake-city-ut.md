## About UPS
UPS is the global leader in package logistics, delivering to 220+ countries with 5.4M employees. We move 24M packages daily.

## About the Role
We're seeking a Training Coordinator to join our Logistics team in Salt Lake City. This role offers the opportunity to contribute meaningfully to our mission while developing your professional skills in a collaborative environment. You'll work with talented colleagues dedicated to excellence and innovation in our field.

## Responsibilities
- Develop and deliver training programs for frontline and management staff
- Coordinate training schedules and manage instructor resources across multiple locations
- Create training materials, documentation, and assessment tools
- Track training completion and performance metrics for compliance reporting
- Evaluate training effectiveness and iterate on program curriculum
- Collaborate with department heads to identify skills gaps and training needs

## Requirements
- **Education:** Bachelor's degree in Human Resources, Education, or Business
- **Experience:** 2-4 years in training, HR operations, or organizational development
- **Skills:** Instructional design, Training delivery, Project management, LMS systems, Communication
- **Certifications:** SHRM-CP or equivalent HR credential preferred
