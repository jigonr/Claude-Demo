# Patient Services Representative

**AbbVie** | Healthcare | Remote | $28,000–$37,000/year | Full-time | Posted Mar 25, 2026

## About AbbVie

AbbVie is a research-driven biopharmaceutical company focused on developing treatments that address serious health conditions. With deep expertise across immunology, oncology, virology, and neuroscience, we're committed to making meaningful differences in patients' lives.

## About the Role

As a Patient Services Representative, you'll be the voice of compassion and support for patients navigating their treatment journeys. Working remotely from Richmond, you'll help patients understand their medications, access assistance programs, and find resources to support their health. Your empathy and dedication directly impact patient outcomes and satisfaction.

## Responsibilities

- Answer patient inquiries about medications, side effects, and treatment options via phone, email, and secure messaging
- Provide education materials and connect patients with copay assistance programs and support services
- Document patient interactions accurately in our CRM system and escalate complex cases appropriately
- Maintain confidentiality and HIPAA compliance in all patient communications
- Collaborate with clinical teams to stay updated on treatment guidelines and patient support programs
