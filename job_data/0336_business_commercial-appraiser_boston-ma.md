## About Vanguard
Vanguard manages $8 trillion in global assets, serving millions of investors with a client-first philosophy since 1975.

## About the Role
We're seeking a Commercial Appraiser to join our Asset Management team in Boston. This role offers the opportunity to contribute meaningfully to our mission while developing your professional skills in a collaborative environment. You'll work with talented colleagues dedicated to excellence and innovation in our field.

## Responsibilities
- Conduct comprehensive valuations of commercial real estate properties
- Analyze comparable property sales and market conditions to determine fair value
- Prepare detailed appraisal reports and supporting documentation
- Interpret financial statements and property operating statements
- Stay current on market trends and property valuation methodologies
- Maintain professional credentials and comply with appraisal standards

## Requirements
- **Education:** Bachelor's degree in Real Estate, Finance, or related field
- **Experience:** 3-5 years of commercial real estate appraisal experience
- **Skills:** Commercial real estate analysis, Financial modeling, Market research, Appraisal software, Attention to detail
- **Certifications:** State-licensed Commercial Real Estate Appraiser, MAI credential preferred
