# Clinical Laboratory Technician

**Company:** Providence Health | **Category:** Healthcare | **Location:** San Francisco, CA (Hybrid) | **Salary:** $62,000-$81,000/year | **Type:** Full-time | **Posted:** Feb 11, 2026

## About Providence Health

Providence Health is a leading not-for-profit health system serving the Pacific Northwest and California, dedicated to advancing health and healing for all. We combine medical expertise with compassion in serving our communities.

## About the Role

You'll work in our clinical laboratory, performing tests that directly inform patient care and treatment decisions. As a Clinical Laboratory Technician, you'll operate sophisticated equipment, ensure accuracy, and maintain the high standards that healthcare depends on. This hybrid San Francisco role offers meaningful healthcare work with flexible scheduling.

## Responsibilities

- Collect and process laboratory specimens following proper protocols
- Operate and maintain laboratory analyzers and equipment
- Perform hematology, chemistry, and immunology testing
- Ensure quality control and accuracy of test results
- Maintain detailed laboratory records and documentation

## Requirements

**Education:** Associate's degree in Clinical Laboratory Science or Medical Technology

**Experience:** 0-2 years

**Certifications:** CLT or MT certification

**Skills:**
- Clinical laboratory testing procedures
- Laboratory equipment operation and maintenance
- Strong attention to detail and accuracy
- HIPAA compliance and patient confidentiality
- Ability to work in fast-paced environment
