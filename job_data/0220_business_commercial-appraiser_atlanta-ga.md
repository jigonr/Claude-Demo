**Company:** Johnson & Johnson | **Category:** Business | **Location:** Atlanta, GA (Remote) | **Salary:** $45,000-$58,000/year | **Type:** Full-time | **Posted:** Feb 03, 2026

## About Johnson & Johnson

Johnson & Johnson is a diversified healthcare company composed of three main segments: Consumer Health, Pharmaceuticals, and Medical Devices. We're dedicated to bringing innovation and quality healthcare solutions to people worldwide.

## About the Role

You'll assess property values that support J&J's global real estate portfolio. As a Commercial Appraiser, you'll conduct valuations, analyze market conditions, and prepare reports that guide real estate decisions. This remote role offers flexibility while contributing to property portfolio management.

## Responsibilities

- Conduct commercial property appraisals following industry standards
- Research market conditions and comparable property sales
- Analyze property characteristics and prepare valuation reports
- Maintain current knowledge of real estate laws and regulations
- Document findings and support property transactions

## Requirements

**Education:** Bachelor's degree in Business, Finance, or Real Estate

**Experience:** 0-2 years

**Certifications:** State appraiser license (or eligible)

**Skills:**
- Real estate valuation methods
- Commercial property analysis
- Strong research and analytical skills
- Attention to detail and accuracy
- Proficiency with appraisal software
