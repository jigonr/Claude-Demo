**Shopify** | Tech | Cambridge, MA | $97,000–$123,000/year | Full-time | Posted Feb 5, 2026

## About Shopify

Shopify is the leading cloud commerce platform that makes it easy for entrepreneurs to build their businesses online. With 1M+ merchants using our platform globally, we're reinventing commerce one build at a time.

## About the Role

As an Associate Software Engineer, you'll be at the center of commerce innovation. You'll contribute to the systems that power store operations, from checkout to inventory management. This role pairs meaningful work on high-impact problems with structured support for your growth. You'll learn from experienced engineers, ship features, and grow into a senior role over time.

## Responsibilities

- Build and ship features across our commerce platform using Ruby, JavaScript, or Python, following our engineering standards
- Contribute to system design discussions and help improve our architecture for millions of concurrent users
- Participate in on-call rotation to support production services and resolve incidents with urgency
- Work with product managers and designers to understand requirements and deliver customer-focused solutions
- Mentor newer engineers and contribute to team knowledge-sharing through code reviews and technical discussions
