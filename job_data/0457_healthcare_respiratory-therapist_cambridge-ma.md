## About Gilead Sciences

Gilead Sciences is a biopharmaceutical company dedicated to discovering, developing, and commercializing medicines to address serious diseases including respiratory conditions, infectious diseases, and oncology. Our Cambridge research campus serves as a hub for clinical trial operations and translational research.

## About the Role

We're looking for a Respiratory Therapist to join our clinical research operations team. You'll work directly with patient cohorts enrolled in our Phase II/III trials, administering evidence-based respiratory interventions and monitoring patient outcomes. This role bridges clinical practice and research methodology, requiring both technical expertise and meticulous attention to protocol adherence.

## Responsibilities

- Administer pulmonary function tests and interpret baseline/follow-up measurements with clinical accuracy
- Monitor patient vital signs and oxygen saturation during study procedures, documenting all findings in electronic case report forms
- Educate trial participants on proper inhaler technique and medication adherence protocols
- Collaborate with principal investigators to identify safety concerns and protocol deviations
- Maintain comprehensive records of patient interactions and maintain strict study participant confidentiality
- Train and supervise junior clinical staff on respiratory assessment procedures

## Requirements

**Education**
Associate's degree in Respiratory Care from an accredited program; current ARDS credential or equivalent required

**Experience**
2-4 years of respiratory care experience, preferably in a clinical trial or research setting

**Skills**
- Pulmonary function testing and interpretation
- Electronic health record management
- Patient education and communication
- Protocol compliance and documentation
- Clinical trial experience preferred

**Certifications**
- ARDS (American Respiratory Care Society)
- BLS/CPR certification current
