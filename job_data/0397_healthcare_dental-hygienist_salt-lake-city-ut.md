# Dental Hygienist

**Company:** CommonSpirit Health | **Category:** Healthcare | **Location:** Salt Lake City, UT (Remote) | **Salary:** $52,000-$68,000/year | **Type:** Full-time | **Posted:** Jan 08, 2026

## About CommonSpirit Health

CommonSpirit Health is a nonprofit health system serving diverse communities with compassionate care and healing. We're committed to advancing health equity and providing accessible, high-quality healthcare services.

## About the Role

You'll provide essential preventive oral healthcare services that set the foundation for patient wellbeing. As a Dental Hygienist, you'll perform cleanings, take radiographs, and educate patients on oral health. This remote role allows flexibility while contributing to preventive healthcare that makes a real difference in patients' lives.

## Responsibilities

- Perform prophylactic cleaning and scaling on patient teeth
- Take and develop dental radiographs and intraoral images
- Screen patients for oral cancer and other pathology
- Apply protective agents like fluoride and sealants
- Educate patients on proper oral hygiene and prevention

## Requirements

**Education:** Associate's or Bachelor's degree in Dental Hygiene

**Experience:** 0-2 years

**Certifications:** RDH license (state-specific)

**Skills:**
- Dental hygiene procedures and techniques
- Patient communication and education
- Radiography operation and safety
- HIPAA compliance and patient confidentiality
- Strong manual dexterity
