# Sterile Processing Technician

**Moderna** | Biotechnology | Research Triangle Park, NC | $33,000–$42,000/year | Full-time | Posted Jan 7, 2026

## About Moderna

Moderna is a biotechnology company pioneering a new approach to medicine by using mRNA to instruct cells to make therapeutic proteins. Located in the Research Triangle, we're advancing breakthroughs in oncology, immunology, and rare disease.

## About the Role

As a Sterile Processing Technician at Moderna, you'll ensure the sterilization and maintenance of instruments critical to our research and clinical operations. Your meticulous work supports the precision that biotech research demands. This hybrid role provides flexibility while maintaining hands-on involvement in critical laboratory operations.

## Responsibilities

- Decontaminate, process, and sterilize surgical instruments and medical equipment using various sterilization methods
- Inspect instruments for damage and functionality; perform preventive maintenance and repairs as needed
- Organize and maintain inventory of sterile supplies and instruments; track usage and reorder as required
- Maintain detailed records of sterilization cycles and equipment maintenance
- Ensure compliance with CDC guidelines and laboratory safety standards

## Requirements

**Education**
High school diploma or GED; completion of sterile processing technician program

**Experience**
1-2 years of sterile processing or instrument sterilization experience

**Skills**
- Sterilization techniques and protocols
- Equipment operation and maintenance
- Attention to detail
- Inventory management
- Data documentation

**Certifications**
Certified Sterile Processing Technician (CSPT, preferred)
