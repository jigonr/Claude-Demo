## About Charles Schwab

Charles Schwab is a leading investment and wealth management company serving millions of clients. Our San Jose headquarters is the center of our technology and operations infrastructure.

## About the Role

As a Compliance Analyst at Charles Schwab's San Jose headquarters, you'll protect client interests by ensuring strict regulatory adherence. Work on-site in this critical role, monitoring compliance and helping our organization maintain the highest standards.

## Responsibilities

- Monitor compliance with financial regulations and internal policies
- Review transactions and communications for regulatory violations
- Prepare compliance reports and documentation for regulatory bodies
- Conduct training on compliance requirements for staff
- Investigate potential compliance breaches and recommend corrective actions
- Stay current with changing regulations and industry standards

## Requirements

**Experience**
3–5 years in compliance, regulatory, or audit roles in financial services

**Skills**
- Knowledge of financial regulations (SEC, FINRA, etc.)
- Compliance monitoring and risk assessment
- Documentation and report writing skills
- Attention to detail and regulatory awareness
- Communication and training abilities
- Proficiency with compliance software systems

**Certifications**
Certified Compliance Officer (CCO) or Series 7/63 preferred
