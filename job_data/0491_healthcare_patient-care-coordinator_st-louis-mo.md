# Patient Care Coordinator

**Company:** Parexel | **Category:** Healthcare | **Location:** St. Louis, MO | **Salary:** $35,000–$46,000 | **Type:** Full-Time | **Posted:** 2026-03-10

## About Parexel

Parexel is a leading clinical research organization providing clinical trial management services to biopharmaceutical and medical device companies. We're dedicated to accelerating the development of new treatments and improving patient outcomes.

## About the Role

As a Patient Care Coordinator at Parexel, you'll play a vital role in clinical research operations. Working remotely, you'll manage patient communications, coordinate study visits, and ensure participants have excellent experiences throughout their involvement in clinical trials.

## Responsibilities

- Schedule and coordinate patient appointments and clinical study visits
- Communicate with patients via phone and email; address questions and concerns
- Maintain patient records and ensure compliance with regulatory requirements
- Support patient recruitment and retention activities
- Assist with protocol implementation and patient education

## Requirements

**Education:** High school diploma or GED; Bachelor's degree preferred

**Experience:** 1-2 years of patient care coordination or healthcare administration

**Skills:**
- Strong communication and customer service abilities
- Organization and attention to detail
- Ability to manage multiple tasks and priorities
- Basic computer proficiency and data entry
- Understanding of patient confidentiality and HIPAA

**Certifications:** None required
