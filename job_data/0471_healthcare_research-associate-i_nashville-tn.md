## About Memorial Sloan Kettering
Memorial Sloan Kettering is one of the world's most prestigious cancer centers, treating 30K+ patients annually.

## About the Role
You'll support groundbreaking cancer research as a Research Associate. You will develop expertise in critical areas while working alongside experienced professionals who are committed to your growth.

## Responsibilities
- Coordinate clinical trial activities and participant enrollment
- Collect and process biological specimens per protocol requirements
- Maintain research records and ensure regulatory compliance
- Monitor participant safety and report adverse events
- Assist with data analysis and manuscript preparation
- Support grant reporting and regulatory submissions

## Requirements

- **Education:** Bachelor's degree in a biological science, nursing, or related field
- **Experience:** 1-3 years of clinical research or laboratory experience
- **Skills:** Clinical research, Regulatory compliance, Data management, Patient communication, EHR/CTMS
- **Certifications:** GCP (Good Clinical Practice) certification
