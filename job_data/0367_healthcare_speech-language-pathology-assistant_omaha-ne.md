# Speech-Language Pathology Assistant

**Company:** Pfizer | **Category:** Healthcare | **Location:** Omaha, NE (Onsite) | **Salary:** $40,000-$52,000/year | **Type:** Full-time | **Posted:** Jan 12, 2026

## About Pfizer

Pfizer is a leading biopharmaceutical company discovering, developing, and manufacturing medicines and vaccines. We're committed to advancing patient health through innovative solutions and breakthrough discoveries.

## About the Role

You'll support patients on their path to communication health and wellness. As a Speech-Language Pathology Assistant, you'll assist licensed SLPs with therapy delivery, patient care, and documentation. This onsite Omaha role offers meaningful healthcare work helping patients improve their quality of life.

## Responsibilities

- Assist speech-language pathologists with patient assessments and treatment
- Implement therapeutic activities and exercises with patients
- Maintain accurate patient treatment records and documentation
- Prepare treatment materials and organize therapy spaces
- Support patient motivation and therapeutic goals under supervision

## Requirements

**Education:** High school diploma or equivalent; SLP assistant certificate or coursework preferred

**Experience:** 0-2 years

**Certifications:** SLPA certification (or eligible)

**Skills:**
- Patient communication and empathy
- Speech pathology terminology and basics
- Ability to follow clinical protocols
- Organizational and documentation skills
- Patience and ability to motivate patients
