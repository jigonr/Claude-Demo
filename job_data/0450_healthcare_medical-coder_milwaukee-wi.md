# Medical Coder

**Company:** Bristol-Myers Squibb | **Category:** Healthcare | **Location:** Milwaukee, WI (Hybrid) | **Salary:** $34,000-$44,000/year | **Type:** Full-time | **Posted:** Mar 22, 2026

## About Bristol-Myers Squibb

Bristol-Myers Squibb is a global biopharmaceutical company committed to developing innovative treatments for serious diseases in areas including oncology, cardiovascular disease, immunology, and fibrosis.

## About the Role

You'll join our clinical operations team as a Medical Coder, converting clinical documentation into standardized medical codes. This hybrid Milwaukee role requires precision, knowledge of coding standards, and commitment to accuracy—skills essential to healthcare operations. You'll work with experienced mentors while building a career in medical coding.

## Responsibilities

- Review medical documentation and assign appropriate diagnostic and procedural codes
- Ensure coding accuracy and compliance with ICD-10, CPT, and HCPCS coding standards
- Verify insurance coverage and resolve coding discrepancies
- Maintain detailed coding records and documentation
- Participate in coding audits and quality assurance reviews

## Requirements

**Education:** High school diploma or equivalent; medical coding program certificate preferred

**Experience:** 0-2 years

**Certifications:** CPC or RHIT certification (or eligible)

**Skills:**
- ICD-10, CPT, and HCPCS coding knowledge
- Medical terminology proficiency
- Attention to detail and accuracy
- Medical coding software and systems
- Knowledge of healthcare billing regulations
