## About the Role
We're seeking a Compliance Analyst to join our Consumer Goods team in Philadelphia. ## Responsibilities
- Monitor regulatory changes and assess impact on business operations and policies
- Conduct compliance audits and identify policy violations or risk areas
- Develop compliance training and communication materials for employees
- Investigate compliance concerns and implement corrective action plans
- Maintain compliance documentation and audit trails
- Partner with department heads to ensure ongoing compliance with regulations

- **Experience:** 2-4 years in compliance, regulatory affairs, or quality assurance
- **Skills:** Regulatory knowledge, Audit and risk assessment, Documentation, Communication, Attention to detail
- **Certifications:** Compliance certification (CCE) or legal credential preferred
