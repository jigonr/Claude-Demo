## About Cisco

At Cisco, we power the internet. Our technology connects people, secures enterprises, and automates networks across every industry and every corner of the globe — and we've been doing it for over 40 years.

## About the Role

We're looking for an IT Support Specialist to join our team at Cisco's San Jose headquarters. You'll keep our internal teams running smoothly by providing hands-on support across platforms, managing enterprise infrastructure, and solving the kinds of problems that only come up when you're operating at global scale. This is a great launchpad for someone who wants to build a career in IT — you'll learn from world-class engineers, get exposure to cutting-edge networking technology, and grow within a company that's invested in your development.

## Responsibilities

- Provide hands-on technical support for hardware, software, and network issues across Windows, macOS, and Linux environments
- Manage and administer Active Directory accounts, group policies, and access permissions for internal users
- Diagnose and resolve network connectivity issues involving TCP/IP, DNS, DHCP, and VPN configurations
- Track, prioritize, and resolve support tickets in ServiceNow, maintaining SLA targets and clear communication with end users
- Collaborate with infrastructure and security teams to deploy system updates, patches, and new endpoint configurations across the organization

## Requirements

- **Education:** Associate's or Bachelor's degree in Information Technology, Computer Science, or equivalent practical experience
- **Experience:** 0-2 years in IT support, helpdesk, or system administration
- **Skills:** Windows, macOS, Linux, Active Directory, TCP/IP, DNS, DHCP, ServiceNow, Troubleshooting
- **Certifications:** None required (CompTIA A+ or Network+ a plus)
