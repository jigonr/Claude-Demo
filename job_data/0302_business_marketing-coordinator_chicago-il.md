# Marketing Coordinator

**Company:** PepsiCo | **Category:** Business | **Location:** Chicago, IL | **Salary:** $47,000–$60,000 | **Type:** Full-Time | **Posted:** 2026-03-16

## About PepsiCo

PepsiCo is one of the world's leading food and beverage companies with a portfolio of iconic brands including Pepsi, Gatorade, Tropicana, Lay's, and Quaker. We operate in more than 200 countries and territories worldwide.

## About the Role

As a Marketing Coordinator in our Chicago office, you'll support the execution of regional marketing initiatives for our diverse brand portfolio. You'll gain hands-on marketing experience, working with experienced marketing professionals and learning how consumer-focused companies build brand awareness and drive sales.

## Responsibilities

- Coordinate marketing campaigns from concept through execution, managing timelines and deliverables
- Organize and maintain marketing asset libraries, ensuring accessibility and brand compliance
- Assist with event planning, promotional activities, and brand activation initiatives
- Track marketing metrics and compile reporting data for leadership review
- Support market research and competitive analysis activities

## Requirements

**Education:** Bachelor's degree in Marketing, Communications, or related field

**Experience:** 1-2 years of marketing, communications, or related experience

**Skills:**
- Project management and strong organizational abilities
- Proficiency with Microsoft Office and marketing software
- Strong written and verbal communication skills
- Creativity and attention to brand guidelines
- Ability to manage multiple projects simultaneously

**Certifications:** None required
