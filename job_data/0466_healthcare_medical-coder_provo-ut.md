## About the Role

As a Medical Coder, you'll ensure accurate coding of medical procedures and health records that support our healthcare operations and surgical innovation. Working onsite in Provo, you'll assign ICD-10 and CPT codes with precision, maintain regulatory compliance, and contribute to high-quality healthcare documentation. This entry-level role offers clear pathways to advance your coding career.

## Responsibilities

- Review medical records and assign appropriate ICD-10 and CPT codes
- Ensure coding accuracy and compliance with regulatory guidelines
- Maintain confidentiality and handle patient information securely
- Resolve coding discrepancies and questions with clinical staff
- Stay current on coding updates and regulatory changes
- Contribute to coding metrics and quality assurance programs
