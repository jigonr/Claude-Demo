## About PepsiCo

PepsiCo is a global leader in convenient foods and beverages serving over 200 countries.

## About the Role

Join PepsiCo's Finance, Planning & Analysis team to drive strategic decision-making through financial modeling and analysis. You'll work on forecasting, budgeting, and performance tracking.

## Responsibilities

- Develop and maintain financial models for strategic planning and forecasting
- Analyze actual performance against budget and identify variances
- Support business unit leaders with financial analysis and recommendations
- Prepare management reporting packages and executive dashboards
- Collaborate on annual budgeting and strategic planning cycles
- Conduct ad-hoc financial analysis to support business decisions
