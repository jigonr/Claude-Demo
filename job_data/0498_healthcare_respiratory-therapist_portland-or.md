# Respiratory Therapist

**Memorial Sloan Kettering** | Healthcare | Portland, OR (Remote) | $50,000–$65,000 USD/year | Full-time | Posted Mar 13, 2026

## About Memorial Sloan Kettering

Memorial Sloan Kettering is the world's premier cancer center, providing cutting-edge cancer treatment and research. We're dedicated to advancing cancer care and improving patient outcomes globally.

## About the Role

As a Respiratory Therapist supporting Memorial Sloan Kettering's cancer patients, you'll provide essential respiratory care to patients undergoing advanced cancer treatment. Work fully remote while making a profound impact on cancer patients' lives and recovery.

## Responsibilities

- Provide respiratory care and oxygen therapy to cancer patients
- Monitor patient respiratory status and vital signs
- Assist with intubation and extubation procedures
- Maintain and troubleshoot respiratory equipment
- Document patient assessments and care provided
- Collaborate with oncology teams on patient care coordination

## Requirements

**Education**
Associate's degree in Respiratory Therapy from accredited program

**Experience**
2–4 years in respiratory therapy, critical care, or oncology support

**Skills**
- Respiratory assessment and care delivery
- Mechanical ventilation management
- Cardiopulmonary monitoring
- Patient communication and compassion
- Attention to detail and safety awareness
- Ability to work in high-stress environments

**Certifications**
- RRT (Registered Respiratory Therapist) certification required
- ACLS (Advanced Cardiac Life Support) certification required
