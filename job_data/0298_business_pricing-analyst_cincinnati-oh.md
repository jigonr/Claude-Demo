# Pricing Analyst

**Company:** Nike | **Category:** Business | **Location:** Cincinnati, OH (Onsite) | **Salary:** $49,000-$63,000/year | **Type:** Full-time | **Posted:** Mar 10, 2026

## About Nike

Nike is the world's leading sports apparel and footwear company, dedicated to helping athletes achieve peak performance. Headquartered in Cincinnati, we innovate across shoes, apparel, and equipment while building a brand that transcends sport.

## About the Role

You'll join our Merchandising & Pricing team at World Headquarters in Cincinnati, playing a key role in optimizing pricing strategies that balance profitability and market competitiveness. Your analytical insights will influence pricing across Nike's global portfolio of iconic products. This onsite role offers immersion in data-driven decision-making.

## Responsibilities

- Analyze sales data, market trends, and competitor pricing to inform pricing recommendations
- Develop pricing models using statistical analysis and Excel tools
- Monitor price performance and elasticity across product categories
- Support promotional planning and margin optimization initiatives
- Prepare reports and presentations for merchandising and finance teams

**Experience:** 0-2 years

**Skills:**
- Statistical analysis and modeling
- Advanced Excel with data analysis functions
- Retail and demand understanding
- Business acumen and market awareness
- Clear communication and presentation skills
