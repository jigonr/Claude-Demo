# Claims Analyst

**Vanguard** | Business | Raleigh, NC | $42,000–$55,000/year | Full-Time (Hybrid) | Posted Jan 21, 2026

## About Vanguard

Vanguard is a global investment management company serving millions of investors worldwide. We're structured uniquely—owned by our clients—which gives us the independence to focus entirely on their interests.

## About the Role

As a Claims Analyst at Vanguard's Raleigh office, you'll investigate and resolve investor disputes with integrity and precision. Working hybrid, you'll analyze claims, research transactions, and determine fair resolutions. Your work directly supports investor confidence in our operations and commitment to service.

## Responsibilities

- Review and analyze investor claims and documented issues
- Evaluate claim merit and determine appropriate resolution
- Research client account transactions and documentation
- Coordinate with internal departments to gather information
- Document findings and recommendations clearly
- Follow up with claimants and ensure timely resolution
