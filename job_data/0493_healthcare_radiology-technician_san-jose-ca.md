## About UCSF Health

UCSF Health is a leading academic health system serving the San Francisco Bay Area with compassion and excellence. We're committed to advancing patient care, research, and medical education.

## About the Role

You'll be part of a world-class imaging team providing diagnostic services that guide treatment decisions. As a Radiology Technician, you'll operate advanced equipment and ensure patient safety while supporting critical clinical care. This hybrid San Jose role offers professional growth in academic medicine.

## Responsibilities

- Operate diagnostic imaging equipment (X-ray, CT, MRI, ultrasound)
- Prepare and position patients for imaging procedures
- Maintain equipment and perform routine quality assurance checks
- Ensure patient safety and comfort throughout procedures
- Maintain accurate imaging records and documentation

## Requirements

**Experience:** 0-2 years

**Certifications:** ARRT or eligible for certification

**Skills:**
- ARRT certification or eligible

- Patient communication and safety

- Attention to detail and precision
