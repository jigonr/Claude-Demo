## About Parexel

Parexel is a leading clinical research organization providing drug development and post-market services to the pharmaceutical, biotechnology, and device industries.

## About the Role

We're seeking a Medical Assistant to support clinical trial operations at our Milwaukee research site. You will collect vital signs, perform EKG testing, assist with blood draws, and provide patient support during study visits. Your work is essential to clinical research advancement.

## Responsibilities

- Collect patient vital signs and document measurements accurately
- Perform 12-lead EKG and submit for physician review
- Assist phlebotomists with blood collection and specimen processing
- Position patients for physical examinations and procedures
- Educate patients about study procedures and protocol requirements
- Maintain patient confidentiality and clean, safe study environment

## Requirements

**Education**
High school diploma or GED; medical assistant certificate or completion of accredited MA program preferred

**Experience**
0-2 years of medical assistant or clinical support experience

**Skills**
- Patient vital signs and phlebotomy support
- EKG operation and troubleshooting
- Medical terminology and patient care
- Attention to detail and patient safety
- Ability to work with diverse patient populations

**Certifications**
- CMA or RMA certification preferred
