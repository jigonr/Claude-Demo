# Staff Accountant

**Company:** Honeywell | **Category:** Business | **Location:** Raleigh, NC (Onsite) | **Salary:** $47,000-$61,000/year | **Type:** Full-time | **Posted:** Feb 02, 2026

## About Honeywell

Honeywell is a diversified technology and manufacturing company with a presence in aerospace, building technologies, and performance materials. We serve customers globally with innovative solutions that make the world safer and more productive.

## About the Role

You'll join our Finance team in Raleigh, managing the accuracy and integrity of our accounting records. This onsite role offers exposure to diverse business units and the opportunity to grow your accounting expertise within a Fortune 100 organization. You'll work with experienced mentors and modern accounting systems.

## Responsibilities

- Record transactions and reconcile general ledger accounts monthly
- Prepare and review financial statements and supporting schedules
- Assist with quarterly and annual close processes
- Investigate and resolve account discrepancies
- Maintain documentation and ensure compliance with accounting standards

## Requirements

**Education:** Bachelor's degree in Accounting or Finance

**Experience:** 0-2 years

**Skills:**
- GAAP accounting principles
- Excel and accounting software
- General ledger and financial reporting
- Attention to detail and analytical skills
- Ability to meet tight deadlines
