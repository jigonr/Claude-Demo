# Network Technician

**Rippling** | Tech | St. Louis, MO (Remote) | $42,000–$55,000 USD/year | Full-time | Posted Mar 20, 2026

## About Rippling

Rippling is the global workforce platform that transforms how companies manage HR, IT, and finance. We serve thousands of companies across the globe, automating the complex workflows that connect people, technology, and money.

## About the Role

As a Network Technician at Rippling, you'll play a critical role in keeping our distributed platform running smoothly. You'll support the network infrastructure that powers our product for thousands of organizations, work fully remote, and have the autonomy to tackle technical challenges that directly impact our reliability and scale.

## Responsibilities

- Monitor network infrastructure health and respond to connectivity issues and alerts
- Troubleshoot network problems using diagnostic tools and follow systematic troubleshooting procedures
- Maintain network documentation and update network diagrams and configurations
- Support network security initiatives by implementing firewalls and access controls
- Collaborate with infrastructure team on network upgrades and optimization projects
- Provide technical support to remote employees regarding connectivity and network access

## Requirements

**Education**
Associate's degree in IT or Network Administration; or equivalent hands-on experience

**Experience**
1–2 years in network administration, network support, or systems administration

**Skills**
- Knowledge of networking fundamentals (TCP/IP, DNS, DHCP, routing)
- Experience with network monitoring and diagnostic tools
- Familiarity with firewall and router configuration
- Basic Linux or Windows system administration
- Problem-solving and troubleshooting skills
- Strong communication and documentation abilities

**Certifications**
CompTIA Network+ or equivalent (preferred within 6 months)
