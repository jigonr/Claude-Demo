## About Moderna
Moderna is a clinical-stage biotechnology company pioneering mRNA therapeutics. We're creating a new class of transformative medicines.

## About the Role
We're seeking a Histotechnician to join our Biotechnology team in San Jose. This role offers the opportunity to contribute meaningfully to our mission while developing your professional skills in a collaborative environment. You'll work with talented colleagues dedicated to excellence and innovation in our field.

## Responsibilities
- Process tissue specimens for microscopic examination following standard protocols
- Operate histology equipment including microtomes, stainers, and embedding systems
- Ensure specimen quality control and maintain accurate records
- Prepare microscope slides for pathologist review
- Maintain clean, organized laboratory environment and equipment
- Support research activities and clinical sample processing

## Requirements
- **Education:** Associate's degree in Histotechnology or related field; certification preferred
- **Experience:** 1-3 years in histopathology or clinical laboratory
- **Skills:** Histology protocols, Microscopy, Laboratory equipment operation, Attention to detail, Documentation
- **Certifications:** HT (Histotechnician) or HTL (Histotechnologist) certification
