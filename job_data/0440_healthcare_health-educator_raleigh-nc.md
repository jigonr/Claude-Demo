## About PPD

PPD is a leading clinical research organization providing drug development, medical device, and diagnostics services to pharmaceutical and biotech companies.

## About the Role

We're seeking a Health Educator to develop and deliver patient education materials and training programs for clinical trial participants. You will create educational content about study procedures, disease states, and medication adherence, ensuring participants understand their role in research.

## Responsibilities

- Develop educational materials and patient guides for clinical trials
- Deliver patient education sessions via virtual and in-person formats
- Create videos and interactive content explaining study procedures
- Assess participant comprehension and learning needs
- Collaborate with physicians and coordinators on educational content
- Track participant satisfaction and education outcomes

## Requirements

**Education**
Bachelor's degree in Health Education, Public Health, Nursing, or related field

**Experience**
1-2 years of health education, patient training, or instructional design experience

**Skills**
- Health education and patient communication
- Content creation and instructional design
- Presentation and public speaking skills
- Understanding of clinical trial processes
- Basic multimedia or presentation tool proficiency

**Certifications**
- CHES certification preferred
