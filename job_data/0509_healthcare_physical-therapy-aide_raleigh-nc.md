# Physical Therapy Aide

**Company:** Mount Sinai Health | **Category:** Healthcare | **Location:** Raleigh, NC | **Salary:** $26,000 - $36,000 | **Type:** Full-Time | **Posted:** 2026-03-22

## About Mount Sinai Health

Mount Sinai Health is an integrated health system dedicated to patient care and medical research.

## About the Role

Support our physical therapy team at Mount Sinai Health, helping patients recover and regain mobility through compassionate care and attention to detail.

## Responsibilities

- Assist patients with exercises and therapeutic activities under therapist supervision
- Help patients transfer safely between equipment and positions
- Maintain clean, organized therapy spaces and equipment
- Document patient progress and communicate updates to therapists
- Provide emotional support and encouragement to patients during recovery
- Help schedule appointments and maintain patient records

## Requirements

**Education:** High school diploma or equivalent; relevant degree or certification strongly preferred

**Experience:** 0-3 years

**Skills:**
- Patient care
- Physical assistance
- Empathy
- HIPAA compliance
- Attention to detail
