# Histotechnician

**Providence Health** | Hospital System | Ann Arbor, MI | $42,000–$55,000/year | Full-time | Posted Mar 13, 2026

## About Providence Health

Providence Health is a nationally recognized nonprofit health system serving communities across the West and Midwest. We're committed to advancing health and healing through excellent patient care and collaborative practice.

## About the Role

Join Providence Health's pathology laboratory as a remote Histotechnician. Your meticulous work processing tissue specimens is foundational to accurate diagnoses that guide patient care. This fully remote position offers the flexibility to work from home while contributing to diagnostic excellence across our health system.

## Responsibilities

- Process tissue specimens using automated and manual techniques, following established protocols and quality standards
- Prepare slides through staining, mounting, and cover-slipping processes; ensure consistent slide quality
- Operate and maintain histology equipment; perform preventive maintenance and troubleshoot issues
- Track specimens through the workflow system and maintain accurate documentation
- Collaborate with laboratory supervisors to improve efficiency and maintain laboratory certifications

## Requirements

**Education**
High school diploma or GED; completion of accredited histotechnology program

**Experience**
1-3 years of histotechnology experience

**Skills**
- Tissue processing and preparation
- Staining and mounting techniques
- Laboratory equipment operation
- Data documentation
- Quality assurance

**Certifications**
Histotechnician certification (HT/HTL, ASCP)
