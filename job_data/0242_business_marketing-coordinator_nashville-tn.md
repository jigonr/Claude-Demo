## About the Role

We're seeking a Marketing Coordinator to support our Nashville facility's corporate communications and marketing initiatives. You will manage marketing campaigns, organize events, create promotional materials, and coordinate with internal stakeholders to amplify our employer brand and business communications.

- Coordinate with graphic designers and vendors for promotional materials
- Manage event planning and logistics for recruitment fairs and open houses
- Maintain marketing calendar and track campaign metrics
- Prepare marketing reports and analysis for management review
- Support social media content creation and employee engagement initiatives
