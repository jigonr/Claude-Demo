# SOC Analyst

**Company:** Monday.com | **Category:** Tech | **Location:** New York, NY (Onsite) | **Salary:** $78,000-$101,000/year | **Type:** Full-time | **Posted:** Mar 03, 2026

## About Monday.com

Monday.com is a work management SaaS platform helping millions of users organize their work and collaborate effectively. We're scaling rapidly and building a secure, reliable platform trusted by enterprises worldwide.

## About the Role

You'll be a defender of our platform and our customers' data. As a SOC Analyst, you'll monitor security events around the clock, respond to threats, and implement protections that keep our infrastructure secure. This onsite NYC role offers hands-on experience in real-world incident response and the opportunity to grow in cybersecurity.

## Responsibilities

- Monitor security alerts and investigate suspicious activities
- Respond to and contain security incidents with proper escalation
- Analyze logs and security events to identify potential threats
- Implement security best practices and hardening measures
- Document incident response procedures and maintain run books

## Requirements

**Education:** Bachelor's degree in Cybersecurity, Computer Science, or related field

**Experience:** 1-3 years

**Certifications:** Security+ or equivalent

**Skills:**
- SIEM platform experience (Splunk, ELK, or similar)
- Network security principles and firewalls
- Incident response and threat analysis
- Linux/Windows system administration basics
- Security compliance frameworks (NIST, ISO 27001)
