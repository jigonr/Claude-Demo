# Bookkeeper

**Company:** Vanguard | **Category:** Business | **Location:** Indianapolis, IN (Remote) | **Salary:** $32,000-$42,000/year | **Type:** Full-time | **Posted:** Jan 22, 2026

## About Vanguard

Vanguard is one of the world's largest investment management companies, committed to providing investors with high-quality, low-cost investment products and services. We're dedicated to putting investors first.

## About the Role

You'll maintain the financial foundation that supports our operations. As a Bookkeeper, you'll record transactions, reconcile accounts, and ensure accuracy of financial records. This remote Indianapolis role offers clear responsibilities and the opportunity to develop accounting expertise.

## Responsibilities

- Record transactions in accounting system and post to general ledger
- Reconcile bank accounts and credit card statements monthly
- Prepare financial reports and account summaries
- Process vendor invoices and expense reports
- Maintain organized financial records and documentation

## Requirements

**Education:** High school diploma or equivalent; accounting coursework preferred

**Experience:** 0-2 years

**Skills:**
- Bookkeeping and accounting principles
- Excel and accounting software
- Attention to detail and accuracy
- Organizational and time management skills
- Basic knowledge of GAAP
