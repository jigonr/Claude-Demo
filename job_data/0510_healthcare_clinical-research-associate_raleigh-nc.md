# Clinical Research Associate

**Genentech** | Healthcare | Raleigh, NC | $47,000–$61,000 USD/year | Full-time | Posted Mar 2, 2026

## About Genentech

Genentech is a leading biotechnology company dedicated to discovering and developing groundbreaking treatments for cancer and genetic diseases. Our research drives scientific innovation that transforms patient lives.

## About the Role

As a Clinical Research Associate at Genentech's Raleigh facility, you'll coordinate clinical research studies that advance novel therapies. Work hybrid supporting the research teams who are reimagining medicine.

## Responsibilities

- Coordinate clinical trial activities including patient enrollment and scheduling
- Assist with informed consent process and document collection
- Monitor patient safety and report adverse events
- Maintain regulatory documentation and trial records
- Communicate with research team and study sites
- Ensure compliance with study protocols and regulatory requirements

## Requirements

**Education**
Bachelor's degree in Life Sciences, Healthcare, or related field

**Experience**
1–2 years in clinical research, healthcare, or pharmaceutical research roles

**Skills**
- Knowledge of clinical research and GCP guidelines
- Strong organizational and detail-oriented skills
- Excellent communication and interpersonal abilities
- Proficiency with electronic case report forms (eCRF)
- Ability to manage multiple priorities
- Understanding of medical terminology and safety reporting

**Certifications**
GCP certification required or within 30 days of hire
