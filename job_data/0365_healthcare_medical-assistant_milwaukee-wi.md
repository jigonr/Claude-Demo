# Medical Assistant

**Sanofi** | Healthcare | Milwaukee, WI (Remote) | $27,000–$35,000/year | Full-Time | Posted Mar 17, 2026

## About Sanofi

Sanofi is a global pharmaceutical company discovering and delivering medicines that address humanity's greatest health challenges. We're committed to making healthcare more accessible and innovative across therapeutic areas.

## About the Role

As a Medical Assistant at Sanofi, you'll support our clinical and operational functions while working fully remote. You'll coordinate patient interactions, manage administrative tasks, and provide clinical support. This entry-level role offers excellent training in healthcare and pharmaceutical operations.

## Responsibilities

- Assist with patient intake and health history documentation
- Schedule appointments and manage patient communications
- Prepare examination rooms and sterilize medical equipment
- Record vital signs and patient information
- Support clinical staff with administrative and clinical tasks
- Maintain patient confidentiality and HIPAA compliance

## Requirements

**Education**
High school diploma or GED

**Experience**
0–1 years in healthcare, patient care, or administrative roles

**Skills**
- Patient care and communication
- Administrative and organizational skills
- Basic medical knowledge and terminology
- Attention to detail and accuracy
- Ability to multitask and manage priorities
- Understanding of HIPAA and confidentiality
