## About the Role
As an FP&A Analyst, you'll provide financial analysis driving strategic planning. You will develop expertise in critical areas while working alongside experienced professionals who are committed to your growth.

## Responsibilities
- Build financial models for strategic planning and forecasting
- Analyze actuals vs. budget and identify variances
- Support annual budgeting and quarterly reforecasting cycles
- Create financial analyses supporting investment decisions
- Present findings to CFO and management team
- Maintain financial data integrity and consolidation systems

## Requirements

- **Education:** Bachelor's degree in Finance, Accounting, or Business Administration
- **Experience:** 2-4 years of financial planning, analysis, or corporate finance experience
- **Skills:** Financial modeling, Forecasting, Variance analysis, Excel, Business acumen
- **Certifications:** CFA (preferred), FP&A certification
