## About Stripe
Stripe processes hundreds of billions in payments annually for the world's best companies. We're building the financial infrastructure for the internet.

## About the Role
We're seeking a Cloud Operations Analyst to join our Fintech team in Palo Alto. This role offers the opportunity to contribute meaningfully to our mission while developing your professional skills in a collaborative environment. You'll work with talented colleagues dedicated to excellence and innovation in our field.

## Responsibilities
- Monitor cloud infrastructure health, performance, and cost optimization across multi-cloud environments
- Analyze cloud billing and identify cost-saving opportunities without impacting performance
- Support infrastructure troubleshooting and participate in incident response
- Automate routine operational tasks using Terraform, CloudFormation, or equivalent tools
- Document system architecture, runbooks, and operational procedures
- Collaborate with development teams on deployment strategies and best practices

## Requirements
- **Education:** Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or equivalent hands-on experience
- **Experience:** 1-3 years in cloud operations, DevOps, or systems engineering roles
- **Skills:** AWS or Google Cloud Platform, Kubernetes or Docker, Infrastructure as Code, Monitoring tools (Prometheus, DataDog), Linux administration
- **Certifications:** AWS Certified Solutions Architect Associate or Google Cloud Associate Cloud Engineer
