# Research Associate I

**Company:** Roche | **Category:** Healthcare | **Location:** Bethesda, MD | **Salary:** $60,000–$78,000 | **Type:** Full-Time | **Posted:** 2026-02-15

## About Roche

Roche is a global pioneer in pharmaceuticals and diagnostics. Our mission is to enable people to live longer, healthier lives through transformative science and innovation in drug discovery and development.

## About the Role

As a Research Associate I, you'll be part of our early-stage drug discovery effort in the Bethesda region. This role provides hands-on laboratory experience contributing to projects that advance from concept to clinical candidacy. You'll work with experienced scientists and have access to world-class facilities and mentorship.

## Responsibilities

- Conduct laboratory experiments following established protocols and standard operating procedures
- Maintain detailed laboratory notebooks and prepare research summaries for documentation
- Perform data analysis and contribute to research publications and presentations
- Manage laboratory supplies, equipment, and inventory; assist with equipment maintenance
- Collaborate with cross-functional teams to support project milestones

## Requirements

**Education:** Bachelor's degree in Chemistry, Biology, Biochemistry, or related life sciences field

**Experience:** 1-2 years of research experience in an academic or industry setting

**Skills:**
- Laboratory techniques in chemistry, cell biology, or molecular biology
- Proficiency with laboratory information management systems
- Strong scientific writing and technical communication
- Attention to detail and commitment to data integrity
- Ability to work independently and as part of a team

**Certifications:** None required
