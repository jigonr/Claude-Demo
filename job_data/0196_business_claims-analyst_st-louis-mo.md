# Claims Analyst

**BlackRock** | Business | St. Louis, MO | $38,000–$49,000 USD/year | Full-time | Posted Jan 2, 2026

## About BlackRock

BlackRock is the world's largest asset manager, with clients ranging from large pension funds to individual investors. Our St. Louis office supports critical client operations and services.

## About the Role

As a Claims Analyst at BlackRock, you'll manage the claims processing that keeps our clients confident in their investments. Work hybrid at our St. Louis office, providing reliable claims support to institutional and individual clients.

## Responsibilities

- Process and review client claims submissions for eligibility and completeness
- Investigate claims discrepancies and coordinate resolutions
- Maintain detailed claims records and documentation
- Communicate with clients regarding claim status and requirements
- Assist with claims data analysis and reporting
- Support compliance with industry standards and regulations

**Skills**

- Customer service and communication skills
- Attention to detail and accuracy
- Excel and database proficiency
- Understanding of asset management or financial services (preferred)
- Ability to manage multiple priorities

**Certifications**
None required
