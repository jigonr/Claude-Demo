# Sales Operations Analyst

**Company:** State Farm | **Category:** Business | **Location:** Minneapolis, MN | **Salary:** $52,000–$68,000 | **Type:** Full-Time | **Posted:** 2026-01-13

## About State Farm

State Farm is one of the largest insurance companies in the United States. For over 100 years, we've helped customers manage risks, protect their assets, and provide for their families' futures.

## About the Role

As a Sales Operations Analyst at State Farm Minneapolis, you'll support our sales team in serving customers across our region. You'll manage systems and data that enable our agents to deliver excellent service and close sales effectively. Working onsite, you'll be part of a customer-focused organization with a strong commitment to community.

## Responsibilities

- Manage customer databases and CRM systems; ensure data accuracy and integrity
- Create sales reports and dashboards tracking pipeline, conversion, and performance metrics
- Analyze sales data to identify trends and recommend process improvements
- Coordinate sales training sessions and process documentation
- Support sales team with commission calculations and incentive program management

## Requirements

**Education:** Bachelor's degree in Business, Finance, or related field

**Experience:** 2-3 years of sales operations, business analysis, or insurance background

**Skills:**
- CRM system management (Salesforce, Microsoft Dynamics, or similar)
- Strong Excel and data analysis capabilities
- Understanding of insurance products or sales processes
- Excellent attention to detail and organizational abilities
- Clear communication and stakeholder management

**Certifications:** None required
