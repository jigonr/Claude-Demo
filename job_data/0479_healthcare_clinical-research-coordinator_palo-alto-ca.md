# Clinical Research Coordinator

**Stanford Medicine** | Academic Medicine | Palo Alto, CA | $67,000–$86,000/year | Full-time | Posted Feb 15, 2026

## About Stanford Medicine

Stanford Medicine is a world-renowned academic health system advancing human health through discovery, innovation, and excellence in patient care. Our research generates breakthroughs that define the future of medicine across cardiology, oncology, neuroscience, and more.

## About the Role

As a Clinical Research Coordinator, you'll be essential to Stanford's research mission. You'll ensure that clinical trials run smoothly and ethically, managing every detail from patient recruitment to regulatory compliance. This onsite role places you at the heart of medical discovery, working alongside physicians and researchers who are advancing treatment options for serious diseases.

## Responsibilities

- Recruit, screen, and enroll eligible patients into clinical trials, ensuring informed consent and participant safety
- Collect and manage study data, conducting quality checks and maintaining regulatory documentation
- Maintain regulatory compliance with IRB and FDA requirements; prepare and update study documentation
- Coordinate study visits, manage patient communication, and track appointments and follow-ups
- Collaborate with physicians, nurses, and research teams to execute study protocols and resolve issues

## Requirements

**Education**
Bachelor's degree in Life Sciences, Healthcare, Business, or related field

**Experience**
2-4 years of clinical research coordination or clinical trial experience

**Skills**
- Clinical trial management
- Data management and quality assurance
- Regulatory compliance (IRB, FDA)
- Electronic data capture systems
- Patient communication and enrollment

**Certifications**
ACRP or SOCRA certification (preferred)
