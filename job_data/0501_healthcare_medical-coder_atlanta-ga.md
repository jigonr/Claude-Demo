# Medical Coder

**Novartis** | Healthcare | Atlanta, GA | $38,000–$49,000 USD/year | Full-time | Posted Feb 15, 2026

## About Novartis

Novartis is a global leader in pharmaceutical innovation, committed to discovering and developing breakthrough medicines that improve patient outcomes. Our Atlanta facility supports our clinical research and development operations, where we work tirelessly to advance novel therapies.

## About the Role

As a Medical Coder at Novartis, you'll support our clinical research programs by accurately coding medical documentation from research studies. Your work directly enables the development of life-changing medications by ensuring data integrity and regulatory compliance. Work on-site in our Atlanta facility as part of a dedicated biotech team.

## Responsibilities

- Review and code patient medical records from clinical research studies using ICD-10, CPT, and study-specific coding conventions
- Verify source documents and coded data for completeness, accuracy, and regulatory compliance
- Communicate with clinical staff and researchers regarding coding inconsistencies or documentation gaps
- Maintain proficiency with study-specific coding manuals and protocol requirements
- Support quality control audits of coded data to ensure compliance with FDA and regulatory guidelines
- Track coding productivity metrics and participate in continuous improvement initiatives

## Requirements

**Education**
High School diploma with completion of medical coding or health information technology program; Associate's degree preferred

**Experience**
1–2 years in medical coding, health information, or clinical research documentation roles

**Skills**
- Knowledge of ICD-10, CPT, and HCPCS coding systems
- Understanding of medical terminology and anatomy
- Attention to detail and accuracy in documentation review
- Ability to work with electronic data capture systems
- Strong organizational skills and ability to prioritize tasks
- Commitment to regulatory compliance and quality standards

**Certifications**
AAPC CPC (Certified Professional Coder) or AHIMA RHIT certification required or attainment within 12 months
