# QA Analyst

**Company:** Snowflake | **Category:** Tech | **Location:** San Diego, CA | **Salary:** $69,000–$89,000 | **Type:** Full-Time | **Posted:** 2026-01-14

## About Snowflake

Snowflake is the leading cloud data platform, trusted by thousands of organizations to democratize data. Our platform processes massive volumes of data in the cloud, enabling analytics, data sharing, and AI applications.

## About the Role

As a QA Analyst at Snowflake San Diego, you'll play a crucial role in ensuring the reliability and performance of our platform. You'll work closely with product and engineering teams to design comprehensive testing strategies and execute quality assurance at scale. This onsite position offers the opportunity to develop expertise in testing complex distributed systems.

## Responsibilities

- Design and execute test plans covering functional, performance, and integration testing
- Create test cases and automated test scripts using Python or Java
- Execute regression testing and identify defects; track and report issues
- Collaborate with product and engineering teams on quality requirements
- Monitor test coverage and quality metrics; recommend improvements

## Requirements

**Education:** Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or related field

**Experience:** 2-4 years of QA or testing experience in software development

**Skills:**
- Test automation using Python, Java, or similar languages
- Familiarity with testing frameworks and CI/CD tools
- Strong analytical and problem-solving abilities
- SQL and database testing knowledge
- Excellent communication and documentation skills

**Certifications:** ISTQB or similar testing certification (preferred)
