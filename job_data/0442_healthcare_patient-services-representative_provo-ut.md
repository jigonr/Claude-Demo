# Patient Services Representative

**Company:** NYU Langone Health
**Category:** Healthcare

## About NYU Langone Health

NYU Langone Health is a world-class academic medical center providing patient care, research, and education through its network of hospitals and clinics.

## About the Role

We're seeking a Patient Services Representative to provide administrative support to our Provo clinic operations. You will schedule appointments, check patients in, collect insurance information, and ensure smooth patient flow while maintaining a welcoming environment.

## Responsibilities

- Schedule patient appointments and manage appointment calendar
- Check patients in and collect demographic and insurance information
- Process payments and provide financial information to patients
- Greet patients in a friendly and professional manner
- Maintain patient records and ensure HIPAA compliance
- Communicate appointment changes and provide patient resources

## Requirements

**Education**
High school diploma or GED; some college preferred

**Experience**
0-2 years of patient services, customer service, or administrative support experience

**Skills**
- Patient communication and customer service
- Scheduling software and administrative systems
- Data entry and accuracy
- Understanding of HIPAA and patient privacy
- Multitasking and time management

**Certifications**
- None required
