# Budget Analyst

**Company:** Aon | **Category:** Business | **Location:** Sacramento, CA (Hybrid) | **Salary:** $57,000-$73,000/year | **Type:** Full-time | **Posted:** Jan 20, 2026

## About Aon

Aon is a global professional services and risk management company, helping organizations manage risks and optimize outcomes. We combine human expertise with data and analytics to deliver solutions that drive growth and resilience.

## About the Role

You'll join our Finance team as a Budget Analyst, playing a key role in financial planning and oversight. You'll work closely with business leaders to develop budgets, monitor spending, and provide insights that enable smart financial decisions. This hybrid role offers solid grounding in financial planning with flexibility to work from Sacramento or remotely.

## Responsibilities

- Develop and maintain annual operating budgets with departmental input
- Prepare quarterly forecasts and variance analysis against actual spending
- Support financial planning processes and provide spending guidance
- Investigate budget variances and recommend corrective actions
- Prepare budget reports and presentations for leadership review

## Requirements

**Education:** Bachelor's degree in Finance, Accounting, or Business

**Experience:** 1-2 years

**Skills:**
- Budget development and financial planning
- Advanced Excel modeling
- Financial analysis and variance reporting
- Attention to detail and organizational skills
- Communication across organizational levels
