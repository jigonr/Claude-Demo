## About Pfizer
Pfizer is one of the world's largest biopharmaceutical companies, developing medicines that save and improve lives across therapeutic areas.

## About the Role
We're seeking a Nursing Aide to join our Pharmaceuticals team in Philadelphia. This role offers the opportunity to contribute meaningfully to our mission while developing your professional skills in a collaborative environment. You'll work with talented colleagues dedicated to excellence and innovation in our field.

## Responsibilities
- Provide direct patient care including hygiene, toileting, and mobility assistance
- Monitor and report patient condition changes to nursing staff
- Maintain clean, safe patient environments and equipment
- Take vital signs and document findings in electronic health records
- Support nursing staff with clinical procedures and patient comfort measures
- Communicate compassionately with patients and families

## Requirements
- **Education:** High school diploma; Nursing Aide certification (CNA) required
- **Experience:** 0-2 years of patient care or healthcare support experience
- **Skills:** Patient care, Communication, Attention to detail, Empathy, Physical stamina
- **Certifications:** Certified Nursing Assistant (CNA), BLS/CPR
