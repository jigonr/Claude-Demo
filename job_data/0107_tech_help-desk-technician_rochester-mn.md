# Help Desk Technician

**Akamai** | Tech | Remote | $37,000–$49,000/year | Full-time | Posted Feb 21, 2026

## About Akamai

Akamai delivers the world's content, applications, and experiences closer to users. We're the global leader in content delivery and edge computing infrastructure.

## About the Role

As a Help Desk Technician at Akamai, you'll be the IT support backbone for our global organization. Working fully remote from Rochester, you'll troubleshoot technical issues, provide excellent customer support, and help employees stay productive. This entry-level IT role offers clear advancement into systems administration or specialized support roles.

## Responsibilities

- Respond to help desk tickets via email, chat, and phone; resolve technical issues efficiently
- Troubleshoot hardware, software, and network problems; escalate complex issues appropriately
- Provide remote support and remote access to assist users; maintain documentation of solutions
- Install software and updates; manage employee hardware provisioning and deprovisioning
- Track tickets and metrics; contribute to team knowledge base and documentation
