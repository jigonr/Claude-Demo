Accenture is a global professional services company providing strategy, consulting, digital, and technology services to organizations worldwide.

## About the Role

As a Business Analyst I at Accenture, you'll support client transformation projects across industries. Working remotely, you'll gather requirements, analyze processes, and recommend improvements. This role offers exposure to diverse business challenges and clear pathways into consulting.

- Gather and document client business requirements; conduct interviews and workshops

- Prepare requirements documentation and process flow diagrams for development teams
- Support solution design and implementation; communicate status to stakeholders
- Track project metrics and prepare reports on project progress and outcomes
