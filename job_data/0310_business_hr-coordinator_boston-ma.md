# HR Coordinator

**Company:** Vanguard | **Category:** Business | **Location:** Boston, MA | **Salary:** $56,000–$72,000 | **Type:** Full-Time | **Posted:** 2026-03-10

## About Vanguard

Vanguard is one of the world's largest investment management companies, known for putting investors first. We're also known for treating our employees well and creating a collaborative, inclusive workplace.

## About the Role

As an HR Coordinator at Vanguard Boston, you'll be the operational support for our human resources team. You'll manage employee data, coordinate benefits programs, and help new employees start their Vanguard journey successfully. This hybrid role offers valuable human resources experience in a people-focused organization.

## Responsibilities

- Maintain employee records and ensure HRIS data accuracy; process updates and changes
- Coordinate benefits administration, open enrollment, and employee communications
- Support onboarding and offboarding processes for new and departing employees
- Prepare HR reports and documentation for management review
- Assist with employee relations matters and grievance tracking

## Requirements

**Education:** Bachelor's degree in Human Resources, Business Administration, or related field

**Experience:** 1-2 years of HR coordination or administrative experience

**Skills:**
- HRIS system proficiency (Workday or similar)
- Strong organizational and administrative abilities
- Excellent written and verbal communication
- Attention to detail with ability to maintain confidentiality
- Ability to work with diverse employee populations

**Certifications:** None required

- Professional development and HR certification support
