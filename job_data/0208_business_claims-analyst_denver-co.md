## About Charles Schwab

Charles Schwab is a leading investment management and brokerage firm committed to helping investors be smarter about their finances. With decades of industry expertise, we serve millions of clients and manage trillions in assets. Our Denver office is a hub for operations and client support excellence.

## About the Role

As a Claims Analyst, you'll be the backbone of our client dispute resolution process. We handle thousands of claims annually, and we need someone who can bring both precision and empathy to each case. You'll work in a hybrid environment, giving you flexibility while staying connected to our team.

## Responsibilities

- Review and process incoming claims submissions, verifying documentation and claim eligibility
- Investigate discrepancies and contact clients to obtain additional information or clarification
- Maintain detailed records of all claim activities and document findings in our case management system
- Coordinate with legal and compliance teams to resolve complex or disputed claims
- Generate monthly reports on claims volume, resolution rates, and trend analysis
- Meet performance targets for claim processing time while maintaining 98%+ accuracy
