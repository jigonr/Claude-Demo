## About Deloitte

Deloitte is the largest professional services network in the world, delivering audit, consulting, tax, and advisory services to many of the most admired brands and public-sector organizations across the globe. At Deloitte, our purpose is to make an impact that matters — for our clients, our people, and our communities.

## About the Role

As an Audit Associate in our Chicago practice, you will serve Fortune 500 clients and high-growth enterprises, executing financial statement audits and assurance engagements that uphold the integrity of the capital markets. You will work within collaborative engagement teams, developing deep technical expertise while building the client relationships that define a successful career in professional services. This role offers a structured path for advancement within the Big 4, with significant investment in your professional growth from day one.

## Responsibilities

- Execute audit procedures over key financial statement areas including revenue recognition, inventory, and accounts receivable in accordance with PCAOB and AICPA standards
- Evaluate the design and operating effectiveness of internal controls, documenting findings and communicating deficiencies to engagement leadership
- Prepare and review audit workpapers, ensuring thorough documentation that meets Deloitte's methodology standards and regulatory requirements
- Leverage data analytics tools and techniques to identify anomalies, assess risk, and enhance the quality and efficiency of audit engagements
- Build and maintain productive working relationships with client personnel, serving as a reliable point of contact for day-to-day audit inquiries

## Requirements

- **Education:** Bachelor's degree in Accounting, Finance, or a related field from an accredited institution; Master's in Accounting preferred
- **Experience:** 0-2 years of experience in public accounting, audit, or assurance services
- **Skills:** GAAP, audit methodology, Excel, data analytics, SAP, written communication
- **Certifications:** None required; CPA eligibility or active candidacy strongly preferred
