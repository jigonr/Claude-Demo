You'll play a critical role in ensuring patient safety through quality oversight. As a Healthcare Quality Associate, you'll audit processes, identify improvements, and ensure regulatory compliance. This remote Ann Arbor role contributes directly to the quality standards that protect patients.

- Prepare quality reports and metrics
- Support continuous improvement initiatives
