## About Mass General Brigham
Mass General Brigham is a world-renowned healthcare system providing innovative care across a network of hospitals.

## About the Role
As a Licensed Practical Nurse, you'll deliver compassionate care to diverse patient populations. You will develop expertise in critical areas while working alongside experienced professionals who are committed to your growth.

## Responsibilities
- Provide direct patient care under RN supervision
- Assess patient vital signs and report changes to nursing staff
- Administer medications and treatments as prescribed
- Maintain accurate patient medical records
- Support infection control and safety protocols
- Assist with patient hygiene, comfort, and education

## Requirements

- **Education:** Diploma or Associate's degree in Nursing; LPN license required
- **Experience:** 1-3 years of nursing or healthcare experience
- **Skills:** Patient care, Medication administration, Vital signs monitoring, Documentation, Teamwork
- **Certifications:** LPN/LVN license, CPR/BLS, ACLS

- Comprehensive health insurance (medical, dental, vision)
