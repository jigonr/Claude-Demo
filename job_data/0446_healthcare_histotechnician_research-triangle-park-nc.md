# Histotechnician

**Genentech** | Healthcare | Research Triangle Park, NC | $42,000–$55,000 USD/year | Full-time | Posted Mar 14, 2026

## About Genentech

Genentech is a biotechnology company specializing in cancer and genetic disease treatment. As part of Roche, we're advancing scientific discovery that transforms patient outcomes globally.

## About the Role

As a Histotechnician at Genentech's Research Triangle Park facility, you'll prepare tissue samples that are central to breakthrough research. Work on-site in one of the world's premier biotechnology facilities, contributing hands-on expertise to groundbreaking discoveries.

## Responsibilities

- Prepare tissue specimens for microscopic examination
- Operate histology laboratory equipment and instruments
- Follow standardized protocols and maintain quality standards
- Document specimen information and maintain sample tracking systems
- Support immunohistochemistry and special staining procedures
- Assist with sample analysis and data collection for research projects

## Requirements

**Education**
High School diploma or Associate's degree in Histology or Medical Laboratory Technology

**Experience**
1–3 years in histology, laboratory, or medical technology roles

**Skills**
- Tissue sample preparation and staining techniques
- Laboratory equipment operation
- Knowledge of histological procedures and protocols
- Attention to detail and accuracy
- Ability to follow complex procedures
- Laboratory safety and quality control awareness

**Certifications**
ASCP Histotechnician (HT) certification required or within 12 months
