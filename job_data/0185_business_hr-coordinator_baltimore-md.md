# HR Coordinator

**Unilever** | Business | Baltimore, MD (Remote) | $45,000–$58,000 USD/year | Full-time | Posted Feb 16, 2026

## About Unilever

Unilever is a global leader in fast-moving consumer goods, with iconic brands trusted by millions of people worldwide. Our commitment to sustainable and responsible growth has made us a destination for talented professionals who want to make a real difference.

## About the Role

As an HR Coordinator at Unilever, you'll play a vital role in employee experience and talent management. You'll handle the essential HR functions that keep our teams connected and supported—from onboarding new talent to managing benefits and supporting employee engagement. Work fully remote while supporting a global organization.

## Responsibilities

- Coordinate new employee onboarding, including documentation, orientation materials, and system setup
- Process employee benefits enrollments and manage benefits-related inquiries and changes
- Maintain and update HR databases and employee records in compliance with company policies
- Schedule interviews, coordinate hiring logistics, and support the recruitment process
- Support employee relations activities including recognition programs and employee engagement initiatives
- Generate HR reports and assist with compensation analysis and payroll coordination

## Requirements

**Education**
Bachelor's degree in Human Resources, Business Administration, or related field

**Experience**
1–3 years in HR coordination, recruitment support, or employee services

**Skills**
- Experience with HRIS systems (Workday preferred)
- Knowledge of HR practices and employment law basics
- Strong organizational and time management skills
- Excellent written and verbal communication
- Proficiency with Microsoft Office suite
- Ability to maintain confidentiality and handle sensitive information

**Certifications**
PHR (Professional in Human Resources) or SHRM-CP certification (preferred within 12 months)
