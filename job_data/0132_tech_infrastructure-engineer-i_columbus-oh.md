## About Datadog

Datadog provides monitoring and analytics infrastructure for cloud environments, helping engineering teams understand the performance and health of their systems at scale.

## About the Role

We're hiring an Infrastructure Engineer I to help build and maintain the resilient infrastructure that powers our cloud monitoring platform. You'll own pieces of our production environment, learn from experienced engineers, and develop expertise in cloud operations and system reliability.

## Responsibilities

- Deploy, configure, and maintain Kubernetes clusters across multiple cloud providers
- Write Terraform and Ansible code to automate infrastructure provisioning
- Troubleshoot infrastructure issues and implement preventive monitoring
- Participate in on-call rotation and respond to production incidents
- Document infrastructure decisions and maintain runbooks
- Collaborate with platform teams to ensure scalability and reliability

## Requirements

**Education**
Bachelor's degree in Computer Science or related field

**Experience**
1-3 years of systems administration, DevOps, or cloud infrastructure experience

**Skills**
- Linux/Unix system administration
- Kubernetes or container orchestration
- Infrastructure-as-code (Terraform, CloudFormation)
- Cloud platforms (AWS, GCP, or Azure)
- Networking and TCP/IP fundamentals

**Certifications**
- None required; cloud certifications preferred
