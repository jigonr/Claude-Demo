# Pharmacy Technician

**Company:** UCSF Health

**Posted:** 2026-03-15

## About UCSF Health

UCSF Health is an integrated academic health system combining world-class clinical care with cutting-edge research and training. Our mail-order and telehealth pharmacy services bring essential medications to patients across California and the western United States.

## About the Role

We're expanding our remote pharmacy operations and seeking a Pharmacy Technician to support our mail-order and telehealth prescription services. You will compound medications, verify prescriptions, manage medication inventory systems, and ensure compliance with state pharmacy regulations. Working remotely, you'll be part of a mission-driven healthcare organization.

## Responsibilities

- Prepare and compound medications according to pharmacist-verified prescriptions
- Verify patient information and insurance coverage in pharmacy management systems
- Label medications and prepare packaging for mail distribution
- Monitor pharmacy inventory and flag items requiring reorder
- Process refill requests and coordinate with patient services teams
- Maintain detailed records for DEA compliance and regulatory audits

## Requirements

**Education**
High school diploma or GED; pharmacy technician program completion or in-progress

**Experience**
1-3 years of pharmacy or healthcare experience

**Skills**
- Pharmacy compounding and preparation
- Medication safety and accuracy
- Pharmacy information systems
- Attention to detail in patient safety-critical work
- Customer service and communication

**Certifications**
- California Pharmacy Technician License (or eligible to obtain)
- Recommended: Certified Pharmacy Technician (CPhT)
