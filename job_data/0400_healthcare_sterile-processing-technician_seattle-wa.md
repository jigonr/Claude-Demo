**Mass General Brigham** | Healthcare | Seattle, WA | $43,000–$56,000/year | Full-time | Posted Mar 13, 2026

## About Mass General Brigham

Mass General Brigham is a leading academic medical system committed to advancing medicine and caring for communities. Our integrated healthcare delivery network spans hospitals, specialty centers, and research facilities.

## About the Role

As a Sterile Processing Technician at Mass General Brigham Seattle, you'll ensure surgical instruments are safe, sterile, and ready for patient care. This hybrid role offers flexibility while maintaining the critical hands-on presence required for surgical support. Your meticulous work directly enables our surgeons to provide excellent patient care.

## Responsibilities

- Decontaminate, inspect, and process surgical instruments and medical equipment according to strict protocols
- Operate sterilization equipment; monitor cycles and validate sterilization effectiveness
- Maintain organized inventory of sterile instruments; track usage and reorder stock
- Document sterilization records and equipment maintenance; ensure compliance with regulations
- Support surgical services; communicate any equipment issues to supervisory staff

## Requirements

**Education**
High school diploma or GED; completion of sterile processing technician program

**Experience**
1-2 years of sterile processing, instrument sterilization, or surgical support experience

**Skills**
- Sterilization processes and techniques
- Medical equipment operation
- Attention to detail
- Data documentation
- Safety compliance

**Certifications**
Certified Sterile Processing Technician (CSPT, preferred)
