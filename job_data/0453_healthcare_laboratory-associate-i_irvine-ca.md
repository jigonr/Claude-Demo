## About Medtronic

Medtronic is the global leader in medical technology, services, and solutions. We treat and monitor millions of patients annually, developing devices and therapies that extend and enhance lives. Our innovation centers in Irvine focus on cardiac and vascular device development and testing.

## About the Role

As a Laboratory Associate I, you'll support critical testing and validation of medical devices that save lives. Working hybrid in Irvine, you'll conduct biocompatibility tests, prepare specimens, and maintain laboratory equipment. This hands-on role offers the opportunity to see how your technical work directly enables breakthrough medical devices.

## Responsibilities

- Prepare specimens and materials for testing and analysis
- Operate and maintain laboratory equipment and instruments
- Record test data and maintain laboratory documentation
- Assist with quality assurance checks and regulatory testing
- Support senior laboratory staff and contribute to test development
