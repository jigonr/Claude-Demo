# Phlebotomy Technician

**Company:** Sanofi
**Category:** Healthcare
**Location:** Miami, FL, US (Remote)
**Salary:** $33,000 - $44,000/year
**Employment Type:** Full-time
**Posted:** 2026-03-11

## About Sanofi
Sanofi is a global biopharmaceutical company dedicated to saving lives with innovative vaccines and treatments.

## About the Role
You'll collect clinical specimens supporting pharmaceutical research. You will develop expertise in critical areas while working alongside experienced professionals who are committed to your growth.

## Responsibilities
- Collect blood samples from study participants following strict clinical protocols
- Maintain sterile technique and adhere to OSHA bloodborne pathogen standards
- Process and label specimens with precise documentation
- Monitor participant safety and comfort during collection
- Maintain equipment and supply inventory for collection stations
- Record all collection activities in electronic health records

## Requirements

- **Education:** High school diploma or equivalent; phlebotomy certification required
- **Experience:** 0-1 year of phlebotomy or clinical specimen collection experience
- **Skills:** Venipuncture techniques, OSHA compliance, Patient communication, Laboratory safety, EHR/LIS systems
- **Certifications:** CPT certification, CPR/BLS certification
