## About Dropbox

Dropbox is the world's leading cloud content platform, helping millions of people and organizations manage their files with flexibility and ease. Our mission is to simplify how the world works together.

## About the Role

We're looking for a Cloud Operations Analyst to join our Boulder team and help manage the infrastructure that powers Dropbox's services for millions of users worldwide. You'll work on-site at our Boulder headquarters, collaborating with talented engineers to solve complex problems at massive scale. If you're passionate about systems thinking and operational excellence, this is your chance to make an impact.

- Collaborate with platform and infrastructure teams to design robust, scalable systems
