# Patient Care Coordinator

**Kaiser Permanente** | Healthcare | Baltimore, MD | $42,000–$55,000/year | Full-Time (Hybrid) | Posted Mar 8, 2026

## About Kaiser Permanente

Kaiser Permanente is one of America's leading integrated healthcare organizations, serving over 12 million members. We're committed to prevention, health, and wellness through an integrated delivery system that coordinates physical and mental health care.

## About the Role

As a Patient Care Coordinator at Kaiser Permanente's Baltimore facility, you'll be the organizational backbone that keeps patient care moving smoothly. Working hybrid, you'll manage schedules, communicate with patients, and coordinate between clinical teams. Your work ensures patients receive timely, well-coordinated care across our integrated health system.

## Responsibilities

- Schedule and manage patient appointments across departments
- Respond to patient inquiries and coordinate care requests
- Verify patient insurance and gather required documentation
- Prepare clinical materials and maintain patient records
- Communicate with clinical staff to coordinate care delivery
- Follow up with patients on test results and treatment plans

## Requirements

**Skills**

- Scheduling and organizational skills

- Electronic health records (EHR) proficiency
- Ability to multitask and manage priorities
- Understanding of HIPAA and patient confidentiality
