## About PepsiCo
PepsiCo is the world's leading food and beverage company with $91B in net revenue and operations in 200+ countries.

## About the Role
As a Tax Analyst, you'll ensure compliance and optimize our global tax position. You will develop expertise in critical areas while working alongside experienced professionals who are committed to your growth.

## Responsibilities
- Prepare and review tax compliance filings across multiple jurisdictions
- Analyze tax implications of corporate transactions and restructurings
- Maintain tax provision calculations and support external audits
- Research tax law changes and assess impact on company strategy
- Work with business units to identify tax planning opportunities
- Document tax positions and maintain supporting schedules

## Requirements

- **Education:** Bachelor's degree in Accounting, Finance, or related field
- **Experience:** 2-4 years of tax preparation or tax compliance experience
- **Skills:** Tax research, Federal/state tax preparation, Tax software, Financial analysis, Attention to detail
- **Certifications:** CPA (preferred), Enrolled Agent (preferred)
