# Junior UX Designer

**Company:** Elastic | **Category:** Tech | **Location:** Des Moines, IA | **Salary:** $52,000–$68,000 | **Type:** Full-Time | **Posted:** 2026-03-14

## About Elastic

Elastic develops the leading search and analytics engine trusted by organizations worldwide. We're committed to making data searchable and actionable for everyone.

## About the Role

As a Junior UX Designer at Elastic Des Moines, you'll contribute to designing experiences used by millions of developers and data professionals. You'll work in a hybrid environment with experienced designers who will mentor you in user-centered design practices, research methods, and design systems thinking.

## Responsibilities

- Design user interfaces and user flows for new features and products
- Create wireframes, mockups, and interactive prototypes using Figma
- Conduct user research, usability testing, and gather feedback on designs
- Collaborate with product and engineering teams on design implementation
- Document design decisions and maintain design systems and component libraries

## Requirements

**Education:** Bachelor's degree in Design, HCI, or related field; or equivalent portfolio demonstrating design skills

**Experience:** 0-2 years of UX design experience, including internships or project work

**Skills:**
- Proficiency with design tools (Figma, Sketch, Adobe XD)
- Understanding of UX principles and design thinking
- Basic prototyping and wireframing capabilities
- Communication and collaboration abilities
- Familiarity with design systems and component-based design

**Certifications:** None required
