## About 3M

3M is a global science company that serves customers around the world with innovative products and solutions that impact lives and help make the world better.

## About the Role

We're seeking a Claims Analyst to manage insurance claims and support our risk management operations. Working remotely, you will investigate claims, review documentation, coordinate with carriers, and ensure timely resolution. Your work protects 3M's assets and supports optimal claim outcomes.

## Responsibilities

- Receive and log incoming insurance claims
- Investigate claims and request documentation from claimants
- Coordinate with insurance carriers and adjusters
- Prepare claim reports and cost analyses
- Track claim status and ensure timely updates
- Identify trends and recommend risk mitigation strategies

## Requirements

**Skills**

- Insurance policy interpretation
- Documentation and report writing
- Communication with internal stakeholders and carriers
- Attention to detail and accuracy

**Certifications**
- ARM or AIC certification preferred
