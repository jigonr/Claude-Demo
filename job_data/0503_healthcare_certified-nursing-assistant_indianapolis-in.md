## About Broad Institute
The Broad Institute advances biomedical research through genomic science. Our research has pioneered breakthroughs in cancer, infectious disease, and stem cell biology.

## About the Role
We're seeking a Certified Nursing Assistant to join our Research team in Indianapolis. This role offers the opportunity to contribute meaningfully to our mission while developing your professional skills in a collaborative environment. You'll work with talented colleagues dedicated to excellence and innovation in our field.

## Responsibilities
- Provide personal care and assistance with activities of daily living for patients
- Monitor and report changes in patient condition to nursing staff
- Maintain patient comfort and ensure safe environment
- Assist with bathing, grooming, dressing, and toileting
- Document care activities and patient interactions
- Support nursing staff with clinical procedures and patient transport

## Requirements
- **Education:** High school diploma or GED; CNA certification required
- **Experience:** 0-2 years of direct patient care experience
- **Skills:** Patient care, Empathy, Physical stamina, Communication, Attention to detail
- **Certifications:** Certified Nursing Assistant (CNA), BLS/CPR
