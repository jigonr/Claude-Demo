## About Morgan Stanley

Morgan Stanley is a leading global financial services firm providing investment banking, wealth management, and insurance solutions to corporations, institutions, and individuals.

## About the Role

We're seeking an Insurance Underwriter for our Raleigh office to assess risk, evaluate insurance applications, and make underwriting decisions for commercial insurance products. You will review medical records, financial documentation, and claim history to determine insurability and premium pricing.

## Responsibilities

- Review insurance applications and supporting documentation for completeness
- Assess risk factors and determine insurability of applicants
- Order and interpret medical reports and financial statements
- Make underwriting decisions within company guidelines
- Prepare underwriting decisions and communicate with brokers
- Maintain accurate underwriting files and case notes

**Skills**

- Understanding of insurance products and underwriting guidelines
- Medical and financial document interpretation
- Attention to detail and accuracy
- Communication and broker relationship management

**Certifications**
- Property and Casualty Insurance license preferred
