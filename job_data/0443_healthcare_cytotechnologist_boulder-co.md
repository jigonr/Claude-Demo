# Cytotechnologist

**Sanofi** | Healthcare | Boulder, CO (Remote) | $60,000–$77,000 USD/year | Full-time | Posted Feb 20, 2026

## About Sanofi

Sanofi is a global biopharmaceutical company focused on discovering and developing medicines to improve patient health. Our research spans cancer, rare diseases, and other therapeutic areas.

## About the Role

As a Cytotechnologist at Sanofi, you'll analyze cell samples that support pharmaceutical research and drug development. Work fully remote in this specialized role, contributing your expertise to medicines that improve lives globally.

## Responsibilities

- Examine and analyze cytological specimens using microscopy
- Identify abnormal cells and cellular structures
- Prepare slide samples according to established protocols
- Maintain detailed records and documentation of findings
- Support quality control and regulatory compliance procedures
- Communicate findings to supervising pathologists and researchers

## Requirements

**Education**
Bachelor's degree in Medical Laboratory Science or related field

**Experience**
2–4 years in cytology, cell biology, or pathology laboratory roles

**Skills**
- Cytology analysis and microscopy techniques
- Knowledge of cell biology and pathology
- Attention to detail and accuracy
- Laboratory safety and quality control
- Computer skills for reporting and data entry
- Professional communication abilities

**Certifications**
ASCP Cytotechnologist (CT) certification required
