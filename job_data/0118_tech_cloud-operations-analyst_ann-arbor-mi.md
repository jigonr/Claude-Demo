## About monday.com

monday.com is a work OS that powers teams to run processes, projects, and portfolios in one digital workspace. The company serves over 200,000 customers worldwide.

## About the Role

We're seeking a Cloud Operations Analyst to support our cloud infrastructure, optimize system performance, and ensure high availability for our work management platform. You will monitor cloud resources, implement automation, and partner with engineering teams on scaling initiatives.

## Responsibilities

- Monitor cloud infrastructure and systems for performance and availability
- Implement infrastructure automation and infrastructure-as-code solutions
- Troubleshoot cloud platform issues and coordinate with vendors
- Optimize cloud costs and resource utilization
- Document operational procedures and create runbooks
- Support incident response and participate in on-call rotation

**Skills**
