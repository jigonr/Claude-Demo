## About the Role
We're seeking talented professionals to support our risk management and operational excellence initiatives. You will develop expertise in critical areas while working alongside experienced professionals who are committed to your growth.

## Responsibilities
- Conduct quantitative risk assessments for supply chain and operations across global facilities
- Develop risk mitigation strategies and monitor implementation effectiveness
- Compile compliance reports for regulatory bodies and internal stakeholders
- Collaborate with cross-functional teams to identify emerging risk exposures
- Maintain risk documentation and lead annual risk assessments
- Support business continuity planning and disaster recovery protocols

## Requirements

- **Education:** Bachelor's degree in Business, Finance, Economics, or related field
- **Experience:** 2-5 years of risk management, operations, or business analysis experience
- **Skills:** Financial analysis, Risk assessment methodologies, Data analysis, Excel/SQL, Risk reporting
- **Certifications:** FRM certification (preferred), Six Sigma (preferred)
