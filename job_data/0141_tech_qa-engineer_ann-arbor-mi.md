# QA Engineer

**Company:** Salesforce | **Category:** Tech | **Location:** Ann Arbor, MI (Remote) | **Salary:** $57,000-$74,000/year | **Type:** Full-time | **Posted:** Jan 23, 2026

## About Salesforce

Salesforce is the world's leading customer relationship management (CRM) platform, trusted by millions of users globally. Our mission is to make every customer successful. We believe that quality is non-negotiable—our customers depend on our platform to power their most critical business processes.

## About the Role

As a QA Engineer on our team, you'll be the quality gatekeeper for features that impact millions of users worldwide. You'll own the responsibility of ensuring our platform meets rigorous quality standards through intelligent testing strategies, automation, and collaboration. This remote role lets you contribute to a global product while working from anywhere.

## Responsibilities

- Execute comprehensive test plans covering new feature releases and platform updates
- Design and implement automated test scripts using Selenium, Cypress, or similar frameworks
- Document test results and create detailed bug reports with reproduction steps
- Collaborate with development teams to clarify requirements and acceptance criteria
- Participate in quality metrics analysis and continuous improvement initiatives

## Requirements

**Education:** High school diploma or equivalent; degree in Computer Science preferred

**Experience:** 0-2 years

**Skills:**
- Test case design and execution
- Selenium, Cypress, or similar automation tools
- SQL for database validation
- Clear written communication
- Salesforce platform knowledge (preferred)
