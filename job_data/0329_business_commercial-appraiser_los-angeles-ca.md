## About Marsh McLennan

Marsh McLennan is a global leader in risk, strategy, and people, advising clients on complex decisions and opportunities. Our appraisal and valuation services support billions of dollars in transactions annually.

## About the Role

We're seeking a Commercial Appraiser to value commercial properties and provide risk assessment reports for insurance and lending purposes. Working remotely across our Los Angeles region, you will analyze property characteristics, comparable market data, and financial performance to determine fair market values. Your appraisals support critical business decisions.

## Responsibilities

- Conduct commercial property inspections and gather data on building systems, condition, and specifications
- Analyze comparable properties and market data to support valuation conclusions
- Prepare comprehensive appraisal reports following USPAP standards
- Research income statements and expense data for income-approach valuations
- Interface with clients to clarify property characteristics and intended use
- Stay current on real estate market trends and valuation methodologies

## Requirements

**Education**
Bachelor's degree in Real Estate, Finance, Business, or related field

**Experience**
2-4 years of commercial real estate appraisal or valuation experience

**Skills**
- USPAP knowledge and compliance
- Valuation methodologies (cost, income, market approaches)
- Commercial real estate market analysis
- Financial statement analysis
- Professional report writing and presentation

**Certifications**
- California Certified Commercial Real Estate Appraiser license required
- CCIM or MAI designation preferred
