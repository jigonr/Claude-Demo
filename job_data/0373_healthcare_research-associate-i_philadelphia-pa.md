# Research Associate I

**Company:** Johns Hopkins Medicine | **Category:** Healthcare | **Location:** Philadelphia, PA (Onsite) | **Salary:** $50,000-$65,000/year | **Type:** Full-time | **Posted:** Feb 11, 2026

## About Johns Hopkins Medicine

Johns Hopkins Medicine represents one of the world's preeminent medical institutions, advancing science and medicine through world-class research, patient care, and education. We're driven by curiosity and the desire to improve human health.

## About the Role

You'll join our research operations team, directly supporting clinical studies that advance medical knowledge. As a Research Associate, you'll manage critical aspects of research conduct—from participant recruitment to data integrity—contributing to discoveries that will shape medicine. This onsite Philadelphia role offers meaningful research experience.

## Responsibilities

- Recruit and screen study participants according to protocol criteria
- Collect biological samples and clinical data with attention to quality
- Maintain detailed research records and ensure regulatory compliance
- Support patient follow-up appointments and retention efforts
- Assist with study reporting and data quality reviews

## Requirements

**Education:** Bachelor's degree in Life Sciences, Healthcare, or related field

**Experience:** 0-2 years

**Certifications:** CITI Human Subjects Research certification (or eligible)

**Skills:**
- Strong attention to detail and accuracy
- Patient communication and empathy
- Knowledge of GCP and research ethics
- Electronic data capture (EDC) systems
- Organizational and time management skills
