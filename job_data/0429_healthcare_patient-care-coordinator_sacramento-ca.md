# Patient Care Coordinator

**Syneos Health** | Healthcare | Sacramento, CA | $44,000–$57,000 USD/year | Full-time | Posted Mar 12, 2026

## About Syneos Health

Syneos Health is the leading fully integrated biopharmaceutical services company, providing clinical research and commercial solutions that help pharmaceutical companies get new medicines to market faster. We're dedicated to accelerating patient access to innovative treatments.

## About the Role

As a Patient Care Coordinator at our Sacramento clinical research site, you'll be the patient-facing representative ensuring smooth trial operations and quality care. Work on-site managing patient interactions, scheduling, and documentation that keeps clinical research on track.

## Responsibilities

- Schedule patient visits and manage appointment calendars for clinical trials
- Conduct patient phone screening calls and collect baseline health information
- Escort patients through study procedures and ensure informed consent compliance
- Collect and organize patient case report forms and study documentation
- Communicate trial status updates to study sponsors and monitors
- Maintain patient confidentiality and follow HIPAA protocols

## Requirements

**Education**
High School diploma or GED; Associate's degree in healthcare preferred

**Experience**
1–2 years in patient services, administrative healthcare, or customer service roles

**Skills**
- Strong communication and patient interaction skills
- Organizational ability and attention to detail
- Proficiency with computer systems and data entry
- Knowledge of clinical trial procedures (training provided)
- Customer service mindset and empathy
- Ability to manage multiple priorities

**Certifications**
HIPAA certification required (training provided)
