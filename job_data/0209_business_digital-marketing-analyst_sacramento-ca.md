## About General Mills

For over 150 years, General Mills has nourished families with beloved brands that define the American household—from Cheerios to Betty Crocker. We're committed to creating products that inspire healthier eating and sustainable living. Our marketing teams drive growth by understanding consumers and crafting campaigns that resonate.

## About the Role

You'll join our Digital Marketing team as an analyst, working with data to fuel smarter marketing decisions. You'll evaluate campaign performance, uncover insights about consumer behavior, and recommend strategies that drive brand growth. This remote position lets you contribute to campaigns reaching millions of households annually.

## Responsibilities

- Analyze digital marketing campaign performance across email, social media, search, and display channels
- Track and report on key performance indicators using analytics platforms and dashboards
- Identify trends in consumer behavior and competitive landscape insights
- Develop actionable recommendations to optimize campaign ROI and conversion rates
- Support A/B testing initiatives and provide statistical analysis of results
