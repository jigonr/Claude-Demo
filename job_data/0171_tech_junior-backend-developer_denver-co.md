## About Plaid

Plaid is the financial connectivity platform that powers the future of fintech. We enable developers and companies to securely access financial data, and we're trusted by millions of users and thousands of the world's most innovative companies.

## About the Role

As a Junior Backend Developer at Plaid, you'll work on the APIs and services that connect the financial ecosystem. Working remotely, you'll contribute to building scalable systems that enable financial innovation and help people and companies achieve their financial goals.

## Responsibilities

- Develop and maintain backend services and APIs using Python or Node.js
- Design and optimize database schemas and queries for performance
- Implement features following agile development practices
- Write unit tests and participate in code reviews
- Collaborate with product and frontend teams on feature specifications

## Requirements

**Experience:** 0-2 years of backend development experience, including internships or project work

**Skills:**
