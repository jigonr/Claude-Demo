## About Capital One

Capital One is a leading financial services company providing banking, credit cards, and consumer finance products to millions of customers. Our Research Triangle Park office is a growing hub for operations and corporate functions.

## About the Role

We're hiring a Benefits Coordinator to manage employee benefits programs, process benefits enrollments, and provide benefits education to our workforce. You will be the primary point of contact for employees with benefits questions and ensure compliance with benefits regulations.

## Responsibilities

- Coordinate annual open enrollment and manage enrollment administration
- Process benefits applications and maintain accurate benefits records
- Answer employee questions about health insurance, retirement, and other benefits
- Prepare benefits communications and education materials
- Reconcile benefits invoices and process payments to carriers
- Ensure compliance with benefits regulations and audit requirements

## Requirements

**Education**
Bachelor's degree in Human Resources, Business, or related field

**Experience**
1-3 years of benefits administration, HR operations, or HR support experience

**Skills**
- Benefits administration and HRIS systems
- Employee communication and customer service
- Attention to detail and accuracy
- Understanding of benefits regulations (ERISA, ACA)
- Data management and record keeping

**Certifications**
- None required; PHR or SHRM-CP preferred
