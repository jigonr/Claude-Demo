# Community Health Worker

**Medtronic** | Healthcare | Remote | $31,000–$40,000/year | Full-time | Posted Jan 6, 2026

## About Medtronic

Medtronic is a global leader in medical technology, services, and solutions. We alleviate pain, restore health, and extend life for millions of people worldwide. Our mission extends beyond hospitals to ensure equitable access to care in communities everywhere.

## About the Role

As a Community Health Worker at Medtronic, you'll be on the front lines of health equity. You'll work with communities facing the greatest health challenges, providing education, building trust, and connecting people to resources. This remote role offers flexibility while maintaining meaningful in-person community impact. Your work directly supports our commitment to health equity and breaking down barriers to care.

## Responsibilities

- Conduct health education sessions in community settings, addressing disease prevention and management strategies
- Build relationships with community members and refer patients to appropriate healthcare resources and programs
- Collect data on community health needs and barriers to care, reporting findings to clinical and program teams
- Serve as a cultural liaison, ensuring healthcare communications are accessible and appropriate for diverse populations
- Participate in community outreach events and health fairs to increase awareness of available medical device therapies

## Requirements

**Education**
High school diploma or GED; Community Health Worker certification or equivalent experience preferred

**Experience**
1-3 years of community health, case management, or social services experience

**Skills**

- Cultural competency
- Data documentation

**Certifications**
Community Health Worker Certificate (preferred)
