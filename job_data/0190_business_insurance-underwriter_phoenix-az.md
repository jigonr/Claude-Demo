## About Travelers

Travelers is one of the largest property and casualty insurance companies in the United States, providing insurance products and services for individuals and businesses since 1864.

## About the Role

We're seeking an Insurance Underwriter for our Phoenix office to evaluate commercial insurance applications, assess risk, and make underwriting decisions. You will review policy applications and communicate underwriting decisions to brokers and customers.

## Responsibilities

- Review commercial insurance applications and identify required documentation
- Order property inspections, loss control assessments, and third-party reports
- Analyze risk factors and make underwriting decisions per guidelines
- Negotiate terms and conditions with brokers and risk managers
- Communicate underwriting decisions and rationale clearly
- Maintain accurate records and track performance metrics

## Requirements

**Education**
Bachelor's degree in Business, Finance, Insurance, or related field

**Experience**
1-3 years of insurance underwriting or risk assessment experience

**Skills**
- Risk assessment and evaluation
- Commercial insurance knowledge
- Policy analysis and interpretation
- Communication and negotiation skills
- Attention to detail and accuracy

**Certifications**
- Arizona insurance license preferred or in-progress
