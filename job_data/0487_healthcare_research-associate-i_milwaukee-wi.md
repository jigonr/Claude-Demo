# Research Associate I

**Syneos Health** | Healthcare | Milwaukee, WI | $42,000–$55,000/year | Full-Time | Posted Feb 6, 2026

## About Syneos Health

Syneos Health is the world's leading, fully integrated biopharmaceutical solutions organization, dedicated to accelerating customer success. We connect human insights with intelligent systems to unlock value for sponsors, sites, and patients throughout the clinical research lifecycle.

## About the Role

As a Research Associate I, you'll be the backbone of our clinical trial operations in Milwaukee. You'll work directly with study participants, manage essential documentation, and support the day-to-day execution of research protocols. If you're organized, compassionate, and detail-oriented, this is a great entry point into a rewarding healthcare career.

## Responsibilities

- Recruit and screen potential study participants according to protocol-specified inclusion/exclusion criteria
- Obtain informed consent and document participant enrollment procedures
- Schedule participant visits and coordinate study-related appointments
- Maintain confidential participant records and trial documentation
- Assist with data collection, entry, and verification under supervision
- Ensure compliance with Good Clinical Practice (GCP) guidelines and regulatory requirements
