## About the Role

We're seeking a Biostatistician I to support clinical research and health services research initiatives. You will design studies, analyze healthcare data, and provide statistical expertise to researchers and clinicians studying treatment effectiveness and health outcomes.

## Responsibilities

- Design statistical analysis plans for clinical and observational studies
- Analyze healthcare data using SAS, R, or Python
- Develop statistical models to evaluate treatment outcomes
- Create statistical tables, figures, and reports for publications
- Collaborate with physicians and researchers on study protocol design
- Present statistical findings to clinical and research teams

**Skills**

- Healthcare data and EHR systems understanding
- Report writing and presentation skills
