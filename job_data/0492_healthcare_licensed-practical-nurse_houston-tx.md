**AstraZeneca** | Healthcare | Houston, TX | $39,000–$52,000/year | Full-Time | Posted Jan 24, 2026

## About AstraZeneca

AstraZeneca is a global biopharmaceutical company committed to discovering, developing, and commercializing innovative medicines that address serious diseases. We're driven by a mission to help patients live longer and better.

## About the Role

As a Licensed Practical Nurse at AstraZeneca's Houston facility, you'll provide clinical nursing care and support our healthcare operations. Working onsite, you'll care for patients, administer medications, and work collaboratively with our clinical team. This role offers the opportunity to advance your nursing career while supporting pharmaceutical innovation.

## Responsibilities

- Provide direct patient care and monitoring under RN supervision
- Administer medications and treatments per established protocols
- Maintain accurate patient records and clinical documentation
- Assist with clinical procedures and patient preparation
- Communicate with patients and families about care plans
- Monitor vital signs and report changes to nursing staff

**Skills**
