# Laboratory Associate I

## About PPD
PPD is a leading clinical research organization conducting trials across therapeutic areas.

## About the Role
You'll perform analytical work supporting pharmaceutical and biotech research. You will develop expertise in critical areas while working alongside experienced professionals who are committed to your growth.

## Responsibilities
- Perform routine laboratory procedures following Standard Operating Procedures
- Prepare and analyze clinical specimens for research studies
- Maintain laboratory equipment and ensure quality control
- Record data accurately in laboratory information systems
- Clean and sterilize equipment following infection control protocols
- Support compliance audits and regulatory inspections

## Requirements

- **Education:** High school diploma or equivalent; some college coursework preferred
- **Experience:** 0-2 years of laboratory or clinical experience
- **Skills:** Laboratory procedures, Sample preparation, Quality control, Safety protocols, ELN systems
- **Certifications:** Bloodborne Pathogen training, CPR/BLS
