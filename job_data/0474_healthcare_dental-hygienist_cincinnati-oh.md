# Dental Hygienist

**Company:** AstraZeneca | **Category:** Healthcare | **Location:** Cincinnati, OH | **Salary:** $46,000 - $61,000 | **Type:** Full-Time | **Posted:** 2026-01-11

## About AstraZeneca

AstraZeneca is a pharmaceutical company discovering, developing, and delivering medicines.

## About the Role

As a Dental Hygienist at AstraZeneca, you'll provide preventive dental care and patient education in a professional, supportive environment.

## Responsibilities

- Perform dental cleanings and scaling procedures
- Take and develop dental X-rays and intraoral photographs
- Educate patients on oral hygiene and disease prevention
- Apply dental sealants and fluoride treatments
- Document findings and communicate with dentists regarding patient status
- Maintain infection control standards and sterilize instruments

## Requirements

**Education:** High school diploma or equivalent; relevant degree or certification strongly preferred

**Experience:** 1-3 years

**Skills:**
- Clinical dentistry
- Patient communication
- Infection control
- X-ray certification
- Attention to detail
