## About ICON
ICON is a leading contract research organization advancing clinical research for pharmaceuticals and medical devices globally.

## About the Role
We're seeking a Respiratory Therapist to join our Clinical Research team in Cincinnati. This role offers the opportunity to contribute meaningfully to our mission while developing your professional skills in a collaborative environment. You'll work with talented colleagues dedicated to excellence and innovation in our field.

## Responsibilities
- Administer respiratory therapy treatments and manage patient airways
- Operate and troubleshoot ventilators and other respiratory support equipment
- Monitor patient response to therapy and adjust treatment parameters
- Maintain accurate patient treatment records and documentation
- Collaborate with clinical team to optimize respiratory care
- Ensure compliance with clinical protocols and safety standards

## Requirements
- **Education:** Associate's degree in Respiratory Therapy; Bachelor's preferred
- **Experience:** 1-3 years of respiratory therapy in clinical research or hospital setting
- **Skills:** Respiratory equipment operation, Patient assessment, Clinical knowledge, Attention to detail, Communication
- **Certifications:** Registered Respiratory Therapist (RRT) required, ACLS/BLS
