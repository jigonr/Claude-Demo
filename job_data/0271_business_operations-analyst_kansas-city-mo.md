## About GE Aerospace

GE Aerospace manufactures advanced aerospace engines and systems for commercial and defense applications. Our Kansas City facility is a cornerstone of our manufacturing operations, producing critical engine components with uncompromising quality standards.

## About the Role

We're seeking an Operations Analyst for our Kansas City Manufacturing facility to optimize production schedules, manage component inventory, and ensure efficient workflow across our advanced aerospace engine manufacturing operations. This role requires meticulous process management and the ability to work in a highly regulated defense contracting environment where precision and accountability are non-negotiable.

## Responsibilities

- Analyze production schedules and identify bottlenecks that impact delivery timelines
- Monitor inventory levels across manufacturing zones and recommend reordering based on production forecasts
- Prepare daily shift reports documenting production metrics, quality incidents, and workforce utilization
- Coordinate with quality assurance teams on defect tracking and corrective action closure rates
- Maintain compliance documentation required for ITAR and security clearance protocols
- Support lean manufacturing initiatives by mapping workflows and identifying waste reduction opportunities
