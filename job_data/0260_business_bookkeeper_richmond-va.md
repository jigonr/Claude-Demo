## About Deloitte

Deloitte is one of the world's leading professional services firms, serving clients across consulting, audit, tax, and advisory services. Our 350,000+ professionals are committed to making an impact that matters, grounded in integrity and excellence.

## About the Role

As a Bookkeeper at Deloitte's Richmond office, you'll manage financial transactions and accounting operations that support our clients and internal operations. Working onsite, you'll gain hands-on experience in accounting fundamentals while contributing to a world-class professional services organization. This entry-level role offers clear progression into accounting careers.

## Responsibilities

- Process accounts payable and accounts receivable transactions
- Maintain general ledger accounts and reconcile to supporting documentation
- Prepare bank reconciliations and cash management reports
- Assist with monthly close procedures and variance analysis
- Prepare financial reports and documentation for external auditors
- Support expense management and billing processes

**Skills**
