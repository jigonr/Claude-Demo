# QA Engineer

**Company:** Elastic | **Category:** Tech | **Location:** Washington, DC | **Salary:** $72,000–$93,000 | **Type:** Full-Time | **Posted:** 2026-02-01

## About Elastic

Elastic develops the leading search and analytics engine used by thousands of organizations worldwide. Our mission is to make data searchable, analyzable, and actionable for everyone.

## About the Role

As a QA Engineer at Elastic, you'll be responsible for ensuring the quality and reliability of our platform. This role combines manual testing expertise with test automation, giving you the opportunity to impact millions of users. You'll work remotely with a distributed team of engineers and QA professionals who are passionate about shipping quality software.

## Responsibilities

- Design and execute automated and manual test cases covering functional, integration, and performance requirements
- Write Python or Java test automation scripts using pytest or JUnit frameworks
- Collaborate with product and engineering teams to understand requirements and identify test scenarios
- Monitor and report on test coverage, defect trends, and quality metrics
- Reproduce and document bugs, working with engineers to verify fixes

## Requirements

**Education:** Bachelor's degree in Computer Science or related field, or equivalent practical experience

**Experience:** 2-4 years of QA engineering or testing experience

**Skills:**
- Test automation using Python, Java, or JavaScript
- SQL for data validation and query testing
- Experience with continuous integration/continuous deployment tools
- Strong analytical and problem-solving capabilities
- Excellent documentation and communication skills

**Certifications:** ISTQB certification (preferred)
