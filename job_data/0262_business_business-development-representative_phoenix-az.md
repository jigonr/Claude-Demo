# Business Development Representative

**Company:** JPMorgan Chase | **Category:** Business | **Location:** Phoenix, AZ | **Salary:** $40,000–$54,000 | **Type:** Full-Time | **Posted:** 2026-03-05

## About JPMorgan Chase

JPMorgan Chase is a leading global financial services firm with a diverse portfolio of business offerings. Our Corporate Banking division serves institutional clients, providing tailored financial solutions and strategic partnerships.

## About the Role

As a Business Development Representative, you'll be an integral part of our Phoenix sales team, responsible for generating new business opportunities and building lasting client relationships. This is an excellent starting point for a career in financial services, offering structured training, mentorship from experienced professionals, and the chance to develop skills in a fast-paced, competitive environment.

## Responsibilities

- Identify and qualify new business opportunities within assigned territory using proprietary business intelligence
- Schedule and prepare client meetings; deliver compelling presentations on JPMorgan Chase products and services
- Maintain accurate prospect tracking in CRM system; update client information and interaction history
- Research potential clients and develop customized pitch strategies aligned with their business needs
- Support team with proposal preparation and client event coordination

## Requirements

**Education:** Bachelor's degree from an accredited university

**Experience:** 0-2 years of sales, business development, or client-facing experience preferred

**Skills:**
- Excellent communication and interpersonal abilities
- Strong work ethic and ability to handle rejection
- Basic financial acumen and business sense
- Microsoft Office proficiency
- Ability to work effectively in a team environment

**Certifications:** None required
