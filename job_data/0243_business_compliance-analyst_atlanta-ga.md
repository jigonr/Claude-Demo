## About FedEx

FedEx operates one of the world's largest and most reliable logistics networks, moving billions of packages annually across 220+ countries. Our Atlanta operations hub oversees compliance and regulatory adherence for a significant portion of our domestic and international shipments.

## About the Role

FedEx Operations Support is hiring a Compliance Analyst to monitor adherence with federal transportation regulations, international shipping standards, and internal operational procedures. Working remotely, you'll evaluate operational processes, identify compliance gaps, and recommend corrective actions that keep FedEx compliant and operational excellence at the forefront.

## Responsibilities

- Review shipment documentation and carrier compliance records against DOT, IATA, and HAZMAT regulations
- Conduct monthly internal audits on select facilities, documenting findings and tracking remediation timelines
- Coordinate with regional operations managers to develop and implement compliance improvement plans
- Analyze incident reports to identify systemic compliance issues and recommend preventive measures
- Maintain tracking database of regulatory updates and their operational impact
- Prepare quarterly compliance scorecards for senior leadership review

## Requirements

**Education**

- Attention to detail in complex regulatory environments
- Cross-functional communication

**Certifications**
- Preferred: DOT HazMat certification or in-progress training
