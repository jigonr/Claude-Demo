We're seeking a Sales Operations Analyst to optimize our sales processes, manage CRM systems, and provide analytical support to our banking teams. You will maintain customer data quality, track sales metrics, and identify opportunities to improve sales effectiveness.

- Identify process improvements and support implementation
- Provide training on CRM systems and sales tools
