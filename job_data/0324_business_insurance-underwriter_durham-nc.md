## About McKinsey & Company

McKinsey & Company is a global management consulting firm dedicated to helping organizations realize their most important goals. Our insurance practice works with the world's leading carriers, brokers, and insurtech companies to drive transformation and growth.

## About the Role

We're looking for an Insurance Underwriter to support our consulting engagements with major insurance clients. You'll provide deep underwriting expertise that helps shape strategic recommendations, gaining firsthand exposure to how consulting transforms the insurance industry. Work fully remote while collaborating with world-class consultants and clients.

## Responsibilities

- Analyze insurance underwriting processes and risk assessment methodologies for client organizations
- Review underwriting guidelines, rating models, and policy requirements to identify optimization opportunities
- Prepare detailed underwriting case studies and process maps for client presentations
- Support due diligence activities for insurance company acquisitions and strategic partnerships
- Collaborate with senior consultants to develop client recommendations based on underwriting insights
- Maintain knowledge of insurance industry trends, regulatory changes, and competitive landscape
