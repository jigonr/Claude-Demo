# Clinical Laboratory Technician

**Sanofi** | Healthcare | Denver, CO | $47,000–$60,000 USD/year | Full-time | Posted Jan 6, 2026

## About Sanofi

Sanofi is a global biopharmaceutical company dedicated to discovering, developing, and delivering medicines to improve patient health. Our Denver facility supports pharmaceutical research and quality assurance.

## About the Role

As a Clinical Laboratory Technician at Sanofi, you'll perform critical laboratory tests that support the development and quality control of our pharmaceutical products. Work hybrid at our Denver facility, contributing hands-on laboratory expertise to medicine development.

## Responsibilities

- Perform clinical and analytical laboratory tests on patient specimens
- Operate and maintain laboratory equipment and instruments
- Prepare specimens and reagents according to established protocols
- Record and report laboratory test results accurately
- Maintain laboratory safety standards and regulatory compliance
- Support quality control and assurance procedures

## Requirements

**Education**
Associate's degree or Bachelor's degree in Clinical Laboratory Science or related field

**Experience**
1–3 years in clinical laboratory, laboratory testing, or pharmaceutical QA roles

**Skills**
- Clinical laboratory testing procedures
- Laboratory equipment operation and maintenance
- Knowledge of laboratory safety and biosafety protocols
- Attention to detail and accuracy
- Understanding of quality control procedures
- Computer skills and data entry proficiency

**Certifications**
Clinical Laboratory Scientist (CLS) or Clinical Laboratory Technician (CLT) certification required

- Hybrid work arrangement
- Competitive salary and benefits
- Health, dental, and vision insurance
- Tuition reimbursement for certifications
