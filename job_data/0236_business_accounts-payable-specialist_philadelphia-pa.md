## About the Role
You'll be part of a global team managing complex transactions and operations. You will develop expertise in critical areas while working alongside experienced professionals who are committed to your growth.

- Maintain vendor master files and payment terms
- Reconcile AP subledger accounts and resolve discrepancies
- Prepare and release payment batches
- Handle vendor inquiries and resolve payment issues
- Support audit activities and regulatory compliance

## Requirements

- **Education:** High school diploma or equivalent; some accounting coursework preferred
- **Experience:** 2-4 years of accounts payable or accounting experience
- **Skills:** AP processing, Invoice management, Vendor management, Excel, ERP systems
- **Certifications:** Accounts Payable Specialist certification (preferred)
