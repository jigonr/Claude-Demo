# Pharmacy Technician

**Company:** Johns Hopkins Medicine | **Category:** Healthcare | **Location:** New York, NY | **Salary:** $47,000–$64,000 | **Type:** Full-Time | **Posted:** 2026-03-19

## About Johns Hopkins Medicine

Johns Hopkins Medicine is a world-renowned academic medical center known for clinical excellence, innovative research, and education. We are committed to providing exceptional patient care and advancing medicine.

## About the Role

As a Pharmacy Technician at Johns Hopkins Medicine New York, you'll play a vital role in our patient care mission. Working in a hybrid arrangement, you'll support our pharmacists in preparing and dispensing medications, managing inventory, and ensuring patient safety. This position offers the opportunity to work in a prestigious academic medical environment.

## Responsibilities

- Prepare medications under pharmacist supervision; verify calculations and dosages
- Manage pharmacy inventory, ordering, and stock rotation
- Process prescription orders and maintain accurate patient medication records
- Assist patients with medication education and answer questions
- Maintain pharmacy cleanliness and comply with safety standards

## Requirements

**Education:** High school diploma or GED; pharmacy technician certification preferred

**Experience:** 1-3 years of pharmacy or healthcare experience

**Skills:**
- Proficiency with pharmacy software and dispensing systems
- Strong organizational and attention to detail
- Excellent customer service and patient interaction skills
- Understanding of pharmaceutical terminology and medications
- Ability to work in a fast-paced healthcare environment

**Certifications:** Pharmacy Technician Certification (CPhT) preferred
