As a Data Analyst, you'll extract insights from massive datasets driving product and business decisions. You will develop expertise in critical areas while working alongside experienced professionals who are committed to your growth.

- Support metrics development and KPI tracking
- Validate data quality and document data sources
- Present findings and recommendations to leadership

- **Experience:** 2-3 years of data analysis, business intelligence, or analytics experience
- **Skills:** SQL, Python/R, Data visualization, Excel, Statistical analysis
- **Certifications:** Google Analytics certification, Tableau
