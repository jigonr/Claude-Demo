# Insurance Underwriter

**Company:** CBRE | **Category:** Business | **Location:** Madison, WI | **Salary:** $45,000 - $58,000 | **Type:** Full-Time | **Posted:** 2026-02-21

## About CBRE

CBRE is the world's largest commercial real estate services firm.

## About the Role

Join CBRE's underwriting team to evaluate and manage insurance risk. You'll review applications and make underwriting decisions on commercial properties and liabilities.

## Responsibilities

- Review insurance applications and supporting documentation
- Evaluate risk factors and financial metrics for commercial accounts
- Make underwriting decisions on coverage and pricing
- Prepare underwriting reports and recommendations
- Communicate with brokers and clients regarding coverage decisions
- Stay current with underwriting guidelines and regulatory requirements

**Experience:** 1-3 years

**Skills:**
- Risk assessment
- Financial analysis
- Attention to detail
- Communication
- Regulatory knowledge
