## About Citigroup

Citigroup is a leading global financial services company providing banking, investment, and advisory services to millions of customers worldwide. We're committed to financial inclusion and sustainable growth.

## About the Role

You'll support the financial operations that drive our business. As a Staff Accountant, you'll manage accounts, record transactions, and ensure accuracy of financial records. This hybrid Kansas City role offers solid foundation in accounting with support from experienced team members.

## Responsibilities

- Record transactions and maintain general ledger accounts
- Reconcile bank accounts and resolve discrepancies
- Prepare financial statements and supporting schedules
- Assist with audit and compliance activities
- Monitor account balances and investigate variances

**Experience:** 0-2 years

**Skills:**
- General ledger and accounting principles
- Excel and accounting software
- Reconciliation and account analysis
- Attention to detail and accuracy
- GAAP knowledge
