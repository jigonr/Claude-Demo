# EKG Technician

**Company:** BioNTech | **Category:** Healthcare | **Location:** Detroit, MI | **Salary:** $29,000 - $38,000 | **Type:** Full-Time | **Posted:** 2026-01-18

## About BioNTech

BioNTech is an immunology and oncology focused biotechnology company pioneering personalized medicine.

## About the Role

Support our cardiology team at BioNTech by performing electrocardiograms and cardiac monitoring procedures with precision and care.

## Responsibilities

- Perform 12-lead ECG procedures and cardiac stress tests
- Operate telemetry monitoring systems and interpret basic rhythms
- Prepare patients, explain procedures, and ensure comfort
- Maintain equipment calibration and troubleshoot technical issues
- Document findings and communicate abnormalities to clinical staff
- Maintain patient confidentiality and follow infection control protocols

**Experience:** 0-3 years

- Patient communication

- Cardiac knowledge
- Attention to detail
