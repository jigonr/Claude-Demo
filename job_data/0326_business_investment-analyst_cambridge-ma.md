## About T. Rowe Price

T. Rowe Price is a global investment manager with over 50 years of experience delivering exceptional risk-adjusted returns. We're recognized for our research rigor, disciplined investment processes, and commitment to long-term value creation for our clients.

## About the Role

As an Investment Analyst at T. Rowe Price's Cambridge office, you'll dive deep into securities research and contribute to investment decisions that impact billions in assets under management. Working alongside experienced portfolio managers, you'll develop analytical skills, build financial models, and influence portfolio strategy. This prestigious role combines rigorous analysis with meaningful impact.

## Responsibilities

- Conduct fundamental security analysis and investment research
- Build financial models and valuation analyses
- Monitor companies and sectors within assigned coverage areas
- Present findings and recommendations to portfolio managers
- Evaluate risks and opportunities in assigned securities
- Collaborate with portfolio management and trading teams
