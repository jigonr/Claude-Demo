# Social Media Coordinator

**Target** | Business | Remote | $37,000–$49,000/year | Full-time | Posted Feb 2, 2026

## About Target

Target is one of America's leading retailers, offering a wide assortment of general merchandise and essentials at great prices. We're committed to making everyday moments matter for our guests.

## About the Role

As a Social Media Coordinator at Target, you'll connect with millions of guests remotely through our social channels. You'll create engaging content, respond to customer inquiries, and build community. This role offers the chance to grow your digital marketing skills while representing a trusted brand.

## Responsibilities

- Create and schedule engaging social media content across platforms (Instagram, TikTok, Facebook, Twitter)
- Respond to customer inquiries and comments; provide excellent customer service on social channels
- Monitor social media trends and competitor activity; recommend content strategies
- Track social metrics and performance; prepare reports on engagement and reach
- Collaborate with marketing and merchandising teams to align social content with campaigns
