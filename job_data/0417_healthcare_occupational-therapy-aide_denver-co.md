# Occupational Therapy Aide

**Company:** Fortrea | **Category:** Healthcare | **Location:** Denver, CO | **Salary:** $29,000–$39,000 | **Type:** Full-Time | **Posted:** 2026-02-23

## About Fortrea

Fortrea is a leading clinical research organization that provides comprehensive services and technologies to support drug development and clinical trials. We partner with sponsors and sites to advance the development of new treatments.

## About the Role

As an Occupational Therapy Aide at Fortrea Denver, you'll support our clinical research operations with a focus on patient care. You'll work with occupational therapists to provide therapeutic support to research participants. This hybrid role offers the opportunity to develop skills in healthcare and contribute to clinical research.

## Responsibilities

- Assist occupational therapists with patient care and therapeutic activities
- Prepare therapy equipment and treatment areas
- Support patient documentation and data collection
- Help maintain patient comfort and provide basic patient support
- Maintain cleanliness and organization of therapy spaces

## Requirements

**Education:** High school diploma or GED; some college or healthcare training preferred

**Experience:** 0-1 years of healthcare or patient care experience

**Skills:**
- Compassionate patient care orientation
- Strong communication and interpersonal abilities
- Ability to follow instructions and maintain organization
- Basic computer and documentation skills
- Physical ability to support patients and handle equipment

**Certifications:** None required

## Benefits

- Health, dental, and vision coverage
- 401(k) plan with company match
- Paid time off and paid holidays
- Occupational therapy training opportunities
- Hybrid work arrangement
