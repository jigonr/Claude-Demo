## About the Role

We're seeking a Database Administrator I to manage and optimize our PostgreSQL and cloud databases supporting our corporate spend management platform. You will monitor database performance, implement backup and recovery strategies, and work with engineering teams to ensure data reliability.

- Implement and test backup and disaster recovery procedures
- Optimize database queries and maintain performance standards
- Manage user access and implement security best practices
- Collaborate with engineering on database design and scaling
- Document database architecture and maintain runbooks
